1 AN ACT relating to vehicle lights.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 189.920 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) All fire department, rescue squad, or publicly owned emergency management
5 agency emergency vehicles and all ambulances shall be equipped with one (1) or
6 more flashing, rotating, or oscillating red lights, visible under normal atmospheric
7 conditions from a distance of five hundred (500) feet to the front of the vehicle, and
8 a siren, whistle, or bell, capable of emitting a sound audible under normal
9 conditions from a distance of not less than five hundred (500) feet. This equipment
10 shall be in addition to any other equipment required by the motor vehicle laws.
11 (2) All state, county, or municipal police vehicles and all sheriffs' vehicles used as
12 emergency vehicles shall be equipped with one (1) or more flashing, rotating, or
13 oscillating blue lights, visible under normal atmospheric conditions from a distance
14 of five hundred (500) feet to the front of the vehicle, and a siren, whistle, or bell,
15 capable of emitting a sound audible under normal conditions from a distance of not
16 less than five hundred (500) feet. This equipment shall be in addition to any other
17 equipment required by the motor vehicle laws.
18 (3) By ordinance, the governing body of any city or county may direct that the police or
19 sheriffs' vehicles in that jurisdiction be equipped with a combination of red and blue
20 flashing, rotating, or oscillating lights.
21 (4) (a) All public safety vehicles shall be equipped with one (1) or more flashing,
22 rotating, or oscillating yellow lights, visible under normal atmospheric
23 conditions from a distance of five hundred (500) feet to the front of the
24 vehicle. Yellow flashing, rotating, or oscillating lights may also be used by
25 vehicles operated by mail carriers while on duty, funeral escort vehicles, and
26 church buses.
27 (b) In addition to the lights required in paragraph (a) of this subsection, a
