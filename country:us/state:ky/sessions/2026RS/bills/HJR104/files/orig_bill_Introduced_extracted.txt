2 Memorial Highway in Lyon County.
3 WHEREAS, Sean Robert King was a devoted husband, loving father of four
4 children, and cherished member of his community; and
5 WHEREAS, on May 24, 2023, Sean Robert King was tragically killed while riding
6 his bicycle on Kentucky Route 93, a loss that deeply affected his family, friends, and all
7 who knew him; and
8 WHEREAS, Sean Robert King was known for his kindness, his commitment to his
9 family, and his dedication to living an active and engaged life; and
10 WHEREAS, Sean Robert King's passing serves as a solemn reminder of the
11 importance of roadway safety and the shared responsibility of motorists and cyclists to
12 protect one another; and
13 WHEREAS, on December 11, 2025, the Lyon County Fiscal Court adopted a
14 Resolution supporting the designation of a portion of Kentucky Route 93 in honor of
15 Sean Robert King;
16 NOW, THEREFORE,
17 Be it resolved by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
18 Section 1. The Transportation Cabinet shall designate a portion of Kentucky
19 Route 93 in Lyon County, from the intersection with Poplar Creek Road (mile point
20 18.239) to the intersection with Kentucky Route 819 (mile point 20.394) as the "Sean
21 Robert King Share the Road Memorial Highway" and shall, within 30 days of the
22 effective date of this Resolution, erect appropriate signage denoting this designation.
