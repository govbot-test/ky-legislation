1 AN ACT relating to members of the bar.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 21A IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The Supreme Court has power to provide for the organization, governance, and
6 membership of the bar of Kentucky, and to adopt rules and regulations to govern
7 the conduct and activity of the bar and its members, except that no member of the
8 bar, without the member's annual written consent, shall be required to join or pay
9 membership dues to any association or entity beyond payment of fees required to
10 support the costs associated with:
11 (a) Admission to the bar; and
12 (b) Discipline of members of the bar.
13 (2) For purposes of this section, a member of the bar is any attorney licensed to
14 practice law in Kentucky.
15 Section 2. KRS 21A.300 is amended to read as follows:

17 days following the filing of a criminal or civil action, or claim for damages, or a

19 service[ shall be subject to the following prohibition. An attorney or an attorney
20 referral service] shall not directly solicit, or knowingly permit another person to

22 a victim of the accident or disaster, or a relative of the victim, for the purpose of
