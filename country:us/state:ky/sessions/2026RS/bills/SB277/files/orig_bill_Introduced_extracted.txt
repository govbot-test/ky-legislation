2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 350.240 is amended to read as follows:
4 The Energy and Environment Cabinet may promulgate[adopt] reasonable administrative
5 regulations in accordance with KRS Chapters 13A for the reclamation of land disturbed
6 or removed in the mining of clay. The administrative[Such] regulations shall encourage
7 water impoundments and shall follow the standards established in Article III of the
8 Interstate Mining Compact. The cabinet shall have the authority to promulgate
9 administrative[adopt such] regulations prior to the effective date of the Interstate Mining
10 Compact and irrespective of whether the state becomes a member or withdraws from
11 membership in the Interstate Mining Compact.
