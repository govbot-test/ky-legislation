4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 186 IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) All written examinations and road skills tests administered to applicants for an
7 instruction permit or operator's license shall be in English only.
8 (2) An applicant shall not use the following during a written examination:
9 (a) A translation dictionary;
10 (b) An electronic device; or
11 (c) An interpreter.
12 Section 2. KRS 186.480 is amended to read as follows:
13 (1) The Department of Kentucky State Police shall examine every applicant for an
14 operator's license as identified in KRS 186.635, except as otherwise provided in this
15 section. The examination shall be held in the county where the applicant resides
16 unless:
17 (a) The applicant is granted written permission by the Transportation Cabinet to
18 take the examination in another county; or
19 (b) The applicant is tested using a bioptic telescopic device.
20 (2) (a) The examination shall include a test of the applicant's eyesight to ensure
21 compliance with the visual acuity and visual field standards set forth in KRS
22 186.577. The vision testing outlined in this subsection shall be administered
23 under the provisions established in KRS 186.577 at, or prior to, the time of
24 application.
25 (b) The examination shall also include a test of the applicant's ability to read and
26 understand highway signs regulating, warning, and directing traffic, the
27 applicant's knowledge of traffic laws, and an actual demonstration of the
