6 (1) The Kentucky General Assembly Medal of Distinction is hereby created.
7 (2) Persons eligible to receive the medal include:

9 or a reserve component of the Armed Forces or National Guard, and
10 veterans of those services who were separated or released therefrom with an

18 telecommunicators, and others who are employed or volunteer their time to
19 render aid to persons in distress;
20 (c) Citizens of the Commonwealth of Kentucky; and
21 (d) Entities operating within the Commonwealth of Kentucky or otherwise
22 providing services to the citizens of the Commonwealth.
23 (3) The medal shall be the preeminent award conferred by the Commonwealth and
24 may be awarded to recognize:
25 (a) The commission of a valorous or heroic deed performed in the saving of a
26 life;
27 (b) An injury or death, or having faced the threat of injury or death, in the
