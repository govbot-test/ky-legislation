2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 6 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) (a) Members of the General Assembly and members-elect of the General
6 Assembly who have received their certificates of election may prefile bills
7 and resolutions for introduction in the next succeeding regular legislative
8 session with the director of the Legislative Research Commission.
9 (b) Bills and resolutions that are prefiled pursuant to this section shall be in the
10 final and correct form for introduction in the General Assembly as required
11 by the Constitution of Kentucky, laws, and rules of the respective houses of
12 the Legislature.
13 (c) The original copy of every bill and resolution prefiled shall include the full
14 signature of the sponsor of the bill or resolution immediately below the
15 typed or printed name of the sponsor.
16 (2) The director of the Legislative Research Commission shall:
17 (a) Receive prefiled bills and resolutions;
18 (b) Assign numbers for introduction in the proper house in order of receipt;
19 (c) Publish the prefiled bill or resolution on the Commission's website once it
20 has been assigned a number; and
21 (d) Subject to the provisions of subsection (4) of this section, deliver the
22 original and all requisite copies of each bill and resolution to the clerk of
23 the Senate or the clerk of the House of Representatives, as appropriate, on
24 the first day of the regular session.
25 (3) Prefiled bills and resolutions shall be considered as introduced on the day of their
26 delivery to each house.
27 (4) Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection (2) of this section, in the event a
