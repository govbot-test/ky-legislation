3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 194A IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The Kentucky all-payer claims database is established in Sections 1 to 6 of this
6 Act to effectuate the following purposes:
7 (a) Allow for targeted population health initiatives;
8 (b) Determine state health status needs;
9 (c) Inform state health care planning;
10 (d) Support research in the areas of health care cost, quality, and accessibility;
11 (e) Improve the accessibility, adequacy, and affordability of health care and
12 health care coverage through the review and dissemination of data;
13 (f) Review health care costs among various treatment settings, providers, and
14 modalities;
15 (g) Evaluate the effectiveness of health care programs and services to improve
16 patient outcomes; and
17 (h) Support the development of quality improvement initiatives.
18 (2) Sections 1 to 6 of this Act shall not be construed to supersede, supplement, or
19 limit the provisions of KRS Chapter 216B relating to certificates of need.
