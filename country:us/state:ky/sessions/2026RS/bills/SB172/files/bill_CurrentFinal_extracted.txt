Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS SB 172/GA
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 278.277 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) In the administration of an electric utility's fuel adjustment clause and any
5 associated tariffs, the commission may, upon the request of an electric utility,
6 extend the period for recovery of fuel adjustment costs that are typically
7 recoverable in order to reduce volatility for consumers and encourage stability in
8 rates.
9 (2) In any review by the commission of any fuel adjustment clause, for any contracts
10 entered into on or after July 1, 2021, the commission shall, in determining the
11 reasonableness of fuel costs in procurement contracts and fuel procurement
12 practices, evaluate the reasonableness of fuel costs in contracts and competing bids
13 based on the cost of the fuel less any coal severance tax imposed by any
14 jurisdiction.
15 (3)[(2)] As used in this section, "fuel adjustment clause" means any clause or
16 provision in any tariff or contract by which an electric utility may immediately
17 recover increases in fuel costs subject to later scrutiny or review by the commission.
18 Section 2. Whereas it is critical that any measure to help control the volatility of
19 electric utility rates be enacted as soon as possible to give relief to the ratepayers of the
20 Commonwealth, an emergency is declared to exist, and this Act takes effect upon its
21 passage and approval by the Governor or upon its otherwise becoming a law.
22 Section 3. This Act may be cited as the Fuel Surcharge Stability Act.
