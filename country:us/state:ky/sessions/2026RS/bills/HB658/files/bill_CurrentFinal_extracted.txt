14 allowable usage within a 12-month period from 180 days to 210 days for certain drivers
15 in farm-related service industries, and the Transportation Cabinet has not taken action to
16 comply with this federal regulation, an emergency is declared to exist, and this Act takes
17 effect upon its passage and approval by the Governor or upon its otherwise becoming a
