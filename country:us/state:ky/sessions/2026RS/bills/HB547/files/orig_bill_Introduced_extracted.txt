Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 93
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 131.030 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The department [of Revenue ]shall exercise all administrative functions of the state
5 in relation to:
6 (a) The state revenue and tax laws;
7 (b) The publishing of administrative writings, tax forms, and instructions to those
8 tax forms on its official website;
9 (c) The licensing and registering of motor vehicles;
10 (d) The equalization of tax assessments;
11 (e) The assessment of public utilities and public service corporations for taxes;
12 (f) The assessment of franchises;
13 (g) The supervision of tax collections; and
14 (h) The enforcement of revenue and tax laws, either directly or through
15 supervision of tax administration activity in other departments to which the
16 department may commit administration of certain taxes.
17 (2) The department shall have all the powers and duties with reference to assessment or
18 equalization of the assessment of property heretofore exercised or performed by any
19 state board or commission.
20 (3) The department shall have all the powers and duties necessary to consider and settle
21 tax cases under KRS 131.110 and refund claims made under KRS 134.580. The
22 department is encouraged to settle controversies on a fair and equitable basis and
23 shall be authorized to settle tax controversies based on the hazards of litigation
24 applicable to them.
25 (4) The department shall have all the powers and duties necessary to collect any debts
26 owed to the Commonwealth, or any local government of the Commonwealth, that
27 are referred to the department by an organizational unit or administrative body in
