1 AN ACT relating to the autism spectrum disorder trust fund and declaring an

3 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 141 IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) For taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2026, any taxpayer required
7 to file a return under KRS 141.180 who is entitled to an income tax refund and
8 who desires to contribute to the autism spectrum disorder trust fund created in
9 Section 2 of this Act may designate an amount, not to exceed the amount of the
10 refund, to be paid to the fund. A designation made under this section shall not
11 affect the income tax liability of the taxpayer, but it shall reduce the income tax
12 refund by the amount designated.
13 (2) The tax refund designation authorized by this section shall be printed on the face
14 of the Kentucky individual income tax form.
15 (3) The instructions accompanying the individual income tax return shall include a
16 description of the autism spectrum disorder trust fund and the purposes for which
17 the funds from the income tax checkoff may be used.
18 (4) The department shall, by July 1, 2027, and by July 1 of each year thereafter,
19 transfer the funds designated by taxpayers under this section to the autism
20 spectrum disorder trust fund created in Section 2 of this Act.
21 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 194A IS CREATED TO
22 READ AS FOLLOWS:
23 (1) The autism spectrum disorder trust fund is hereby created in the State Treasury
24 as a separate trust fund. The fund shall be administered by the Cabinet for
25 Health and Family Services.
26 (2) The fund shall receive amounts collected from the income tax checkoff created in
27 Section 1 of this Act, and any other proceeds from grants, contributions,
