UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2037
1 A RESOLUTION recognizing February 5, 2026, as Kentucky Nurses Day in the
2 Commonwealth.
3 WHEREAS, nurses positively impact the health and well-being of Kentuckians
4 across the Commonwealth; and
5 WHEREAS, the nursing profession is recognized as the nation's most trusted
6 profession; and
7 WHEREAS, nurses have been licensed in Kentucky since 1914, and over 100,000
8 nurses are currently licensed in the Commonwealth, working in a variety of settings and
9 specialties; and
10 WHEREAS, Kentucky's nurses have been continuously dedicated to providing care,
11 teaching nursing students, contributing to the scientific understanding of illness and
12 health, and promoting good health policy;
13 NOW, THEREFORE,
14 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
15 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
16 Section 1. The members of the House of Representatives hereby recognize
17 February 5, 2026, as Kentucky Nurses Day in the Commonwealth.
18 Section 2. When the House of Representatives adjourns this day, it does so in
19 honor, recognition, and celebration of Kentucky's nurses and the service and high-quality
20 care that they provide to Kentuckians.
21 Section 3. The Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to transmit a
22 copy of this Resolution to Representative Rebecca Raymer for delivery.
