7 person:

12 Equivalency Diploma[, as evidenced by:

14 twelfth grade or the equivalent that has been filed with the nominating
15 petition required by KRS 118.315; or

17 a twelfth grade equivalency examination that has been filed with the
18 nominating petition required by KRS 118.315];

20 160.380(5);
21 (e)[(d)] Does not hold any elective federal, state, county, or city office;
22 (f)[(e)] Is not, at the time of his or her election, directly or indirectly interested
23 in the sale to the board of books, stationery, or any other property, materials,
24 supplies, equipment, or services for which school funds are expended;
25 (g)[(f)] Has not been removed from membership on a board of education for
26 cause; and
