1 AN ACT relating to housing and declaring an emergency.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 198A IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Affordable housing" means housing that is eligible for state or federal
7 funds to support its construction, repair, or rehabilitation as a result of its
8 affordability;
9 (b) "Affordable housing funds" means state or federal funds that directly
10 finance, in whole or in part, the:
11 1. Construction, repair, or rehabilitation of affordable housing; or
12 2. Rental of housing by eligible low-income persons; and
13 (c) "Agency":
14 1. Means any state agency, local government, quasi-governmental entity,
15 or other governmental entity in the Commonwealth that expends
16 affordable housing funds; and
17 2. Includes the Kentucky Housing Corporation.
18 (2) Notwithstanding any law or administrative regulation to the contrary, an agency
19 that expends affordable housing funds shall expend:
20 (a) At least ninety percent (90%) of those funds on single-family detached
21 housing; and
22 (b) No more than ten percent (10%) of those funds on multifamily housing;
23 in any given fiscal year.
24 (3) The requirement in subsection (2) of this section:
25 (a) Supersedes any condition contained in a grant or other appropriation; and
26 (b) Does not apply to funds expended by an agency other than affordable
27 housing funds, including but not limited to funds used to defray its
