UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1914
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 43 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Beginning in fiscal year 2026-2027, and every two (2) years thereafter, the
6 Auditor shall audit the contract procurement systems of the Department of
7 Highways.
8 (2) The audit under this section shall include statistical analysis to detect:
9 (a) Bid rigging;
10 (b) Tacit collusion;
11 (c) Market allocation;
12 (d) Market division;
13 (e) Complementary or cover bidding;
14 (f) Bid rotation;
15 (g) Bid suppression; and
16 (h) Any other scheme or practice designed to circumvent competitive bidding
17 processes.
18 (3) The Auditor may contract for professional services to fulfill the statistical
19 analysis requirements of this section.
20 (4) The Department of Highways shall reimburse the Auditor for the cost of the audit
21 required by this section, including the cost of contracted professional services.
22 (5) The Auditor shall submit the audit required under this section to the Legislative
23 Research Commission by October 31, 2027, and subsequently by October 31st of
24 each odd-numbered year.
