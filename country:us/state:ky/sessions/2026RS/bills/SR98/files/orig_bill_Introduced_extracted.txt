1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor and loving memory of Paige

4 Brenda Sue Davidson and the late Utah Feltner; and

10 (Dexter) Davidson, her grandmother, Martha Greer; her sisters, Paula Bush and Mary

16 Kentucky:
17 Section 1. The Senate extends its sincere condolences to the family and friends
