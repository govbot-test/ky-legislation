UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 962
1 AN ACT relating to crimes and punishments.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 507.060 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) A person is guilty of vehicular homicide when:
5 (a) He or she causes the death of another; and
6 (b) The death results from the person's operation of a motor vehicle, including but
7 not limited to boats and airplanes:[,]
8 1. In excess of twenty-five (25) miles per hour above the speed limit;
9 2. In violation of KRS 189.580(1)(a); or
10 3. While under the influence of alcohol, a controlled substance, or other
11 substance which impairs driving ability as described in KRS 189A.010.
12 (2) Vehicular homicide is a Class B felony.
13 Section 2. This Act may be cited as Daniel's Law.
