UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2476
1 AN ACT relating to digital asset wallets.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 369.130 TO 369.139 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "wallet provider" or "provider" means a person that
6 offers or provides a wallet.
7 (2) A wallet provider:
8 (a) Shall:
9 1. Provide live customer service via a toll-free telephone number during
10 operating hours, which shall not be less than the hours between 8:00
11 a.m. and 10:00 p.m. local time within the Commonwealth; and
12 2. Provide a mechanism for, and assist any person who owns a wallet
13 that was provided by the provider with, resetting any password, pin,
14 seed phrase, or other similar information that is necessary to access
15 the contents the self-hosted wallet; and
16 (b) May require any person requesting assistance under paragraph (a)2. of this
17 subsection to verify the person's identity prior to providing the assistance by
18 means other than providing the password, pin, seed phrase, or other
19 information.
20 (3) (a) A violation of this section shall be deemed to be an unfair, false, misleading,
21 or deceptive act or practice in the conduct of trade or commerce in violation
22 of KRS 367.170.
23 (b) All of the remedies, powers, and duties provided to the Attorney General or
24 any other person under KRS 367.110 to 367.300, and the penalties provided
25 in KRS 367.990, pertaining to acts and practices declared unlawful by KRS
26 367.170, shall apply with equal force and effect to a violation of this section.
27 (4) (a) The remedies and penalties prescribed in this section shall be cumulative.
