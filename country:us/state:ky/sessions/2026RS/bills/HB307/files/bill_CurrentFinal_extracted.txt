1 AN ACT relating to proactive postsecondary admission.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 164 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Council" means the Council on Postsecondary Education; and
7 (b) "Department" means the Kentucky Department of Education.
8 (2) There is hereby established a proactive postsecondary admissions program to:
9 (a) Notify high school students of eligibility for prospective admission to a
10 public postsecondary education institution; and
11 (b) Provide instructions to high school students on how to access the common
12 online application to apply for full admission to public postsecondary
13 education institutions in Kentucky.
14 (3) No later than July 1, 2027, each public postsecondary education institution shall:
15 (a) Establish a minimum academic qualification for prospective admission to
16 the institution through the proactive postsecondary admissions program
17 that exclusively includes:
18 1. A minimum grade point average; or
19 2. A combination of a minimum grade point average and minimum score
20 on a nationally recognized college readiness examination accepted by
21 the institution; and
22 (b) Implement a common online application approved by the council for the
23 purpose of evaluating the admission of full-time, first-year students.
24 (4) Each public school district shall:
25 (a) Annually invite each student enrolled in a public high school to participate
26 in the proactive postsecondary admissions program and select a preferred
27 communication method for program correspondence; and
