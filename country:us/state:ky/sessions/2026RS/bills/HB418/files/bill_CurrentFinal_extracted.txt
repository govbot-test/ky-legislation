2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 403 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 In any proceeding for child custody under this chapter where the court has made a
6 finding that a party has committed domestic violence and abuse as defined in KRS
7 403.720 against another party to the proceeding or a child of the parties:
8 (1) The court shall not remove custody from or reduce the parenting time of the
9 nonoffending party to whom the child is bonded or attached if the court finds that
10 the:
11 (a) Nonoffending party is competent and not abusive; and
12 (b) Removal would be solely for purposes of improving the relationship between
13 the child and the offending party; and
14 (2) Any order to remediate resistance of a child to contact with a violent or abusive
15 parent shall primarily address the behavior of that parent.
16 Section 2. KRS 403.270 is amended to read as follows:
17 (1) (a) As used in this chapter and KRS 405.020, unless the context requires
18 otherwise, "de facto custodian" means a person who has been shown by clear
19 and convincing evidence to have been the primary caregiver for, and financial
20 supporter of, a child who within the last two (2) years has resided with the
21 person for an aggregate period of six (6) months or more if the child is under
22 three (3) years of age and for an aggregate period of one (1) year or more if
23 the child is three (3) years of age or older or has been placed by the
24 Department for Community Based Services. Any period of time after a legal
25 proceeding has been commenced by a parent seeking to regain custody of the
26 child shall not be included in determining whether the child has resided with
27 the person for the required minimum period.
