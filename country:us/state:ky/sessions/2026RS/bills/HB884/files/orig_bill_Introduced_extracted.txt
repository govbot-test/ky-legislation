UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2459
1 AN ACT relating to alpha-gal syndrome.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 214 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The Cabinet for Health and Family Services shall include alpha-gal syndrome in
6 its list of noncommunicable diseases that health care providers and health care
7 facilities are required to report to a local health department or to the Department
8 for Public Health in accordance with KRS 214.010.
9 (2) The laboratory that confirms a positive test result of alpha-gal syndrome shall
10 submit the confirmation to the Department for Public Health within five (5) days
11 using a reporting system required by the department. The laboratory shall use
12 diagnostic tests for alpha-gal syndrome that are recommended by the United
13 States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).
14 (3) The department shall follow up annually on reported cases of alpha-gal
15 syndrome using appropriate random sampling methods for confirming that
16 positive cases continue to meet the most current CDC surveillance case definition
17 of alpha-gal syndrome.
18 (4) The department shall submit an annual report to the CDC summarizing its
19 findings related to the reporting and incidence of alpha-gal syndrome.
