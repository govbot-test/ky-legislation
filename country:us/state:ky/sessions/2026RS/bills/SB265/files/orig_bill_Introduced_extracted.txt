1 AN ACT relating to nourishment and choice in the Supplemental Nutrition
2 Assistance Program.
3 WHEREAS, the health of Kentuckians is intrinsically tied to daily nourishment
4 choices; and
5 WHEREAS, a major step in decreasing poor health outcomes, including obesity
6 and chronic disease, is reducing consumption of sugar-sweetened and artificially flavored
7 beverages as well as foods of minimal nutritional value; and
8 WHEREAS, the core mission of the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program is
9 supporting access to nutritious and nourishing foods that alleviate hunger and combat
10 malnutrition; and
11 WHEREAS, the continued improvement of Kentuckians' health depends upon
12 realigning the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program with its core mission by
13 empowering Kentuckians benefiting from the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance
14 Program to make the most nourishing and healthiest possible choices;
15 NOW, THEREFORE,
16 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
17 Section 1. KRS 205.1783 is amended to read as follows:
18 In order to improve access to the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program, reduce
19 administrative costs associated with the program, [and ]enhance program integrity, and
20 support new dietary guidelines, and promote better health outcomes of participants and
21 the use of healthy food for nutrition purposes, the cabinet shall:
22 (1) Within one hundred eighty (180) days after July 14, 2022:
23 (a) Establish a transitional benefit alternative as described in 7 C.F.R. secs.
24 273.26 to 273.32;
25 (b) Request a waiver from the United States Department of Agriculture to
26 implement:
