1 AN ACT relating to identity documents.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 186.400 TO 186.640 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "communication disorder" means a health condition or
6 disability that may impede effective communication.
7 (2) The application for any initial or renewal instruction permit, operator's license,
8 or personal identification card under KRS 186.400 to 186.640, shall allow an
9 applicant to choose to indicate on the document that the applicant has a
10 communication disorder.
11 (3) The cabinet shall:
12 (a) Ensure that the instruction permit, operator's license, or personal
13 identification card of an applicant who has indicated a communication
14 disorder on an application includes a notation of the communication
15 disorder on the face of the instruction permit, operator's license, or personal
16 identification card;
17 (b) Consult with developmental disability and intellectual disability advocacy
18 groups with respect to the design of the communication disorder notation;
19 (c) Notify a person with a communication disorder notation on an instruction
20 permit, operator's license, or personal identification card who turns
21 eighteen (18) years of age that the person may request that the
22 communication disorder notation be removed;
23 (d) Delete any record of a person's communication disorder notation on request
24 by the person at any time;
25 (e) Establish public outreach efforts to educate the general public on the
26 availability of the communication disorder notation; and
27 (f) Put information on its website about the availability of a communication
