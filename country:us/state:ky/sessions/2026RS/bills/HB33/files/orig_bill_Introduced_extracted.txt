2 (1) A controller shall:
3 (a) Limit the collection of personal data to what is adequate, relevant, and
4 reasonably necessary in relation to the purposes for which the data is
5 processed as disclosed to the consumer;

7 purposes that are neither reasonably necessary to nor compatible with the
8 disclosed purposes for which the personal data is processed as disclosed to the
9 consumer, unless the controller obtains the consumer's consent;
10 (c) Establish, implement, and maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and
