UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 58
1 AN ACT relating to promoting human trafficking.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 529.110 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) A person is guilty of promoting human trafficking when the person intentionally:
5 (a) Benefits financially or receives anything of value from knowing participation
6 in human trafficking; or
7 (b) Recruits, entices, harbors, transports, provides, obtains, advertises, maintains,
8 patronizes, or solicits by any means, or attempts to recruit, entice, harbor,
9 transport, provide, obtain, advertise, maintain, patronize, or solicit by any
10 means, another person, knowing that the person will be subject to human
11 trafficking.
12 (2) Promoting human trafficking is a Class B[C] felony unless a victim of the
13 trafficking is under eighteen (18), in which case it is a Class A[B] felony.
