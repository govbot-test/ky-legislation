UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1605
1 AN ACT relating to exempting the Commonwealth of Kentucky from the
2 observance of daylight saving time.
3 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 2 IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) The Commonwealth of Kentucky shall be exempt from the advancement of time
7 provision known as daylight saving time as provided for in 15 U.S.C. sec. 260a.
8 (2) The Commonwealth of Kentucky and its political subdivisions shall at all times
9 observe the standard time of the United States as provided for in 15 U.S.C. secs.
10 261 to 263.
11 Section 2. This Act takes effect October 31, 2026.
