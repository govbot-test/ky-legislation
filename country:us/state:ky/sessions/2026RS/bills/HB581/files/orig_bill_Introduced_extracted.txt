Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2054
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 14 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "qualified jailer" means an individual who:
6 (a) Is currently serving or previously served as a duly elected or appointed jailer
7 in the Commonwealth of Kentucky; and
8 (b) Has not been:
9 1. Removed from office; or
10 2. Disqualified from holding office under the Constitution of Kentucky
11 or the Kentucky Revised Statutes.
12 (2) The Secretary of State shall issue a jailer service card to a qualified jailer who
13 applies to receive a card under this section. The jailer service card shall serve as
14 an acknowledgement by the Secretary of State that the cardholder currently
15 serves or previously served as a jailer in the Commonwealth of Kentucky.
16 (3) The Secretary of State shall promulgate administrative regulations in accordance
17 with KRS Chapter 13A to establish:
18 (a) Protocols for verifying the identity of the applicant and that the applicant is
19 a qualified jailer;
20 (b) The form of applications for the issuance of a jailer service card;
21 (c) The design and printed content of the jailer service card which shall, at a
22 minimum:
23 1. Identify the cardholder;
24 2. Distinguish between currently serving jailers and jailers who have
25 previously served; and
26 3. Identify the county in which the cardholder holds or held the office of
27 jailer;
