1 AN ACT relating to the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program education and
2 training plan.
3 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
4 Section 1. KRS 205.2003 is amended to read as follows:
5 (1) The secretary shall promulgate[by] administrative regulations in accordance
6 with[subject to the provisions of] KRS Chapter 13A to develop a work program for
7 recipients of public assistance under Title IV-A of the Federal Social Security Act
8 to provide for immediate employment or preparation for employment.
9 (2) The self-sufficiency of participants shall be the primary goal of the work program.[
10 ] Participants shall bear the ultimate responsibility in pursuit of this goal.
11 (3) In partnership with participants, the cabinet shall assess their strengths and needs
12 and provide supportive services to assist in the pursuit of work and self-sufficiency.
13 (4) (a) The cabinet shall develop and implement initiatives under Kentucky's
14 Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program education and training plan
15 established in accordance with 7 C.F.R. sec. 273.7 to promote improved
16 employment outcomes and workforce participation. The initiatives shall:
17 1. Be related to healthy workforce habits among eligible individuals;
18 2. Include establishing partnerships with public and private entities,
19 including but not limited to workforce boards, educational institutions,
20 employers, and community organizations;
21 3. Include partner-funded programs supporting household food security
22 and improved nutrition, including but not limited to an incentive
23 program for the purpose of increasing the purchase and consumption
24 of nutritious foods both at work and at home;
25 4. Include job readiness, occupational training, and work-based learning
26 opportunities;
27 5. Implement employment assessment and individualized employment
