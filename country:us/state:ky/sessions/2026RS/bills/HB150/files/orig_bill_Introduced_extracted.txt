1 AN ACT relating to roofing contractors.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 198B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 As used in Sections 1 to 9 of this Act:
6 (1) "Roofing contractor":
7 (a) Means any person licensed by the department, including a subcontractor,
8 who in the ordinary course of business:
9 1. Engages in the business of commercial or residential roofing services
10 for a fee; or
11 2. Offers to engage in or solicits roofing-related services, including but
12 not limited to alteration, construction, installation, maintenance,
13 renovation, repair, and waterproofing; and
14 (b) Does not mean a person:
15 1. Engaged in the demolition of a structure or the cleanup of
16 construction waste and debris that contains roofing material; or
17 2. Working under the direct supervision of the roofing contractor and
18 who is hired by the roofing contractor as an employee, day laborer, or
19 contract laborer; and
20 (2) "Roofing contractor's license" or "license" means the license issued by the
21 department to a roofing contractor.
22 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 198B IS CREATED TO
23 READ AS FOLLOWS:
24 (1) A person who is not licensed as a roofing contractor shall not represent himself
25 or herself to the public as a roofing contractor or use any terms, titles, or
26 abbreviations that express or imply that the person is a roofing contractor.
27 (2) A person shall not practice as a roofing contractor, unless that person has met

2 SECTION 3. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 198B IS CREATED TO
3 READ AS FOLLOWS:
4 (1) The department shall:
5 (a) Administer and enforce Sections 1 to 9 of this Act; and
6 (b) Evaluate the qualifications of applicants for licensure.
7 (2) The department may:
8 (a) Refuse to issue or renew a roofing contractor's license;
9 (b) Suspend or revoke a roofing contractor's license;
10 (c) Impose supervisory or probationary conditions upon a licensee;
11 (d) Impose administrative disciplinary fines;
12 (e) Issue written reprimands or admonishments; and
13 (f) Take any combination of the actions permitted in this subsection.
14 (3) The department may seek injunctive relief in the Franklin Circuit Court or the
15 Circuit Court of the county in which the violation occurred or the business of the
16 accused is located to stop any unlawful practice prohibited by Sections 1 to 9 of
17 this Act and administrative regulations promulgated thereunder. The department
18 may also seek injunctive relief if an unlicensed person inappropriately uses the
19 title "roofing contractor."
20 (4) The department may enter into reciprocal agreements with other states having
21 licensure, certification, or registration qualifications and requirements of a
22 similar scope of practice to those of this state.

1 An applicant for a roofing contractor's license shall:
2 (1) Be at least eighteen (18) years of age;

12 and unemployment insurance laws and administrative regulations and has

15 SECTION 6. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 198B IS CREATED TO
16 READ AS FOLLOWS:
17 (1) The department shall select and approve an examination to be used in
18 determining the competency of persons to be licensed under Sections 1 to 9 of this
19 Act. Any examination selected and approved for licensing shall be a nationally
20 recognized examination that has been determined through proper validation
21 techniques to successfully measure an individual's competency to perform the
22 licensed practice.
23 (2) The department shall offer the examination on a regularly scheduled basis in
24 localities around the state.
25 (3) The department may contract with an outside entity or testing service for the
26 administration of the examination required for licensure.
