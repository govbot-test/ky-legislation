Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2002
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 164 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Residential dual enrollment program" means a dual enrollment program
7 in which students of the program reside in student housing; and
8 (b) "Student housing" means a facility owned, leased, contracted, occupied, or
9 operated by a public postsecondary education institution that is used to
10 provide residential accommodations to students of the institution.
11 (2) Beginning with the 2027-2028 academic year, a public postsecondary education
12 institution shall not require a student who is within six (6) months of attaining
13 the age of eighteen (18) years or older on the first day of a semester to:
14 (a) Reside in student housing;
15 (b) Pay a fine or fee for not residing in student housing; or
16 (c) Enter into a student housing contract or agreement as a condition of
17 admission, enrollment, or entry into any activity, course, program, or
18 service offered by the institution.
19 (3) This section shall not apply to:
20 (a) A student who is not more than seventeen (17) years and six (6) months of
21 age on the first day of a semester and whose parent or guardian has not
22 submitted written consent for the student to reside outside of student
23 housing;
24 (b) Residential dual enrollment programs; or
25 (c) The model and practice school under KRS 164.380.
