Section 1. KRS 367.620 is amended to read as follows:
As used in KRS 367.620 to 367.628:
(1) "Contractor":
(a) Means a person that engages in the business of contracting, providing, or offering to contract or[, to]
provide, goods or services relating to real estate; and
(b) Includes any person that directly or indirectly solicits or offers a real estate goods or services contract;
(2) "Goods or services relating to real estate":
(a) Except as provided in paragraph (c) of this subsection, means goods, services, or goods and services
relating to real estate;[ and]

Section 3:

Section 4:

Section 5:
2. Otherwise adjusting losses or damages covered by an insurance policy;
(3) "Insured" means a person that is entitled, or may be entitled, to receive benefits or payments under a property,
casualty, or property and casualty insurance policy;
(4) "Person" has the same meaning as in KRS 367.110;
(5) "Real estate":
(a) Means any parcel of real estate located in this state that is used for any purpose; and
(b) Includes:

Section 6:
1. Residential real estate; and

Section 7:
2. Commercial real estate; and
(6) "Real estate goods or services contract" means a contract for the provision of goods or services relating to real
estate, where the goods or services are expected to be paid from property, casualty, or property and casualty
insurance proceeds.
