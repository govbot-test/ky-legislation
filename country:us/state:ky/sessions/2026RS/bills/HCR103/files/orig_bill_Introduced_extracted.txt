1 Commonwealth of Kentucky, the Senate concurring therein:
2 Section 1. The Legislative Research Commission is hereby directed to establish
3 the Health Care Workforce Task Force. The task force shall review existing initiatives
4 aimed at increasing the number of health care professionals while maintaining high
5 quality standards of care, consider new or expansions of existing initiatives, and
6 recommend any statutory or regulatory changes that may facilitate these initiatives.
7 Section 2. The Health Care Workforce Task Force shall be composed of the
8 following members with final membership of the task force being subject to the
9 consideration and approval of the Legislative Research Commission:
10 (1) Three members of the House of Representatives appointed by the Speaker of
11 the House of Representatives, one of whom shall be designated by the Speaker of the
12 House of Representatives as a co-chair of the task force;
13 (2) Three members of the Senate appointed by the President of the Senate, one of
14 whom shall be designated by the President of the Senate as a co-chair of the task force;
15 (3) One member of the House of Representatives appointed by the Minority Floor
16 Leader of the House of Representatives; and
17 (4) One member of the Senate appointed by the Minority Floor Leader of the
18 Senate.
19 Section 3. The Health Care Workforce Task Force shall at a minimum meet
20 monthly during the 2026 Interim of the General Assembly, and shall submit
21 recommendations to the Governor and to the Legislative Research Commission for
22 referral to the Interim Joint Committees on Health Services and Education by December
23 1, 2026.
24 Section 4. Provisions of this Resolution to the contrary notwithstanding, the
25 Legislative Research Commission shall have the authority to alternatively assign the
26 issues identified herein to an interim joint committee or subcommittee thereof, and to
27 designate a study completion date.
