UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1385
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 48.311 is amended to read as follows:
4 Any branch budget bill enacted by the General Assembly shall be enacted so that each
5 section, each subsection, and each appropriation sum by specified fund or funds accounts
6 shall be a separate and specific appropriation and law. If any section, any subsection, any
7 paragraph, or any provision is found by a court of competent jurisdiction in a final,
8 unappealable order to be invalid or unconstitutional, the decision of the courts shall not
9 affect or impair any of the remaining sections, subsections, paragraphs, or provisions
10 contained in that branch budget bill.
