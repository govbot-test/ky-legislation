1 A RESOLUTION recognizing Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Day at the State
2 Capitol.
3 WHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Day at the State Capitol is a national
4 program through which Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority members throughout the United
5 States meet with their legislators to address issues that are pertinent to their communities;
6 and
7 WHEREAS, founded in 1908, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., is a noteworthy
8 organization whose achievements in community service have redounded to the benefit of
9 the public good; and
10 WHEREAS, the record of Alpha Kappa Alpha’s origin, growth and development,
11 activities, evolving goals, and accomplishments is more than an interesting chronicle of a
12 colorful bit of college-based Americana. It is, rather, a significant and inspiring reflection
13 of the development of a minority group in a changing culture; and
14 WHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority's unending quest to be "Supreme in
15 Service to All Mankind" spans across domestic and international boundaries through the
16 service of members of more than 1,115 chapters around the world; and
17 WHEREAS, the state of Kentucky hosts 18 of these chapters on college and
18 university campuses and has active alumnae groups throughout the state; and
19 WHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., is a union of 390,000 energetic,
20 ambitious, and dedicated college-trained women who have consciously chosen this
21 affiliation to promote community initiatives that strengthen the bonds of sisterhood in
22 support of their service mission, empower families, build economic wealth, enhance the
23 environment, advocate for social justice, and uplift the community; and
24 WHEREAS, it is incumbent upon the members of the Senate to honor those
25 estimable organizations whose outstanding contributions to this Commonwealth have
26 greatly benefited their fellow citizens; and
27 WHEREAS, it is appropriate to commend the members of Alpha Kappa Alpha
