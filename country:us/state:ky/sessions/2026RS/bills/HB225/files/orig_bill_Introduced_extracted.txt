1 AN ACT relating to a tax credit for volunteer firefighters.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 141 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Commission" means the Kentucky Fire Commission;
7 (b) "Qualified fire department" has the same meaning as in KRS 95A.510; and
8 (c) "Volunteer firefighter" means a firefighter who:
9 1. Volunteers for a qualified fire department;
10 2. Is active with a qualified fire department for the entire taxable year for
11 which the credit is claimed; and
12 3. Appears on the list of certified volunteer firefighters provided by the
13 commission pursuant to subsection (4) of this section;
14 (2) For taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2027, but before January 1,
15 2031, a refundable credit shall be allowed for a volunteer firefighter against the
16 tax imposed by KRS 141.020 in the amount of one thousand dollars ($1,000),
17 with the ordering of credits as provided in Section 2 of this Act.
18 (3) (a) The purpose of the credit permitted by this section is to increase the number
19 of volunteer firefighters volunteering with a qualified fire department in the
20 Commonwealth.
21 (b) By November 1 of each year in which a volunteer firefighter credit is
22 claimed, the department shall report to the Legislative Research
23 Commission for referral to the Interim Joint Committee on Appropriations
24 and Revenue:
25 1. The number of taxpayers that claimed the credit for each taxable year;
26 2. The total amount of credits claimed for each taxable year;
27 3. Based on the mailing address of the return, the total amount of credits
