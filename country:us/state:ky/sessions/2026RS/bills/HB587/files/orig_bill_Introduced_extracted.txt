UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2021
1 AN ACT proposing to create a new section of the Constitution of Kentucky relating
2 to exempting property from taxation.
3 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
4 Section 1. Are you in favor of allowing the General Assembly to provide a
5 property tax exemption for the permanent residence of a veteran with a service-connected
6 disability by amending the Constitution of Kentucky to read as stated below?
7 SECTION 2. IT IS PROPOSED THAT A NEW SECTION BE ADDED TO
8 THE CONSTITUTION OF KENTUCKY TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
9 The General Assembly may provide by law a property tax exemption for the
10 permanent residence of an owner who is a veteran of the Armed Forces of the United
11 States with a service-connected disability. If provided, the law shall specify the amount
12 of the exemption, the qualifications for claiming the exemption, and whether the
13 exemption transfers to the veteran's surviving spouse. A permanent residence shall not
14 receive more than one exemption under this section.
15 An exemption created under this section shall not be combined with an
16 exemption under Section 170 of this Constitution, and is notwithstanding provisions of
17 Sections 171, 172, and 174 of this Constitution to the contrary.
18 Section 3. This amendment shall be submitted to the voters of the
19 Commonwealth for their ratification or rejection at the time and in the manner provided
20 for under Sections 256 and 257 of the Constitution, KRS 118.415, and Sections 4 and 5
21 of this Act.
22 Section 4. Notwithstanding any provision of KRS 118.415 to the contrary, the
23 Secretary of State shall cause the entirety of the question in Section 1 of this Act and the
24 entirety of the proposed amendment to the Constitution of Kentucky contained in Section
25 2 of this Act to be published at least one time in a newspaper of general circulation
26 published in this state, and shall also cause to be published at the same time and in the
27 same manner the fact that the amendment will be submitted to the voters for their
