1 AN ACT relating to the governance of the Kentucky School for the Deaf.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 167.017 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) (a) The superintendent of the Kentucky School for the Deaf shall be appointed by
5 the chief state school officer. The appointment shall be made upon
6 recommendation of a search committee which shall be appointed by the
7 Kentucky School for the Deaf Board[ of Education] whenever appointment of
8 a superintendent becomes necessary.
9 (b) The appointment of the superintendent shall be made within thirty (30) days
10 of the recommendation of the search committee, unless the chief state
11 school officer provides written notification to the Kentucky Board of
12 Education and the Kentucky School for the Deaf Board of the reason for
13 the failure to make the appointment within the allotted time.
14 (c) The superintendent of the Kentucky School for the Deaf shall carry out all
15 duties assigned to him or her by the chief state school officer, in
16 consultation with the Kentucky School for the Deaf Board, for the operation
17 of the Kentucky School for the Deaf, including those duties typically
18 reserved for a school principal when the Kentucky School for the Deaf is
19 without a principal.
20 (2) (a) The search committee for the superintendent of the Kentucky School for the
21 Deaf shall be appointed within thirty (30) days of receiving notice of a
22 vacancy and shall consist of the representative of the[ State] Department of
23 Education selected by the chief state school officer, one (1) representative of
24 the Kentucky Association of the Deaf, Inc., one (1) representative of the
25 Kentucky School for the Deaf Alumni Association, Inc., one (1)
26 representative of the Kentucky School for the Deaf faculty, one (1) parent of a
27 student at the Kentucky School for the Deaf, and one (1) representative of the
