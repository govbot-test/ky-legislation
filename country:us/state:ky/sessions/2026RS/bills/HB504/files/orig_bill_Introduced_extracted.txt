10 PART III
11 GENERAL PROVISIONS
12 1. Expenditure Authority: The Director of the Administrative Office of the
13 Courts, with the approval of the Chief Justice, may expend any of the funds appropriated
14 for court operations and administration in any lawful manner and for any legal purpose
15 that the Chief Justice shall authorize or direct. No executive agency of state government
16 shall have the power to restrict or limit the expenditure of funds appropriated to the
17 Judicial Branch of government, except that funds appropriated in this Act shall not be
18 expended for any purpose not specifically authorized by the General Assembly in this
19 Act. The Court of Justice shall prepare a report of actual expenditures citing specific
20 statutory or budgetary authorization for the reported expenditures and detailing
21 expenditures for the Supreme Court, Court of Appeals, Circuit Court, Family Court,
22 District Court, Administrative Office of the Courts, Judicial Retirement, Local Facilities
23 Fund, Local Facilities Use Allowance Contingency Fund, and for services performed by
24 the Circuit Court Clerks' offices. The Court of Justice shall provide this report on a
25 quarterly basis to the Interim Joint Committee on Appropriations and Revenue.
26 2. Severability of Budget Provisions: Appropriation items and sums in this Act

11 PART IV
12 BUDGET REDUCTION OR SURPLUS EXPENDITURE PLAN
13 The Judicial Branch shall participate in any Budget Reduction Plan or Surplus
14 Expenditure Plan in accordance with KRS Chapter 48, except that obligations essential to
15 the constitutional duties and use allowance of the Judicial Branch shall be exempt from
16 any Budget Reduction Plan. The level of participation in a Budget Reduction Plan shall
17 be at the discretion of the Chief Justice and shall not exceed the actual percentage of
18 revenue shortfall.
