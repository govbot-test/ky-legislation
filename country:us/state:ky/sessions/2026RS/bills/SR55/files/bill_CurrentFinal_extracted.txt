11 and support programs because of their unique needs; and

13 profound autism in April 2023. The research concluded that the percentage of eight-year-

16 autism; and

18 profound autism were more likely to be female, from racial and ethnic minority groups,
19 of low socioeconomic status, born preterm or with low birth weight, have self-injurious
20 behaviors, have seizure disorders, and have lower adaptive scores; and

23 of their unique challenges is critical;
