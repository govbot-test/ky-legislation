Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS HB 732/GA
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 336 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Hiring party" means a person who hires or enters into a contract with a
7 self-employed worker;
8 (b) "Portable benefit plan" means a benefit plan that is provided by a third-
9 party insurance provider chosen by a self-employed worker and assigned to
10 a beneficiary rather than to an employer or hiring party, including but not
11 limited to:
12 1. Health insurance;
13 2. Unemployment insurance;
14 3. Disability insurance;
15 4. Workers' compensation;
16 5. Life insurance; or
17 6. Retirement benefits; and
18 (c) "Self-employed worker" means a person who earns his or her living from
19 an independent pursuit of economic activities, rather than from a separate
20 company or individual.
21 (2) A public or private entity, including an internet or application-based company,
22 may voluntarily contribute funds to a portable benefit plan as a form of
23 compensation to a self-employed worker.
24 (3) Contributions to a portable benefit plan shall not be used as criterion for
25 determining a worker's employment classification.
