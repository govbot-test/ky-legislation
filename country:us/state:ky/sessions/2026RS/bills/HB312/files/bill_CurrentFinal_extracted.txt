1 AN ACT relating to concealed firearms and deadly weapons.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 237 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Provisional license" means a license to carry concealed firearms or other
7 deadly weapons issued pursuant to this section; and
8 (b) "Standard license" means a license to carry concealed firearms or other
9 deadly weapons issued pursuant to KRS 237.110.
10 (2) The Department of Kentucky State Police shall issue provisional licenses to carry
11 concealed firearms or other deadly weapons, or a combination thereof, to persons
12 who:
13 (a) Are eighteen (18) years of age but younger than twenty-one (21) years of
14 age; and
15 (b) Notwithstanding KRS 237.110(4)(c), otherwise meet the criteria set forth in
16 KRS 237.110(4) and the requirements of KRS 237.110(3).
17 (3) (a) Except as provided in this section, the issuance and regulation of
18 provisional licenses to carry concealed firearms and deadly weapons shall
19 be governed by KRS 237.110.
20 (b) Any reference in the Kentucky Revised Statutes to a license issued pursuant
21 to KRS 237.110 shall also be considered a reference to a provisional license
22 issued pursuant to this section.
23 (4) A provisional license issued pursuant to this section shall:
24 (a) Be valid throughout the Commonwealth and, except as provided in other
25 specific sections of the Kentucky Revised Statutes or federal law, permit the
26 holder of the provisional license to carry firearms, ammunition, other
27 deadly weapons, or a combination thereof, at any location in the
