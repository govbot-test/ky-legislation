3 WHEREAS, Caroline Whitlock Mudd was born June 19, 1961, in Campbellsville,

7 WHEREAS, Caroline Mudd had a quiet, steady way of caring for others that made

13 WHEREAS, Caroline Mudd was a devoted and loving wife to her husband Jimmy

18 WHEREAS, Caroline Mudd loved her sons wholeheartedly and without condition,

20 accomplishment being her role as a mother; and
21 WHEREAS, Caroline Mudd celebrated the successes of her sons, comforted them

23 WHEREAS, Caroline Mudd created a home with her nurturing heart, where her
24 family felt safe, supported, and deeply loved; and
25 WHEREAS, Caroline Mudd will continue to guide and inspire her sons throughout
26 their lives with the lessons she taught, through both her words and her example; and
27 WHEREAS, Caroline Mudd lived a life of remarkable professional dedication and
