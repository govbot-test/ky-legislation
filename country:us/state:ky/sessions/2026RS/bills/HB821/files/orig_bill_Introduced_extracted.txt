1 AN ACT relating to transportation.

3 Section 1. KRS 174.080 is amended to read as follows:
4 [(1) ]The secretary may promulgate administrative regulations in accordance with KRS
5 Chapter 13A to carry out the duties of this chapter and to comply with federal laws
6 regarding the use of federal transportation funds[not inconsistent with the provisions of
7 1974 Acts ch. 74.

12 Chapters 175, 176, 177 and 180].

14 The secretary of the Transportation Cabinet, the commissioner of the Department of
15 Vehicle Regulation, or a duly authorized agent, is hereby authorized and directed to
16 negotiate with other states for the purpose of developing and joining the "Nonresident
17 Violator Compact." The department may promulgate regulations in accordance with
18 KRS Chapter 13A concerning the administration of such compact and shall prescribe the
19 penalties and procedures for the required compliance.
