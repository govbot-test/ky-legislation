11 GENERAL PROVISIONS
12 1. Expenditure Authority: The Director of the Legislative Research
13 Commission, under the supervision of the Legislative Research Commission, may expend
14 any of the funds appropriated for legislative operation and administration in any lawful
15 manner and for any legal purpose consistent with the policies and practices of the
16 Commission. No executive agency or statute governing the executive agencies of state
17 government shall have the power to restrict or limit the actions of, or the expenditure of
18 funds appropriated to, the Legislative Research Commission for the Legislative Branch of
19 government.
20 2. Capitol and Capitol Annex Capital Construction Expenditures: Any
21 expenditure authorized by the Director of the Legislative Research Commission, under
22 the supervision of the Legislative Research Commission, relating to implementation of
23 KRS 56.463(4)(b), or relating to the Capitol Building, and funded by previous or current
24 appropriations to the Legislative Research Commission for the Legislative Branch of
25 government shall not be governed by KRS 7A.010, 7A.120, 45.750 to 45.810,
26 48.010(16), 48.020, and 48.110.

18 adjustment provided to state employees in the State/Executive Branch Budget.

20 shall be paid out of an administrative account which shall be funded by transfers of the
21 necessary moneys, in appropriate ratio, from the funds provided for in KRS 21.550 and
22 21.560.
