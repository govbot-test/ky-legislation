2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 141 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Earned income credit" means the credit allowed under Section 32 of the
7 Internal Revenue Code; and
8 (b) "Family size tax credit" means the credit allowed under KRS 141.066(3).
9 (2) For taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2027, but before January 1,
10 2031, there shall be allowed a refundable Kentucky working credit against the tax
11 imposed under KRS 141.020, with the ordering of the credit as provided in
12 Section 2 of this Act.
13 (3) The Kentucky working credit shall:
14 (a) Be equal to thirty percent (30%) of the federal earned income credit allowed
15 and claimed by the taxpayer for the taxable year; and
16 (b) Not exceed two thousand dollars ($2,000) per return.
17 (4) Taxpayers whose modified gross income qualifies them for the family size tax
18 credit shall not be eligible for the Kentucky working credit established in this
19 section.
20 (5) The taxpayer claiming the credit shall provide any information required by the
21 department to verify:
22 (a) A qualifying child; or
23 (b) Eligibility for the earned income credit.
24 (6) Part-year and nonresident taxpayers shall be allowed a prorated Kentucky
25 working credit based upon their portion of Kentucky adjusted gross income as
26 determined by KRS 141.019 to their adjusted gross income as defined in Section
27 62 of the Internal Revenue Code.
