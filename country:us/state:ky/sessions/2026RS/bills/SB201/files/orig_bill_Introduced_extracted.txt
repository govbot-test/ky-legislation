UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1695
1 AN ACT relating to Medicaid coverage for evaluation and management services.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 205 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 The Department for Medicaid Services and any managed care organization with whom
6 the department contracts for the delivery of Medicaid services shall provide coverage
7 for evaluation and management services. If the department or a managed care
8 organization establishes coverage limits for evaluation and management services,
9 coverage required under this section shall not be limited to fewer than two (2)
10 evaluation and management service units per Medicaid-participating provider, per
11 recipient, per date of service.
12 Section 2. If the Cabinet for Health and Family Services or the Department for
13 Medicaid Services determines that a state plan amendment, waiver, or any other form of
14 authorization or approval from any federal agency to implement Section 1 of this Act is
15 necessary to prevent the loss of federal funds or to comply with federal law, the cabinet
16 or department:
17 (1) Shall, within 90 days after the effective date of this section, request the
18 necessary federal authorization or approval to implement Section 1 of this Act; and
19 (2) May only delay implementation of the provisions of Section 1 of this Act for
20 which federal authorization or approval was deemed necessary until the federal
21 authorization or approval is granted.
22 Section 3. Sections 1 and 2 of this Act shall constitute the specific authorization
23 required under KRS 205.5372(1).
