Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1384
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 48.030 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) Each branch of government, during the preparation of its budget recommendation
5 and before its submission to the General Assembly, shall examine its statements
6 and estimates and shall make or cause to be made such further investigations with
7 such hearings as deemed advisable, and shall direct such changes or revisions as
8 warranted.
9 (2) Upon request, the Legislative Research Commission shall assist the judicial branch
10 in the preparation of its budget and supporting documents.
