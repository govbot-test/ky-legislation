1 AN ACT relating to Kentucky State University and declaring an emergency.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 164 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Kentucky State University, recognized as an 1890 land-grant university that is
6 Kentucky's only public Historically Black College or University (HBCU), shall be
7 a four (4) year residential polytechnic institution that focuses on highly technical,
8 industry-based applied learning and offers liberal studies and polytechnic
9 programs that are aligned with the workforce needs of the Commonwealth and
10 consistent with the historical mission of an HBCU.
11 (2) The General Assembly declares that a state of financial exigency exists at
12 Kentucky State University for five (5) years from the effective date of this Act or
13 until such a date that the General Assembly affirmatively declares, based upon
14 the recommendation of the Council on Postsecondary Education, that the
15 university's finances are stable, whichever occurs first.
16 (3) (a) Kentucky State University shall not enter into any obligation or make any
17 expenditure costing twenty thousand dollars ($20,000) or more without
18 prior approval of the Council on Postsecondary Education, including but
19 not limited to any purchase, contract, or increase due to a personnel action.
20 (b) Kentucky State University shall:
21 1. Provide a monthly report of university finances to the Council on
22 Postsecondary Education in the format requested by the council. The
23 council shall provide a quarterly update on the financial status of the
24 university to the Governor and the Legislative Research Commission;
25 2. Fully cooperate with the council in its exercise of the financial
26 oversight granted to the council under this subsection;
27 3. Timely provide all information and documentation deemed by the

21 satisfy any debt in excess of one thousand dollars ($1,000).
22 (2) Notwithstanding KRS 131.565 or any other statute to the contrary, the
23 Department of Revenue shall not decline the withholding of individual income
24 tax refunds for debts referred to the department by Kentucky State University.
