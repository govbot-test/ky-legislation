1 A RESOLUTION confirming the appointment of Michael McGhee to the Board of

5 Board of Directors of the Kentucky Employers' Mutual Insurance Authority for a term
6 expiring March 12, 2030; and
7 WHEREAS, the appointment is subject to Senate confirmation; and

9 KRS 342.807;

12 Kentucky:

16 notification of its adoption to Michael McGhee of Elkton, Todd County, Kentucky and to
17 Governor Andy Beshear, 703 Capital Avenue, Room 133, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
