UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 893
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 42 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Agency" means any state or local governmental department, cabinet,
7 institution, board, commission, office, or any other unit of government; and
8 (b) "Grant" means money, equipment, material, or services that may be
9 available to an individual or entity from any agency through an application
10 process administered by the agency.
11 (2) The Finance and Administration Cabinet shall establish a grant database to serve
12 as a single, unified entry point for individuals and entities to access and review
13 grant opportunities that are eligible for application. The database shall:
14 (a) Include the ability to use keyword searches to discover all available grant
15 opportunities; and
16 (b) Provide in the information section of the related grant all information
17 reported by agencies as required under subsection (4) of this section.
18 (3) The primary goals of the grant database include:
19 (a) Ensuring state and local grants are easily accessible for individuals and
20 entities of the Commonwealth; and
21 (b) Clearly stating requirements for grants to ensure submissions are qualified
22 for the potential grant.
23 (4) Beginning December 1, 2026, all agencies shall report quarterly to the Finance
24 and Administration Cabinet the grant opportunities that are currently available
25 from their agency. The report shall include:
26 (a) The title of the grant;
27 (b) The statute or administrative regulation that authorizes the grant;
