1 A RESOLUTION honoring the contributions of Christian women leaders in the
2 Commonwealth of Kentucky through She Leads Kentucky.
3 WHEREAS, the Commonwealth of Kentucky is blessed with a rich heritage of
4 faith, resilience, and leadership, exemplified by women who, guided by their Christian
5 values, lead businesses, ministries, and organizations, and excel in their fields with
6 distinction and purpose; and
7 WHEREAS, She Leads Kentucky, an organization dedicated to validating,
8 encouraging, and empowering Christian women, gives "honor where honor is due,"
9 recognizing those who operate at high levels of Christian excellence or whose names are
10 whispered by the Holy Spirit to its chairs and executive leadership team; and
11 WHEREAS, She Leads Kentucky is a ministry initiative of Divine Justice, Inc., a
12 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and is affiliated with She Leads America. The founder
13 and president of She Leads America is the Honorable Rev. Elizabeth Doyle. The founder
14 and executive director of She Leads Kentucky is Marti Ellen Thurman, JD, CCRP, and
15 the assistant director is Barbara Ann Jeter. The current executive leadership team of She
16 Leads Kentucky includes Emily Thurman, Rebecca Bowen, Cheryl Wise, and Carolyn
17 Corum; and
18 WHEREAS, the members of She Leads Kentucky, through their unwavering
19 commitment to their faith and their communities, inspire others by exemplifying the
20 values of integrity, servant leadership, and service; and
21 WHEREAS, their leadership, through She Leads Kentucky, strengthens the moral
22 and economic fabric of Kentucky, reflecting the principles of diligence, wisdom, and
23 grace that uplifts families, workplaces, and congregations across our great
24 Commonwealth; and
25 WHEREAS, by working with their legislative partner, the Honorable Senator
26 Lindsey Tichenor, She Leads Kentucky honors these extraordinary women by having
27 their names entered into the official record of the Kentucky General Assembly through an

2 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper for the Commonwealth of Kentucky to

6 NOW, THEREFORE,
7 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
8 Kentucky:
9 Section 1. The Senate of the Commonwealth of Kentucky recognizes and
10 honors the Christian women leaders identified by She Leads Kentucky for their
11 exceptional contributions to business, ministry, organization leadership, and field of
12 excellence.
13 Section 2. Senate recognizes that the purpose of She Leads Kentucky is to
14 validate, encourage, and empower by giving honor to Christian women who lead a
15 business, ministry, organization, or operate at high levels of Christian excellence in their
16 field, or where the Holy Spirit whispers their name to its chairs or executive leadership
17 team.
18 Section 3. The Senate commends She Leads Kentucky for its mission to
19 validate, encourage, and empower these women, giving honor where honor is due as
20 guided by faith and the prompting of the Holy Spirit.
21 Section 4. The Senate encourages all Kentuckians to celebrate the achievements
22 of these remarkable women and to support their continued efforts to lead with purpose,
23 integrity, and devotion to the betterment of our state and its people.
