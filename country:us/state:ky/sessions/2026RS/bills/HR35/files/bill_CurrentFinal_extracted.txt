2 Anesthetists Day in Kentucky.
3 WHEREAS, certified registered nurse anesthetists (CRNAs) are advanced practice
4 registered nurses who are among the most trusted healthcare professionals, have been
5 providing anesthesia care to patients in the United States for more than 150 years, and
6 safely administering more than 45 million anesthetics to patients each year; and
7 WHEREAS, CRNAs are the primary anesthesia providers in rural communities,
8 continue to be the primary providers of anesthesia care to United States military
9 personnel, and comprise two-thirds of the anesthesia providers in the Commonwealth;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, CRNAs practice in every setting in which anesthesia is delivered
12 including traditional hospital surgical suites and obstetrical delivery rooms, the offices of
13 dentists, podiatrists, ophthalmologists, and plastic surgeons, and ambulatory surgical
14 centers; and
15 WHEREAS, CRNAs are the sole providers of anesthesia services in nearly half of
16 the counties in Kentucky; and
17 WHEREAS, CRNAs are qualified to make independent judgments regarding all
18 aspects of anesthesia care based on their education, training, and licensure; and
19 WHEREAS, CRNAs have been the main providers of anesthesia care to U.S.
20 military personnel on the front lines since the Civil War;
21 NOW, THEREFORE,
22 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
23 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
24 Section 1. The members of the House of Representatives do hereby recognize
25 January 21, 2026, as Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists Day in Kentucky.
26 Section 2. When the House of Representatives adjourns this day, it does so in
27 honor, recognition, and celebration of Kentucky's certified registered nurse anesthetists
