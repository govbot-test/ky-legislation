1 AN ACT relating to the promotion of organ and bone marrow donation.
2 WHEREAS, the General Assembly declares that there is a great need for living
3 organ and bone marrow donors in the Commonwealth; and
4 WHEREAS, potential living donors should be able to perform their lifesaving
5 service without the risk of loss of income or employment; and
6 WHEREAS, encouraging donation by living donors is in the interest of the public
7 health and is a public purpose;
8 NOW, THEREFORE,
9 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
10 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 141 IS CREATED TO
11 READ AS FOLLOWS:
12 (1) As used in this section:
13 (a) "Leave of absence period":
14 1. Means the period, not exceeding the hourly equivalent of forty (40)
15 hours for each living donor, during which an employer provides a
16 paid leave of absence to a living donor for the specific purpose of
17 organ or bone marrow donation; and
18 2. Does not include a period during which a living donor utilizes any
19 annual or sick leave that the living donor has been granted by the
20 employer;
21 (b) "Living donor" means an employee who is a Kentucky resident and is
22 absent from work solely for the purpose of donating an organ or bone
23 marrow; and
24 (c) "Organ" means any part of a human intestine, kidney, liver, lung,
25 pancreas, or uterus.
26 (2) For taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2027, but before January 1,
27 2031, there is hereby established a nonrefundable employers' organ and bone
