1 AN ACT relating to children in foster care.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 199 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 As used in Sections 1 to 3 of this Act:
6 (1) "Cabinet" means the Cabinet for Health and Family Services; and
7 (2) "Trust fund" means the foster child tutoring and academic assistance trust fund
8 established in Section 3 of this Act.
9 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 199 IS CREATED TO
10 READ AS FOLLOWS:
11 (1) The cabinet shall establish and operate a Tutoring and Academic Assistance
12 Grant program for children in foster care.
13 (2) The purpose of the program shall be to coordinate and facilitate tutoring and
14 academic assistance between school districts and community organizations in
15 order to improve graduation rates and create stability in learning careers for
16 children in foster care.
17 (3) The cabinet shall establish processes and procedures for school districts and
18 community organizations to apply for grants from the trust fund in order to
19 provide tutoring and academic assistance services to children in foster care.
20 SECTION 3. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 199 IS CREATED TO
21 READ AS FOLLOWS:
22 (1) There is hereby established in the State Treasury a trust and agency account to be
23 known as the foster child tutoring and academic assistance trust fund. The fund
24 shall consist of grants, contributions, appropriations, or other moneys made
25 available for the purposes of the trust fund.
26 (2) The fund shall be administered by the cabinet.
27 (3) Moneys in the trust fund shall be used to support tutoring and academic
