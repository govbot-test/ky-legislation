UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2364
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 501.080 is amended to read as follows:
4 Intoxication is a defense to a criminal charge only if the intoxication[such condition]
5 either:
6 (1) Negates[Negatives] the existence of an element of the offense; or
7 (2) Is not voluntarily produced and deprives the defendant of substantial capacity to
8 either:[ to ]
9 (a) Appreciate the criminality of his or her conduct; or[ to ]
10 (b) Conform his or her conduct to the requirements of the law.
