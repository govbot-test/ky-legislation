1 A RESOLUTION confirming the reappointment of John Stovall to the Kentucky
2 Lottery Corporation Board of Directors.
3 WHEREAS, on October 9, 2025, in accordance with KRS 154A.030 and by virtue
4 of Executive Order 2025-647, Governor Andy Beshear reappointed John Stovall to the
5 Kentucky Lottery Corporation Board of Directors for a term expiring November 28,
6 2029; and
7 WHEREAS, the reappointment is subject to Senate confirmation; and
8 WHEREAS, John Stovall has been determined to meet the requirements of KRS
9 154A.030;
10 NOW, THEREFORE,
11 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
12 Kentucky:
13 Section 1. The Senate hereby confirms the reappointment of John Stovall to the
14 Kentucky Lottery Corporation Board of Directors.
15 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
16 notification of its adoption to John Stovall of Louisville, Jefferson County, Kentucky, and
17 to Governor Andy Beshear, 703 Capital Avenue, Room 133, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
