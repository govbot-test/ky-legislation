1 AN ACT relating to Medicaid coverage for adult day health care and in-home
2 attendant care services.
3 WHEREAS, many elderly Kentuckians enrolled in the Medicaid program are in
4 need of and greatly benefit from in-home attendant care services to ensure their safety
5 and well-being at home; and
6 WHEREAS, while access to in-home attendant care services is critical to the safety,
7 well-being, and ability of elderly Kentuckians to age in place, they also benefit greatly
8 from access to adult day health care services; and
9 WHEREAS, adult day health care centers offer elderly Kentuckians an opportunity
10 for mental and psychological stimulation through regular interactions with other
11 individuals their own age; and
12 WHEREAS, when adult day health care services are not available, many elderly
13 individuals suffer isolation and loneliness even when receiving in-home attendant care
14 services; and
15 WHEREAS, adult day health care services offer additional personalized care and
16 engagement opportunities that can significantly improve mental health and health care
17 outcomes of service recipients; and
18 WHEREAS, the decision by the Department for Medicaid Services to establish a
19 single coverage limit for adult day health care services and in-home attendant care
20 services has forced many elderly Kentuckians to choose between adult day health care
21 services or in-home attendant care services; and
22 WHEREAS, access to adequate and necessary services and supports for the elderly
23 is critical to ensuring such individuals are able to age in place; and
24 WHEREAS, when elderly individuals are not able to access needed services and
25 support, they often have no choice but to seek care in a skilled nursing facility or assisted
26 living facility; and
27 WHEREAS, evidence suggests that health outcomes for elderly individuals are

3 WHEREAS, residential or institutionalized care is more costly and less cost-

5 NOW, THEREFORE,
6 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
7 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 205 IS CREATED TO
8 READ AS FOLLOWS:
9 (1) (a) The Department for Medicaid Services shall include coverage for adult day
10 health care services and in-home attendant, or personal care, services in
11 each 1915(c) waiver program administered by the department that enrolls
12 adult participants.
13 (b) Coverage required under this section shall include at least forty (40) hours
14 per week of adult day health care services, and separately at least forty (40)
15 hours per week of in-home attendant, or personal care, services.
16 (c) Coverage limits established in this section shall not provide for a single or
17 combined coverage limit for the two (2) services or in any way aggregate
18 coverage limits for adult day health care services and in-home attendant, or
19 personal care, services.
20 (2) The Department for Medicaid Services shall, within ninety (90) days after the
21 effective date of this Act, promulgate administrative regulations in accordance
22 with KRS Chapter 13A to implement this section.
23 Section 2. If the Cabinet for Health and Family Services or the Department for
24 Medicaid Services determines that a state plan amendment, waiver, or any other form of
25 authorization or approval from any federal agency to implement Section 1 of this Act is
26 necessary to prevent the loss of federal funds or to comply with federal law, the cabinet
27 or department:
