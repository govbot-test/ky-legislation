3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF SUBTITLE 17A OF KRS CHAPTER 304
4 IS CREATED TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Biosimilar" means any biological product that is:
7 1. Licensed under 42 U.S.C. sec. 262(k); and
8 2. Listed in the FDA's Database of Licensed Biological Products, also
9 referred to as the Purple Book, as biosimilar to or interchangeable
10 with a reference biological product;
11 (b) "Brand drug" means:
12 1. A drug for which an application has been approved under 21 U.S.C.
13 sec. 355(c); or
14 2. A biological product, other than a biosimilar, that is licensed under 42
15 U.S.C. sec. 262(a);
16 (c) "FDA" means the United States Food and Drug Administration;
17 (d) "Formulary" means a list of prescription drugs that:
18 1. Is developed by:
19 a. A Pharmacy and Therapeutics Committee; or
20 b. Other clinical or pharmacy experts; and
21 2. Represents a health plan's prescription drugs approved for use;
22 (e) "Generic drug" means a drug:
23 1. For which an application has been approved under 21 U.S.C. 355(j);
24 and
25 2. Which is listed in the FDA's Approved Drug Products with
26 Therapeutic Equivalence Evaluations, also referred to as the Orange
27 Book, as therapeutically equivalent to a reference listed drug, even if
