UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2346
1 AN ACT relating to alcoholic beverages.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 241.065 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The number of quota retail package licenses issued by the Alcoholic Beverage
5 Control Board to licensees in counties containing cities of the first class, and
6 including such cities, shall not exceed a number equal to one (1) for every one
7 thousand five hundred (1,500) persons resident in such county.
8 (2) In order that a fixed and approved standard of population as prescribed in
9 subsection (1) of this section may be adopted, the annual estimates of population as
10 determined by the Kentucky State Data Center at the University of Louisville shall
11 be used in every year except a census year, and during a census year the United
12 States government census figures of population shall be controlling.
13 (3) A quota retail package license in a county containing a city of the first class shall
14 not be granted for premises located closer than one (1) mile from the nearest
15 other quota retail package license premises. The distance between locations of
16 quota retail package licenses as prescribed by this section shall be measured by
17 following the lesser of the shortest route of ordinary pedestrian travel or the
18 shortest route of ordinary vehicular travel along public roads or thoroughfares
19 from the main entrances of both quota retail package license premises.
20 (4) The location or status of any establishment operating with a quota retail package
21 license on the effective date of this Act shall not be affected by subsection (3) of
22 this section. Subsection (3) of this section shall not impair or limit the ability of
23 the location's owner or operator to renew the license for a location exempted
24 under this subsection.
25 (5) Any establishment exempted by subsection (4) of this section shall lose its exempt
26 status if the quota retail package license for that premises has lapsed, been
27 revoked, failed to renew, sold, transferred, or has become inactive or unavailable
