1 A RESOLUTION confirming the appointment of Steve Zahn to the Kentucky Film
2 Leadership Council.
3 WHEREAS, on July 1, 2025, in accordance with KRS 154.12-282 and by virtue of
4 Executive Order 2025-386, Governor Andy Beshear appointed Steve Zahn to the
5 Kentucky Film Leadership Council for a term expiring June 30, 2028; and
6 WHEREAS, the appointment is subject to Senate confirmation; and
7 WHEREAS, Steve Zahn has been determined to meet the requirements of KRS
8 154.12-282, representing a film profession;
9 NOW, THEREFORE,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
11 Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The Senate hereby confirms the appointment of Steve Zahn as a
13 member of the Kentucky Film Leadership Council.
14 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
15 notification of its adoption to Steve Zahn of Georgetown, Scott County, Kentucky, and to
16 Governor Andy Beshear, 703 Capital Avenue, Room 133, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
