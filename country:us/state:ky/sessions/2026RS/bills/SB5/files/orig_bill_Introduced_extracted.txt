UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1746
1 AN ACT relating to Kentucky-grown agricultural product procurement.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 158 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Agricultural product" has the same meaning as in KRS 260.016; and
7 (b) "Kentucky-grown agricultural product" has the same meaning as in KRS
8 260.016.
9 (2) A local school board or local school district participating in any of the United
10 States Department of Agriculture Child Nutrition Programs may purchase
11 Kentucky-grown agricultural products in accordance with federal law and shall
12 not be subject to KRS 45A.345 to 45A.460 or 424.260.
13 (3) This section shall not be construed to exempt a local school board or local school
14 district from any other requirement established by state or federal law.
