1 AN ACT relating to data centers.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF SUBCHAPTER 10 OF KRS CHAPTER
4 224 IS CREATED TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "data center" means a centralized facility that:
6 (a) Is used primarily or exclusively for electronic information services,
7 including but not limited to the management, storage, processing, and
8 dissemination of electronic data and information through the use of
9 computer systems, servers, networking equipment, and related components;
10 (b) Has electrical load that is either fixed, portable, or distributable, including
11 but not limited to structures that are not affixed to the ground or are easily
12 removed from a location; and
13 (c) Has, along with all loads of affiliated companies and companies with
14 common ownership, an actual or expected monthly peak electric load
15 requirement of greater than fifty (50) megawatts.
16 (2) The requirements of this section shall only apply to a data center that is located
17 on land that was valued and taxed as agricultural land under KRS 132.450 at any
18 time in the ten (10) years preceding the commencement of the construction of the
19 data center.
20 (3) Prior to commencing the operation of a data center, its owners shall file with the
21 cabinet, in the form and manner as the cabinet may require, a decommissioning
22 plan that describes how the data center will be decommissioned and dismantled at
23 the end of its useful life, which shall include, at a minimum:
24 (a) Removal of all above-ground facilities and any underground components
25 and foundations of above-ground facilities to a depth that the cabinet deems
26 necessary for the land to be returned to agricultural purposes;
27 (b) Returning the land to a substantially similar state as it was prior to the

2 (c) Securing a bond or other similar security for the project to assure financial
3 performance of the decommissioning obligation, provided that:
4 1. The amount of the proposed bond or similar security shall be
5 determined by an independent, licensed engineer who has no financial

7 data center is located. The proposed amount of the bond or similar
8 security shall be the net present value of the total estimated cost of
9 completing the decommissioning plan, less the current net salvage
10 value of the data center's components;
11 2. The bond or other similar security is provided by an insurance
12 company or surety that is approved by the cabinet, and it names the
13 cabinet as the primary beneficiary; and
14 3, The bond or other similar security provides that at least thirty (30)
15 days prior to its cancellation or lapse, the surety shall notify the data
16 center owner and the cabinet of the impending cancellation or lapse.
17 The notice shall specify the reason for the cancellation or lapse and
18 provide the data center owner the opportunity to cure the cancellation
19 or lapse prior to it becoming effective. If the data center owner fails to
20 cure the cancellation or lapse within fifteen (15) days of receiving the
21 notice, or if the data center ceases to operate for thirty (30) or more
22 days, the cabinet may make a demand on the bond and initiate and
23 complete the decommissioning plan.
