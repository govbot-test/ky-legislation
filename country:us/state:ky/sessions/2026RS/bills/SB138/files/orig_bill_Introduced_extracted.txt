3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 507 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "abuse or neglect" means the infliction of physical pain,
6 physical or mental injury, or the deprivation of services by a person that are
7 necessary to maintain the health and welfare of a child.
8 (2) A person is guilty of child abuse homicide when:
9 (a) He or she intentionally abuses or neglects another person who is under
10 eighteen (18) years old; and
11 (b) The death is a result of the abuse or neglect.
12 (3) Child abuse homicide is a capital offense.
