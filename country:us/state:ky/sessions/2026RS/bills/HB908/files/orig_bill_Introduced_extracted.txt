UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2445
1 AN ACT relating to government proceedings.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 13A.3102 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) An ordinary administrative regulation with a last effective date on or after March 1,
5 2013, shall expire seven (7) years after its last effective date, except as provided by
6 the certification process in KRS 13A.3104.
7 (2) An ordinary administrative regulation with a last effective date before March 1,
8 2013, shall expire on March 1, 2020, except as provided by the certification process
9 in KRS 13A.3104.
10 (3) For all administrative regulations that expire under this section or KRS 13A.3104,
11 the regulations compiler shall:
12 (a) Delete them from the Kentucky Administrative Regulations Service;
13 (b) Add them to the list of ineffective administrative regulations; and
14 (c) [Beginning on September 1, 2020, and ]At least once every six (6) months[
15 thereafter], publish a list of administrative regulations that have expired since
16 the most recent previous list was published under this paragraph.
17 (4) [Within three (3) months of June 27, 2019, and ]At least once every six (6) months[
18 thereafter], the regulations compiler shall publish a list of existing administrative
19 regulations and their corresponding last effective dates.
