1 NOW, THEREFORE,
2 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
3 Kentucky:
4 Section 1. The Senate hereby honors the Kentucky Association for
5 Environmental Education (KAEE) on the occasion of its 50th anniversary.
6 Section 2. The Senate recognizes KAEE for its exemplary governance, long-
7 term organizational planning, and service to environmental education.
8 Section 3. The Senate encourages continued collaboration among KAEE, state
9 agencies, universities, and community partners to expand high-quality environmental
10 education for all ages and communities.
11 Section 4. The Senate commends KAEE’s past and present presidents and
12 board members for five decades of service.
13 Section 5. The Senate encourages public participation in the 2026 annual
14 conference in Lexington, Kentucky, from November 12th through the 14th.
15 Section 6. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit six copies of this
16 Resolution to Senator Robin Webb for presentation to KAEE Chair Louis Ross, KAEE
17 Executive Director Ashley Hoffman, Secretary Jamie Link of the Kentucky Education
18 and Labor Cabinet, Secretary Rebecca Goodman of the Energy and Environment
19 Cabinet, and President Aaron Thompson of the Kentucky Council on Postsecondary
20 Education.
