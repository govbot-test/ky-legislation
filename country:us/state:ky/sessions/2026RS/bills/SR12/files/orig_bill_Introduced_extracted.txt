3 WHEREAS, James Allen Ewing was born on January 22, 1942, to John Henry and

5 WHEREAS, James Allen Ewing graduated from Greensburg High School where he

12 professional organizations and enjoyed photography, hunting, and spending time with
13 family and friends; and

18 NOW, THEREFORE,
19 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
20 Kentucky:
21 Section 1. The Senate expresses its most profound sympathy upon the passing
22 of James Allen Ewing and extends heartfelt condolences to his family, friends, and
23 community.
24 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor and loving
25 memory of James Allen Ewing.
