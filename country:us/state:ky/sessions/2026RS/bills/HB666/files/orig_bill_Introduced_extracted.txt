1 AN ACT relating to taxation.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 132.027 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) No city or urban-county government shall levy a tax rate which exceeds the
5 compensating tax rate defined in KRS 132.010 until the city or urban-county
6 government has complied with the provisions of subsection (2) of this section.
7 (2) (a) Cities or urban-county governments proposing to levy a tax rate which
8 exceeds the compensating tax rate defined in KRS 132.010 shall hold a public
9 hearing to hear comments from the public regarding the proposed tax rate.
10 The hearing shall be held in the principal office of the taxing district, or, in the
11 event the taxing district has no office, or the office is not suitable for a
12 hearing, the hearing shall be held in a suitable facility as near as possible to
13 the geographic center of the district.
14 (b) The city or urban-county government shall advertise the hearing by causing to
15 be published at least twice in two (2) consecutive weeks, in the newspaper of
16 largest circulation in the county, a display type advertisement of not less than
17 twelve (12) column inches, the following:
18 1. The tax rate levied in the preceding year, and the revenue produced by
19 that rate;
20 2. The tax rate proposed for the current year and the revenue expected to
21 be produced by that rate;
22 3. The compensating tax rate and the revenue expected from it;
23 4. The revenue expected from new property and personal property;
24 5. The general areas to which revenue in excess of the revenue produced in
25 the preceding year is to be allocated;
26 6. A time and place for the public hearing which shall be held not less than
27 seven (7) days nor more than ten (10) days after the day the second
