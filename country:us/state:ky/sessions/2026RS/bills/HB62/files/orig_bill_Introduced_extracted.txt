UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 201
1 AN ACT relating to unlawful employment.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 434 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "unauthorized alien" means a person who is unlawfully
6 present in the United States under the federal Immigration and Nationality Act, 8
7 U.S.C. sec. 1101 et seq.
8 (2) A person is guilty of unlawful employment when he or she is unlawfully present
9 in the United States and knowingly seeks employment or performs work in
10 exchange for any form of remuneration.
11 (3) Unlawful employment is a Class A misdemeanor.
