2 WHEREAS, Kentucky's vibrant arts scene and creative cultural resources serve as
3 economic drivers, strengthen regional identities, attract tourists, and assist with
4 community development; and
5 WHEREAS, the arts and culture industry generates $6.9 billion annually,
6 representing 2.5 percent of Kentucky's gross domestic product and creating 51,094 jobs;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, the arts contribute to the success of our children through education and
9 participation in a variety of arts programs, engaging and inspiring students and teaching
10 them creative thinking skills; and
11 WHEREAS, Kentucky's creative arts industries play a valuable role in developing
12 the innovative workforce that will be necessary for future community and economic
13 growth and for the attraction and retention of top talent; and
14 WHEREAS, a thriving arts community attracts tourists who desire to learn,
15 experience, and discover Kentucky's cultural attractions, and many cultural tourists
16 invigorate local economies by staying longer and spending more money than other
17 tourists; and
18 WHEREAS, Kentucky's rich culture and heritage are reflected in its arts scene and
19 talented artisans, which are nationally and internationally recognized;
20 NOW, THEREFORE,
21 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
22 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
23 Section 1. The House of Representatives hereby recognizes January 22, 2026,
24 as Kentucky Arts Day.
25 Section 2. The Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to transmit a
26 copy of this Resolution to Representative Lindsey Burke.

UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1850
1 A RESOLUTION recognizing January 22, 2026, as Kentucky Arts Day.
2 WHEREAS, Kentucky's vibrant arts scene and creative cultural resources serve as
3 economic drivers, strengthen regional identities, attract tourists, and assist with
4 community development; and
5 WHEREAS, the arts and culture industry generates $6.9 billion annually,
6 representing 2.5 percent of Kentucky's gross domestic product and creating 51,094 jobs;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, the arts contribute to the success of our children through education and
9 participation in a variety of arts programs, engaging and inspiring students and teaching
10 them creative thinking skills; and
11 WHEREAS, Kentucky's creative arts industries play a valuable role in developing
12 the innovative workforce that will be necessary for future community and economic
13 growth and for the attraction and retention of top talent; and
14 WHEREAS, a thriving arts community attracts tourists who desire to learn,
15 experience, and discover Kentucky's cultural attractions, and many cultural tourists
16 invigorate local economies by staying longer and spending more money than other
17 tourists; and
18 WHEREAS, Kentucky's rich culture and heritage are reflected in its arts scene and
19 talented artisans, which are nationally and internationally recognized;
20 NOW, THEREFORE,
21 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
22 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
23 Section 1. The House of Representatives hereby recognizes January 22, 2026,
24 as Kentucky Arts Day.
25 Section 2. The Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to transmit a
26 copy of this Resolution to Representative Lindsey Burke.
