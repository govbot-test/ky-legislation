UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 570
1 A RESOLUTION confirming the appointment of David Wallace to the Kentucky
2 State Fair Board.
3 WHEREAS, on March 31, 2026, in accordance with KRS 247.090 and by virtue of
4 Executive Order 2026-001, Commissioner Jonathan Shell appointed David Wallace to
5 the Kentucky State Fair Board for a term expiring March 31, 2030; and
6 WHEREAS, the appointment is subject to Senate confirmation; and
7 WHEREAS, David Wallace has been determined to meet the requirements of KRS
8 247.090, representing agriculture and agriculture-related business;
9 NOW, THEREFORE,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
11 Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The Senate hereby confirms the appointment of David Wallace to the
13 Kentucky State Fair Board.
14 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
15 notification of its adoption to David Wallace of Boone County, Kentucky, and to
16 Commissioner Jonathan Shell, Corporate Drive Complex, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
