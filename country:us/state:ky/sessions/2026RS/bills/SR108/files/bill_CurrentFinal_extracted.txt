18 NOW, THEREFORE,
19 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
20 Kentucky:
21 Section 1. The members of the Senate hereby express their most profound
22 sympathy upon the passing of Dr. Thomas "Tom" Shelton.
23 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor and loving
24 memory of Dr. Thomas "Tom" Shelton.

26 Resolution to Senator Reginald Thomas for delivery to the family.
