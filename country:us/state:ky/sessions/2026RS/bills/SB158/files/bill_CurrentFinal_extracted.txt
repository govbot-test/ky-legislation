6 SECTION 3. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 367 IS CREATED TO
7 READ AS FOLLOWS:
8 (1) (a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this subsection, a vehicle financial
9 protection product offered, sold, or given to a consumer in this state shall
10 comply with Sections 1 to 7 of this Act.
11 (b) Paragraph (a) of this subsection shall not apply to any debt waiver offered
12 by a state or federal bank or credit union in compliance with applicable
13 state and federal law.

15 products issued before, on, or after the effective date of this Act shall not be
16 considered insurance.

18 product offered prior to the effective date of this Act.
