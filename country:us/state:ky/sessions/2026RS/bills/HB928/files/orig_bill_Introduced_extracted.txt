1 AN ACT relating to the General Assembly.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 7 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The Special Committee on Sexual Misconduct is hereby established as an
6 independent authority and shall be attached to the Legislative Research
7 Commission for administrative purposes only.
8 (2) The committee shall be composed of the following eight (8) members:
9 (a) Three (3) members of the Senate, appointed by the President of the Senate;
10 (b) One (1) member of the Senate, appointed by the Minority Floor Leader of
11 the Senate;
12 (c) Three (3) members of the House of Representatives, appointed by the
13 Speaker of the House of Representatives; and
14 (d) One (1) member of the House of Representatives, appointed by the Minority
15 Floor Leader of the House of Representatives.
16 (3) The committee shall:
17 (a) Receive and investigate complaints submitted by an employee of the
18 Legislative Research Commission or a member of the public alleging that a
19 member of the General Assembly has committed sexual harassment, sexual
20 abuse, or sexual assault against:
21 1. An employee of the Legislative Research Commission;
22 2. Another member of the General Assembly; or
23 3. A member of the public;
24 (b) Upon completion of its investigation, but no later than ninety (90) days after
25 receipt of a complaint submitted in accordance with paragraph (a) of this
26 subsection, submit a report of its findings and any recommended punitive
27 actions to be taken, if any, to the:
