UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2373
1 AN ACT relating to collective bargaining agreements.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 160 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Bargain collectively" means to carry out in good faith the mutual
7 obligation of the parties, or their representatives, to:
8 1. Meet at reasonable times, including meetings in advance of the
9 budget-making process;
10 2. Negotiate in good faith with respect to wages, hours, and other
11 conditions of employment;
12 3. Negotiate an agreement;
13 4. Negotiate any question arising under any agreement; and
14 5. Execute a written contract incorporating any agreement reached.
15 The obligation shall not be interpreted to compel either party to agree to a
16 proposal or require either party to make a concession; and
17 (b) "Local association" means any organization that acts as a representative
18 for members employed by a school or school district.
19 (2) A local board of education and any local association shall bargain collectively in
20 pursuit of a collective bargaining agreement between a local board of education
21 and any local association that represents the interests of school employees or
22 personnel.
23 (3) A local board of education or local association found in violation of subsection
24 (2) of this section by a court of competent jurisdiction shall owe to the aggrieved
25 party an amount not to exceed five hundred dollars ($500).
