1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor and loving memory of Granville
2 Reid, Jr.
3 WHEREAS, on January 10, 2026, Granville Reid, Jr. traversed these earthly bounds
4 at the age of 81; and
5 WHEREAS, Granville Reid, Jr. was born on July 22, 1944, in Manchester,
6 Kentucky, to Daisy and Granville Reid, Sr.; and
7 WHEREAS, Granville Reid, Jr. is survived by his loving wife, Aileen Smith Reid;
8 and his children, David Wayne Reid, Susan Reid (Troy) Fields, and Farrah Nichole
9 McIntosh; and was a cherished great-grandfather to Alexander McIntosh; and
10 WHEREAS, Granville Reid, Jr., is also survived by his sisters, Phyliss (Jennings)
11 Fields, Brenda Reid Crawford, and Sandra Reid; and
12 WHEREAS, Granville Reid, Jr. was preceded in death by his sons, Logan Ray
13 Marlow and Todd Tyron Marlow; his parents; and his brothers and sisters, Shirley Reid,
14 Bobby Reid, Kenneth Reid, Charles Reid, Peggy Marshall, Shelby Warren, and Leah
15 Fisher; and
16 WHEREAS, Granville Reid, Jr. was held as a cherished father, grandfather, and
17 mentor to so many children in Clay County, Kentucky, through his wife's babysitting
18 activities. Granville Reid Jr. and his wife Aileen had a profound impact on the lives of so
19 many children who were not their own but who they nevertheless loved and nurtured as if
20 they were; and
21 WHEREAS, Granville Reid, Jr. will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved
22 him, leaving a void that can never be filled;
23 NOW, THEREFORE,
24 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
25 Kentucky:
26 Section 1. The Senate hereby honors and remembers the life and legacy of
27 Granville Reid, Jr. upon his passing.
