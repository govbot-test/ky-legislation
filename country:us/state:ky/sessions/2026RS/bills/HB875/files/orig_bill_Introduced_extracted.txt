1 AN ACT relating to interscholastic athletics.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 156 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) For the purposes of this section:
6 (a) "Appropriate documentation" means:
7 1. The results of a nationally recognized standardized test or its
8 equivalent as determined by the department;
9 2. A portfolio of a student's school work from the previous year
10 demonstrating the student's performance is at the student's grade
11 level, as determined at the discretion of the public school's principal or
12 designee; or
13 3. If the student was enrolled in a public school or a private, parochial,
14 or church school certified under KRS 156.160 during the preceding
15 school year, records demonstrating the student's eligibility based on
16 the student's academic success at that school;
17 (b) "Department" means the Kentucky Department of Education or the agency
18 designated by the Kentucky Board of Education to manage interscholastic
19 athletics in the common schools under KRS 156.070; and
20 (c) "Nonpublic school" means a private, parochial, or church school
21 regardless of whether the school is certified under KRS 156.160, that is not
22 already participating in interscholastic athletics as managed by the
23 department.
24 (2) A student enrolled in a nonpublic school shall be eligible to participate in
25 interscholastic athletics sponsored or engaged in by the public school to which
26 the student would be assigned according to the attendance policies adopted by the
27 local board of education of the school district in which the student resides. If the
