2 WHEREAS, the members of the Senate have traditionally taken great pleasure in

4 the children of this Commonwealth who are most in need; and
5 WHEREAS, for 83 years, the CSX Santa Train has been one of the nation's most
6 cherished holiday traditions, bringing joy and hope to the heart of Appalachia; and
7 WHEREAS, since 1943, the Santa Train has made a 110-mile journey through
8 Kentucky, Virginia, and Tennessee, with Santa and his elves tossing toys, handing out
9 backpacks filled with supplies, and spreading Christmas cheer to children and their
10 families; and

13 who were gathered along its route, which spanned from Shelbiana, Kentucky, to
14 Kingsport, Tennessee; and

23 exemplary charitable events at this time;
24 NOW, THEREFORE,
25 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
