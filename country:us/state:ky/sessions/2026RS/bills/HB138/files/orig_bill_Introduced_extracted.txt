10 paragraph provides for nine (9) levels of minimum salaries based
11 upon the population of the county in the year prior to the election of

13 Local Government shall, by November 1 of each year preceding the
14 election of the coroner, certify for each county the population group
15 applicable to each county based on the most recent estimates of the
16 United States Department of Commerce, Bureau of the Census. The
17 salary schedule shall remain as determined by the Department for
18 Local Government, regardless of changes in the population estimates
19 or the actual census count that may occur during the term for which
20 the coroner has been elected or appointed.

22 within each population group. A coroner's salary shall be at a
23 minimum equal to the amount stated for the first step within the
24 applicable population group for the first year or portion thereof
25 the coroner serves in office. Thereafter, on January 1 of each
26 subsequent year, the coroner's salary shall be advanced
27 automatically to the next step in the salary schedule for the

25 commissioner of the Department for Local Government, to obtain the
26 minimum amount of approved training in any year, the coroner shall
27 lose all training incentives previously accumulated.
