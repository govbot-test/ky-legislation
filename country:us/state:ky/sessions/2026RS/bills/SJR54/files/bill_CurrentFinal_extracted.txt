3 WHEREAS, this matching funding model strengthens community ownership and

13 and Archives and the Cabinet for Health and Family Services to cooperate in exploring

15 in areas where enrollment is lower than the state average, to children from low-income
16 families, and to children living in foster care. Opportunities to enroll children as part of
17 the birth certificate completion process as well as other ways to increase participation
18 shall be considered.
19 Section 2. The Department for Libraries and Archives and the Cabinet for
20 Health and Family Services are directed to submit a report on progress in identifying
21 ways to increase enrollment in the program to the Governor and the Legislative Research
22 Commission by November 1, 2026.
