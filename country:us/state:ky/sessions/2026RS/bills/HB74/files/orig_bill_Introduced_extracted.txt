4 Section 1. Are you in favor of amending the current Constitution of Kentucky
5 to create a new section restricting government expenditures and requiring voter approval

8 SECTION 2. IT IS PROPOSED THAT A NEW SECTION BE ADDED TO
9 THE CONSTITUTION OF KENTUCKY TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
10 (1) All forms of government, including the state, counties, consolidated local
11 governments, urban-county governments, unified local governments, charter counties,
12 special purpose districts, and municipal utilities, shall have voter approval, through a
13 ballot question, in advance of any new tax or fee creation, rate increase, or an
14 extension of an expiring tax or fee.
15 (2) (a) Voter approval shall be sought through a ballot question in the next
16 general election.
17 (b) A ballot question seeking voter approval under this section shall provide the
18 following information:
19 1. A short summary of the new tax, tax rate increase, fee, or extension of an
20 expiring tax;
21 2. A description of what the tax or fee is imposed upon;
22 3. The rate at which the tax is assessed;
23 4. The median cost to the taxpayer per year;
24 5. Where the proceeds of the tax or fee will go; and
25 6. Two short statements, one in support and one in opposition to the ballot
26 question, that are nonpartisan.
