UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1743
1 A RESOLUTION honoring John and Debbie Rogers, of Somerset, Kentucky, on
2 the occasion of their 50th wedding anniversary.
3 WHEREAS, John and Debbie Rogers were married on December 12, 1975; and
4 WHEREAS, their union yielded three children, Ben Rogers, Samantha Richardson,
5 and Lauren New; and five grandchildren, Hadley Rogers, Will Richardson, Olivia
6 Richardson, Charlotte New, and Skyler New; and
7 WHEREAS, John Rogers is a former State Senator and Republican leader; and
8 WHEREAS, less than six percent of all married couples surpass 50 years of
9 marriage; and
10 WHEREAS, on December 12, 2025, John and Debbie Rogers celebrated 50
11 wonderful years of loving marriage, and they marked the momentous occasion with a
12 celebration on December 13, 2025;
13 NOW, THEREFORE,
14 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
15 Kentucky:
16 Section 1. The Senate hereby honors and congratulates John and Debbie Rogers
17 on the momentous occasion of their 50th wedding anniversary, and wishes them many
18 more years of love and happiness in their union.
19 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
20 Resolution to Senator Robert Stivers for presentation to John and Debbie Rogers.
