UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 998
1 AN ACT relating to public holidays.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 2.110 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The first day of January (New Year's Day), the third Monday of January (Birthday
5 of Martin Luther King, Jr.),[ the nineteenth day of January (Robert E. Lee Day),]
6 the thirtieth day of January (Franklin D. Roosevelt Day), the twelfth day of
7 February (Lincoln's Birthday), the third Monday in February (Washington's
8 Birthday), the last Monday in May (Memorial Day),[ the third day of June
9 (Confederate Memorial Day, and Jefferson Davis Day),] the fourth day of July
10 (Independence Day), the first Monday in September (Labor Day), the second
11 Monday in October (Columbus Day), the eleventh day of November (Veterans
12 Day), the twenty-fifth day of December (Christmas Day) of each year, and all days
13 appointed by the President of the United States or by the Governor as days of
14 thanksgiving, are holidays[,] on which all the public offices of this Commonwealth
15 may be closed.
16 (2) No person shall be compelled to labor on the first Monday in September (Labor
17 Day) by any person.
