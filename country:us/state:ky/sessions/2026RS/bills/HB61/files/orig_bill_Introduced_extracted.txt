2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 39A IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The Kentucky Emergency Volunteer Corps (KEV Corps) is established under the
6 authority of the adjutant general and shall be composed of volunteers in each
7 county, acting locally under the direction of the local emergency management
8 director and in accordance with the provisions of this section.
9 (2) The KEV Corps shall:
10 (a) Be an unarmed volunteer unit organized under the Division of Emergency
11 Management;
12 (b) Be under the direction of the adjutant general; and
13 (c) Act locally under the local emergency management director;
14 to provide trained volunteer support during emergency situations in accordance
15 with subsection (9) of this section.
16 (3) (a) The KEV Corps shall consist of able-bodied citizens who:
17 1. Are residents of Kentucky between the ages of eighteen (18) and sixty-
18 four (64);
19 2. Are not current members of the Armed Forces of the United States,
20 National Guard, or Reserve components thereof;
21 3. Have a high school diploma or High School Equivalency Diploma;
22 4. Submit to and pass a state and national criminal history background
23 check;
24 5. Complete KEV Corps basic training or show documentation of
25 military service within the past five (5) years; and
26 6. Maintain certifications or other training standards required by the
27 adjutant general.

4 (4) Each KEV Corps volunteer shall attend and complete basic training provided at
5 the direction of the adjutant general and have continued training no less than
6 one (1) day a month and one (1) week each calendar year.
7 (5) Training standards for KEV Corps shall include:
8 (a) The United States and Kentucky Constitutions, civics, and common law
9 training;

16 (e) Emergency communications, radio, and linking to amateur or ham radio
17 communities;
18 (f) Physical training and self-defense;
19 (g) Field and survival skills; and
20 (h) Advanced training for leaders and job-specific skills as identified by the
21 adjutant general.

23 (a) Khaki colored pants;

4 (8) The Department of Military Affairs may develop a command structure to
5 coordinate any regional or statewide emergency use with the consent of the local
6 KEV Corps units or members.
7 (9) The KEV Corps may be used in response to severe weather, earthquakes,
8 declared emergencies, and at the discretion of the adjutant general or the local
9 emergency management director.
