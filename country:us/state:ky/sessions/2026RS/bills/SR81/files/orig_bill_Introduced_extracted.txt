UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2041
1 A RESOLUTION urging the Kentucky High School Athletic Association
2 (KHSAA) to recognize girls flag football as an official sport for its member schools.
3 WHEREAS, flag football is rapidly increasing in popularity, particularly in youth
4 leagues, due to the fewer number of violent collisions involved in the sport; and
5 WHEREAS, the sport has especially been embraced by young girls, many of whom
6 are aging out of youth leagues and entering the high school ranks; and
7 WHEREAS, many states across the nation have recognized this emerging trend,
8 with 17 states having already sanctioned girls flag football as an official interscholastic
9 sport; and
10 WHEREAS, an additional 21 states, including Kentucky, have unsanctioned pilot
11 programs where girls are participating in this growing sport; and
12 WHEREAS, in Kentucky, the Kentucky Girls High School Flag Football
13 Association (KGHSFFA) completed its second year of competition in November 2025,
14 growing from 12 competing teams in its first year to 24 teams in year two; and
15 WHEREAS, in the 2025 KGHSFFA championship, the Assumption High School
16 Rockets defeated the Jaguars of Mercy Academy by a score of 13 to 6 to capture their
17 second consecutive title; and
18 WHEREAS, the enthusiasm of the participants in this nascent sport was perhaps
19 best expressed by Reese Owen, an Assumption senior and former gymnast who joined
20 the flag football team, who said, "It is my favorite sport I have ever done. I've done
21 almost every sport in the books, and I would say football has been my favorite"; and
22 WHEREAS, in a recent survey conducted by the KHSAA, 88 member schools said
23 they would field a girls flag football team if the sport were sponsored by the KHSAA;
24 and
25 WHEREAS, sanctioning this sport would continue to build on the KHSAA's 60-
26 year history of expanding athletic opportunities for young women through interscholastic
27 athletics;
