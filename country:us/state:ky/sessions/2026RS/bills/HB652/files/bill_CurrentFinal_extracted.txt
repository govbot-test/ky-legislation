3 Section 1. KRS 158.4433 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) As used in this section, "school mapping data" means mapping information
5 provided in an electronic or a digital format to assist first responders in responding
6 to emergencies at schools.
7 (2) (a) The General Assembly does hereby establish the School Mapping Data
8 Program to be administered by the Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for
9 School Safety]. The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety]
10 shall utilize available funds to develop school mapping data for each public
11 school, public charter school, and any private school that requests to
12 participate in the program. The program shall be administered by the
13 Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] pursuant to this
14 section, which may include contracting for services pursuant to the relevant
15 provisions of the Kentucky Model Procurement Code in KRS Chapter 45A.
16 The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] shall be
17 responsible for ensuring that all funds received for and expended related to the
18 School Mapping Data Program are utilized for the purposes of school
19 mapping.
20 (b) The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] shall provide the
21 data developed by the program to the state school security marshal,
22 participating schools, participating districts, and local law enforcement and
23 public safety agencies for use in response to emergencies and for conducting
24 drills required under KRS 158.162(5). The school mapping data shall be
25 excluded from the application of KRS 61.870 to 61.884.
26 (c) The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] shall apply for
27 any federal grant funds that may be used to accomplish the purposes of the
