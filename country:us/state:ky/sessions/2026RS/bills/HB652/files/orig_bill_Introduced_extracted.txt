4 Section 1. KRS 158.4433 is amended to read as follows:
5 (1) As used in this section, "school mapping data" means mapping information
6 provided in an electronic or a digital format to assist first responders in responding
7 to emergencies at schools.
8 (2) (a) The General Assembly does hereby establish the School Mapping Data
9 Program to be administered by the Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for
10 School Safety]. The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety]
11 shall utilize available funds to develop school mapping data for each public
12 school, public charter school, and any private school that requests to
13 participate in the program. The program shall be administered by the
14 Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] pursuant to this
15 section, which may include contracting for services pursuant to the relevant
16 provisions of the Kentucky Model Procurement Code in KRS Chapter 45A.
17 The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] shall be
18 responsible for ensuring that all funds received for and expended related to the
19 School Mapping Data Program are utilized for the purposes of school
20 mapping.
21 (b) The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] shall provide the
22 data developed by the program to the state school security marshal,
23 participating schools, participating districts, and local law enforcement and
24 public safety agencies for use in response to emergencies and for conducting
25 drills required under KRS 158.162(5). The school mapping data shall be
26 excluded from the application of KRS 61.870 to 61.884.
27 (c) The Kentucky 911 Services Board[Center for School Safety] shall apply for
