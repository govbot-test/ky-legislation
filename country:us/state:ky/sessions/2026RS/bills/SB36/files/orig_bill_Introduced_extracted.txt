UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1558
1 AN ACT relating to parentage fraud.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 530 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) A person is guilty of parentage fraud when he or she:
6 (a) Seeks to legally establish another person as the biological parent of a child
7 in the person's custody with the intent to:
8 1. Deprive the other person of property; or
9 2. Prevent the child's actual biological parent from exercising parental
10 rights to the child; and
11 (b) Knows or reasonably should know that the other person is not the child's
12 biological parent.
13 (2) This section shall not apply when the:
14 (a) Child involved was conceived as the result of any degree of rape as
15 described in KRS Chapter 510 or incest as described in KRS 530.020;
16 (b) Child involved has been or is the process of being adopted; or
17 (c) Victim of the offense was the defendant's spouse at the time of the offense.
18 (3) Parentage fraud is a Class B misdemeanor.
