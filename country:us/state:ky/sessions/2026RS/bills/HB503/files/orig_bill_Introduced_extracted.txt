3 PART II
4 GENERAL PROVISIONS
5 1. Expenditure Authority: The Director of the Legislative Research
6 Commission, under the supervision of the Legislative Research Commission, may expend
7 any of the funds appropriated for legislative operation and administration in any lawful
8 manner and for any legal purpose consistent with the policies and practices of the
9 Commission. No executive agency or statute governing the executive agencies of state
10 government shall have the power to restrict or limit the actions of, or the expenditure of
11 funds appropriated to, the Legislative Research Commission for the Legislative Branch of
12 government.
13 2. Capitol and Capitol Annex Capital Construction Expenditures: Any
14 expenditure authorized by the Director of the Legislative Research Commission, under
15 the supervision of the Legislative Research Commission, relating to implementation of
16 KRS 56.463(4)(b), or relating to the Capitol Building, and funded by previous or current
17 appropriations to the Legislative Research Commission for the Legislative Branch of
18 government shall not be governed by KRS 7A.010, 7A.120, 45.750 to 45.810,
19 48.010(16), 48.020, and 48.110.
20 3. Severability of Budget Provisions: Appropriation items and sums in this Act
21 conform to KRS 48.311. If any section, any subsection, or any provision thereof shall be
22 invalid or unconstitutional, the decision of the courts shall not affect or impair any of the
23 remaining sections, subsections, or provisions.
24 4. Duplicate Appropriation: Any appropriation item and sum in this Act and in
25 an appropriation provision in another Act of the 2026 Regular Session of the General
26 Assembly which constitutes a duplicate appropriation shall be governed by KRS 48.312.
27 5. Priority of Individual Appropriations: KRS 48.313 shall control when a

16 PART III
17 BUDGET REDUCTION OR SURPLUS EXPENDITURE PLAN
18 The Legislative Branch shall participate in any Budget Reduction Plan or Surplus
19 Expenditure Plan in accordance with KRS Chapter 48, except that obligations essential to
20 the constitutional duties of the Legislative Branch shall be exempt from any Budget
21 Reduction Plan. The level of participation in a Budget Reduction Plan shall be at the
22 discretion of the Director and shall not exceed the actual percentage of revenue shortfall.
