8 SECTION 4. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 199 IS CREATED TO
9 READ AS FOLLOWS:
10 (1) As used in this section:
11 (a) "Child-care microcenter" means a category of regulated child care
12 supervised by the cabinet that serves between four (4) and twenty-four (24)
13 children in a nonresidential setting for more than three (3) hours per day;
14 (b) "Eligible applicant" means a provider of a child-care program that:
15 1. Has been licensed or certified by the cabinet for at least three (3) years
16 at the time of application;
17 2. Is in good standing with the cabinet; and
18 3. Participates in the quality-based graduated early care and education
19 rating system established under KRS 199.8943;
20 (c) "Nontraditional work hours" means hours of employment that generally
21 but not exclusively occur between 7 p.m. to 5 a.m. Monday to Friday, or
22 between 7 p.m. on Friday to 5 a.m. on Monday;
23 (d) "Opportunity zones" means a population census tract that is a low-income
24 community and designated as a qualified opportunity zone under Sections
25 1400Z-1 and 1400Z-2 of the Internal Revenue Code; and
26 (e) "Rural areas" means a county with a population of less than fifty thousand
27 (50,000) residents.
