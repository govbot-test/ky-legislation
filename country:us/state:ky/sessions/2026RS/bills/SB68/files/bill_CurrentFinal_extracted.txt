1 AN ACT relating to executive branch agencies.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 148.280 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The commission:
5 (a) Shall have the authority and control of such property as now is under its
6 custody and control, and of such property as may hereafter be placed under its
7 control or transferred to it by the State Property and Buildings Commission,
8 for any purpose mentioned in this section;
9 (b) May erect and repair buildings on the Kentucky Horse Park and make any and
10 all necessary or proper improvements, and generally carry on a program of
11 development and extension of facilities designed to accomplish the objectives
12 defined in this section; and
13 (c) Shall promote the progress of the state and stimulate public interest in the
14 advancement and development of the state by providing the facilities of the
15 Kentucky Horse Park for exhibitionary, competitive, and other events relative
16 to various aspects of the horse industry and other functions calculated to
17 advance and enhance the tourist industry, economy, entertainment, cultural,
18 and educational interests of the public.
19 (2) The commission may take, acquire, and hold property, and all interest herein, by
20 deed, gift, devise, bequest, lease, or by transfer from the state property so acquired
21 in the manner provided by law, and may dispose of any property so acquired in the
22 manner provided by law.
23 (3) The commission may promulgate administrative regulations in accordance with
24 KRS Chapter 13A to govern the operation, maintenance, or use of property under
25 its custody and control, as are necessary to:
26 (a) Maintain decency and good order;
27 (b) Protect the peace or safety of the general public;
