1 AN ACT relating to postsecondary education.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 164.6941 TO 164.6951 IS
4 CREATED TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) For the purposes of this section:
6 (a) "Athletics coach" or "coach" means an employee of an institution or an
7 institution's agent whose duties include the management or operation of an
8 institution's intercollegiate athletics team;
9 (b) "Council" means the Council on Postsecondary Education;
10 (c) "Intercollegiate athletics compensation" means any compensation, whether
11 through a contract or not, given to a student-athlete or athletics coach:
12 1. For the use of the name, image, or likeness of a student-athlete or
13 coach; or
14 2. To compensate the student-athlete or coach for participating in
15 intercollegiate athletics; and
16 (d) "Intercollegiate athletics enterprise" or "enterprise" means a person or
17 entity that provides student-athletes or coaches with intercollegiate athletics
18 compensation.
19 (2) (a) An intercollegiate athletics enterprise that provides a cumulative sum of
20 more than one thousand dollars ($1,000) in intercollegiate athletics
21 compensation in any calendar year to coaches and student-athletes shall
22 register with the council as an intercollegiate athletics enterprise within five
23 (5) days of meeting the amount.
24 (b) An enterprise may proactively register with the council prior to exceeding
25 the one thousand dollar ($1,000) threshold.
26 (3) Upon registration under subsection (2) of this section, the council shall issue the
27 intercollegiate athletics enterprise a certificate of registration, and the enterprise

2 with the laws of the Commonwealth, including this section, and may renew the

4 (4) An applicant for registration as an intercollegiate athletics enterprise shall
5 submit an application to the council in a form prescribed by the council and shall
6 include necessary information for the enforcement of this section.
7 (5) The council may refuse to issue a certificate of registration to an applicant that
8 has previously violated this section or engaged in conduct that significantly
9 adversely reflects on the applicant's fitness to engage in intercollegiate athletics
10 compensation.
11 (6) Each registered intercollegiate athletics enterprise shall:
12 (a) Quarterly report to the council all contracts entered into with student-
13 athletes and coaches, the institution at which the student-athlete is enrolled
14 or coach is employed, and the amounts of intercollegiate athletics
15 compensation provided to each student-athlete and coach; and
16 (b) By April 15 of each year, pay an annual registration fee to the council equal
17 to ten percent (10%) of all intercollegiate athletics compensation that the
18 enterprise provided to student-athletes and coaches in the previous year.
19 The council may establish financial penalties for failure to comply with this
20 subsection.
21 (7) The department shall deposit all fees and penalties into a restricted account
22 established under Section 2 of this Act. The moneys in the account shall be
23 divided into amounts for each postsecondary institution based on that
24 institution's portion of the intercollegiate athletics compensation to all
25 intercollegiate athletics compensation reported.

1 (1) The Council on Postsecondary Education shall establish a restricted account to
2 receive the fees and penalties paid in accordance with Section 1 of this Act. The

4 distributed to postsecondary institutions in accordance with this section.
5 (2) Each public postsecondary institution and each postsecondary institution licensed
6 by the council that participates in intercollegiate athletics as defined in KRS
7 164.6941 shall establish a scholarship program in compliance with this section
8 using the funds identified for that institution in Section 1 of this Act.
9 (3) To be eligible for an institution's scholarship, a student shall:
10 (a) Be a citizen or permanent resident of the United States;
11 (b) Be a Kentucky resident as determined by the institution in accordance with
12 criteria established by the council for the purposes of admission and tuition
13 assessment;
14 (c) Be enrolled at the institution as a student who is not a student-athlete
15 participating in intercollegiate athletics as defined in KRS 164.6941;
16 (d) Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid for the academic
17 year in which the scholarship is awarded; and
18 (e) Complete and submit a scholarship application to the institution.
19 (4) (a) The institution shall annually grant scholarships under this section by
20 lottery from eligible student applicants.
21 (b) The number of the institution's students receiving a scholarship under this
22 section shall not be fewer than the number of institution's student-athletes
23 who received intercollegiate athletics compensation during the previous
24 year under Section 1 of this Act.
25 (c) The institution shall set the amount and duration of the scholarships and
26 may also set requirements for continued eligibility for the scholarship
