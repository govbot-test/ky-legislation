UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2078
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 164 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 The Council on Postsecondary Education shall:
6 (1) Promulgate administrative regulations in accordance with KRS Chapter 13A to
7 prohibit public funds from being allocated or expended, either directly or
8 indirectly, by or on behalf of a public postsecondary institution towards a degree
9 in a low-earning outcome program as described in 20 U.S.C. sec. 1087d(c). The
10 administrative regulations shall include:
11 (a) A list of funds subject to this prohibition, including:
12 1. Individual student financial aid;
13 2. Base operational or instructional funding;
14 3. Separately or specially appropriated aid or grants that are not
15 mandated programs as defined in KRS 164.092;
16 4. Capital or facilities funding; and
17 5. Performance-based funding; and
18 (b) A list of current degrees in low-earning outcome programs at public
19 postsecondary institutions;
20 (2) Annually review updated federal determinations of low-earning outcome
21 programs as described in 20 U.S.C. sec. 1087d(c) and amend or promulgate
22 administrative regulations in accordance with subsection (1) of this section to
23 ensure the list of degrees in low-earning outcome programs remains current; and
24 (3) Submit an annual report by May 1, 2027, and each May 1 thereafter, to the
25 Legislative Research Commission for referral to the appropriate interim joint
26 committee that describes:
27 (a) The low-earning outcome programs prohibited from receiving public funds;
