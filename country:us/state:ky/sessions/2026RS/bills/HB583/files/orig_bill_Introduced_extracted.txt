1 AN ACT relating to the school-based Medicaid program.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 160 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Beginning in the 2027-2028 school year, if a school district elects to:
6 (a) Provide healthcare services through a school nurse to students, the school
7 district shall bill the school-based Medicaid program for medically
8 necessary services provided to Medicaid enrolled students for whom the
9 district has obtained parental consent to bill; and
10 (b) Contract with a third-party billing vendor to bill the school-based Medicaid
11 program for covered services, the school district shall contract with a
12 vendor that has the capacity to:
13 1. Submit Medicaid billing claims for all codes listed in the current
14 Medicaid Technical Assistance Guide; and
15 2. Bill for any code within the scope of practice for nurse practitioners.
16 (2) (a) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, school nurses shall be
17 eligible to receive preventative dental services training provided by the
18 Department for Public Health.
19 (b) School districts shall be permitted to bill for preventative dental services
20 under the school-based Medicaid program when providing preventative
21 dental services that are within the scope of practice of a school nurse to
22 Medicaid enrolled students for whom the district has obtained parental
23 consent to bill.
24 (3) This section shall not:
25 (a) Apply to a school district that contracts with its local health department for
26 school nurse services; or
27 (b) Be interpreted as requiring a school district to provide healthcare services
