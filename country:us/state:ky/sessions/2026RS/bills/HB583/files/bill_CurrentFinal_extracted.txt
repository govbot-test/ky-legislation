1 AN ACT relating to the school-based Medicaid program.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 160 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Beginning in the 2027-2028 school year, if a school district elects to:
6 (a) Provide healthcare services through a licensed or certified healthcare
7 professional, the school district shall bill the school-based Medicaid
8 program for medically necessary services provided to Medicaid enrolled
9 students for whom the district has obtained parental consent to bill; and
10 (b) Contract with a third-party billing vendor to bill the school-based Medicaid
11 program for covered services, the school district shall contract with a
12 vendor that has the capacity to submit Medicaid billing claims for all codes
13 listed in the current Medicaid Technical Assistance Guide, including all
14 codes within the scope of practice of nurse practitioners.
15 (2) (a) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, school nurses shall be
16 eligible to receive preventative oral health services training provided by the
17 Department for Public Health.
18 (b) School districts shall be permitted to bill for preventative oral health
19 services under the school-based Medicaid program when providing
20 preventative oral health services that are within the scope of practice of a
21 school nurse to Medicaid enrolled students for whom the district has
22 obtained parental consent to bill.
23 (c) Preventative oral health services provided under this subsection shall be:
24 1. Limited to services within the scope of practice for school nurses
25 established under KRS Chapter 314; and
26 2. Provided in accordance to protocols developed by the Department for
27 Public Health in consultation with the Kentucky Board of Dentistry.
