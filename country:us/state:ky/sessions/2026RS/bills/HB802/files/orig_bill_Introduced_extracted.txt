Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2457
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 100.123 is amended to read as follows:
4 At any time, the legislative bodies of the cities and counties comprising two (2) or more
5 adjacent planning units whose combined territory form a logical functional area, or
6 portion thereof, by reasons of physical, economic, or social features may enter into an
7 agreement to form a regional planning unit. The agreement for a[such] regional unit may
8 permit the continuation of any of the joint units and their planning commissions, or may
9 serve to replace them. If any joint units are to continue in operation, the agreement shall
10 state clearly the division or assignment of functions between the regional and joint units.
11 The[Such] regional units[,] shall be financed by the legislative parties in accordance with
12 the agreement.
