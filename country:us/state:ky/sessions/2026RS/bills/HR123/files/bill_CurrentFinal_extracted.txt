UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2569
1 A RESOLUTION recognizing March 19, 2026, as Random Acts of Cody Kindness
2 Day in Kentucky, in honor of Cody Reeves Gollihue.
3 WHEREAS, Cody Reeves Gollihue was born in Morehead, Kentucky, on March
4 19, 1996, to Elliott and Lea Ann Reeves-Gollihue; and
5 WHEREAS, Cody possessed a unique quality to draw people in with his magnetic
6 smile, and from an early age he was a friend to everyone; and
7 WHEREAS, Cody went out of his way to extend kindness to friends and complete
8 strangers, believing that we are all created in God's image and should always be kind to
9 one another; and
10 WHEREAS, Cody, along with his younger sister, Ellie, worked as a dynamic duo to
11 plot ways in which to toss kindness into the world, including to animals, children, and the
12 elderly; and
13 WHEREAS, Cody believed it would only take one act of kindness to create a chain
14 reaction and turn the world into a kinder place; and
15 WHEREAS, after Cody passed away on May 26, 2017, his family, friends, and
16 community rallied to create Random Acts of Cody Kindness (RACK); and
17 WHEREAS, RACK was officially launched on May 30, 2017, and has since
18 become a global kindness movement that has been shared across social media platforms;
19 and
20 WHEREAS, RACK empowers Cody's family, friends, and people across the world
21 to perform an act of kindness on or around Cody's birthday, heaven date, and other days
22 of the year as a way to honor and remember Cody's kindness; and
23 WHEREAS, RACKs have been performed in all 50 states and in 127 countries, and
24 there are more than 74,000 RACK members;
25 NOW, THEREFORE,
26 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
27 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
