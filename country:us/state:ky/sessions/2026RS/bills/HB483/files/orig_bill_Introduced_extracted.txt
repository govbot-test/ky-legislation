1 AN ACT relating to the purchase of firearms by state employees.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 45A.600 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) (a) When an agency of Kentucky state government or a public university safety
5 and security department organized pursuant to KRS 164.950 transitions from
6 the use of one (1) firearm owned by that agency to another, the agency may
7 sell the firearm that is being replaced directly to an employee of the agency if:
8 1. The firearm was issued to the employee in the course of employment;
9 and
10 2. The employee is otherwise authorized by law to own or possess the
11 firearm.
12 (b) 1. The agency shall notify employees of the intended replacement of
13 firearms and that employees may purchase firearms pursuant to
14 paragraph (a) of this subsection.
15 2. The employee desiring to purchase the firearm issued to the employee
16 shall notify the head of the issuing agency of his or her intention not less
17 than ten (10) days after receiving notice of the intended replacement.
18 (2) (a) Upon retirement from state employment, a state employee to whom a firearm
19 has been issued during the course of employment may purchase the firearm
20 which was issued during the course of employment if the employee is
21 otherwise authorized to privately own or possess the firearm.
22 (b) The employee desiring to purchase the firearm issued to the employee shall
23 notify the head of the issuing agency of such intention not less than thirty (30)
24 days prior to the scheduled date of retirement.
25 (3) (a) The purchase price of a firearm purchased under subsection (1) of this
26 section shall be the fair market value determined by the Finance and
27 Administration Cabinet, Division of Personal Property.
