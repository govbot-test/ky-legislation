UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2633
1 A RESOLUTION recognizing April 28th, 2026, as Pay It Forward Day.
2 WHEREAS, the objective of the "pay it forward" concept is to promote community
3 spirit through acts of kindness; and
4 WHEREAS, "pay it forward" is a worldwide effort supported by people in more
5 than 85 countries on six continents; and
6 WHEREAS, together, the citizens of the Commonwealth of Kentucky, its counties,
7 its cities, and the citizens of other states and countries, can make a difference by creating
8 positive change in our cities, counties, states, countries, and world one deed at a time;
9 NOW, THEREFORE,
10 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
11 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The House of Representatives hereby recognizes April 28th, 2026, as
13 Pay It Forward Day.
14 Section 2. The House of Representatives furthermore urges the citizens of the
15 Commonwealth of Kentucky to "pay it forward" when opportunity presents itself,
16 knowing that such efforts have manifold effects in promoting community spirit, creating
17 positive change, and improving the day-to-day lives of not only the citizens of this
18 Commonwealth and the United States of America, but also of the citizens of the world.
19 Section 3. The Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to transmit a
20 copy of this Resolution to Representatives Shane Baker, Josh Branscum, Josh Bray, Ken
21 Upchurch, and Speaker Pro Tempore David Meade.
