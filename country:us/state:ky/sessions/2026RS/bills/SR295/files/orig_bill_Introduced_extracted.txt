1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor and loving memory of Brenda
2 Holmes.
3 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes was born on November 30, 1954, to the late Stanley
4 and Sallie Smith; and
5 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes confessed her faith in Christ at an early age and
6 became a devoted member of Shiloh Baptist Church; and
7 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes attended Eastern Kentucky University, where she
8 pursued her passion for education and received her bachelor's degree; and
9 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes nurtured the minds of many young children
10 throughout her career, devoting 29 years of her life to teaching elementary school
11 students; and
12 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes continued to have an impact on the lives of students
13 even after retirement, volunteering her time as a teacher for Vacation Bible School and
14 the Read to Succeed program; and
15 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes also discovered a passion for photography and poetry
16 during retirement and enjoyed sharing her poetry and words of encouragement with
17 others; and
18 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes deeply loved her family and especially treasured
19 making memories with her beloved grandchildren; and
20 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes departed this life on April 4, 2026, having lived a life
21 of service to others and to God; and
22 WHEREAS, Brenda Holmes is survived by her husband of 47 years, Edward
23 Holmes; children, Margot Smith and Ryan (Georgina) Holmes; two sisters, Doris
24 (George) White and Beverly Clayborne; aunt, Virginia Smith; grandchildren, Jordynn
25 Smith, Jalynn Smith, Vivian Holmes, and Olivia Holmes; and a host of cousins, nieces,
26 nephews, and brothers- and sisters-in-law;
