3 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
4 Section 1. Are you in favor of amending the present Constitution of Kentucky
5 to add a new section enshrining and protecting the current expansion of Medicaid
6 eligibility for non-disabled, low-income adults whose income does not exceed 138% of
7 the federal poverty level and prohibiting additional burdens or restrictions on eligibility
8 or enrollment standards except as may be required under federal law?
9 SECTION 2. IT IS PROPOSED THAT A NEW SECTION BE ADDED TO
10 THE CONSTITUTION OF KENTUCKY TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
11 (1) The Commonwealth of Kentucky shall, to the extent permitted under federal
12 law, provide Medicaid benefits to any person aged nineteen to sixty-four whose income
13 is at or below one hundred thirty-eight percent of the federal poverty level for the
14 applicable family size, as authorized by federal law. Any person who qualifies under
15 this section shall receive coverage that meets or exceeds the benchmark or benchmark-
16 equivalent coverage requirements, as those terms are defined by federal law.
17 (2) The Commonwealth of Kentucky shall not impose greater or additional
18 burdens or restrictions on eligibility or enrollment standards, methodologies, or
19 practices on any person eligible under this section than those imposed on any person
20 otherwise eligible for Medicaid under Kentucky law, except that the Commonwealth of
21 Kentucky may, to the extent required by federal law or regulation, impose a work
22 requirement or cost-sharing requirement on any person eligible under this section.
23 Section 3. This amendment shall be submitted to the voters of the
24 Commonwealth for their ratification or rejection at the time and in the manner provided
25 for under Sections 256 and 257 of the Constitution, KRS 118.415, and Sections 4 and 5
26 of this Act.

10 later than the second Monday after the second Tuesday in August preceding the next
11 regular election at which members of the General Assembly are to be chosen in a year in

13 later than the Thursday after the first Tuesday in September preceding a regular election
14 in a year in which there is an election for President and Vice President of the United
15 States, the Secretary of State shall certify the complete text of Section 1 of this Act and
16 the entirety of the proposed amendment to the Constitution of Kentucky contained in
17 Section 2 of this Act to the county clerk of each county, and the county clerk shall have
18 the entirety of the text and the amendment, as so certified, indicated on the ballots
19 provided to the voters in paper or electronic form as applicable to the voting machines in
20 use in each county or precinct.
