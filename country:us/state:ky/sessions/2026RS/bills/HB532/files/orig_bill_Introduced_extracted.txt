12 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 260.850 TO 260.869 IS CREATED
13 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:

15 by the processor. Batch records shall be maintained for a minimum of three (3)
16 years and shall be made available to the department upon request.
17 (2) (a) Each batch shall undergo independent testing to determine:
18 1. Cannabinoid profile;
19 2. Compliance with delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinol concentration
20 restrictions as established in KRS 260.850 to 260.869; and
21 3. The presence or absence of:
22 a. Pesticides;
23 b. Microbial contaminants;
24 c. Residual solvents; or
25 d. Mycotoxins.
26 (b) Testing shall be performed by a lab that:
27 1. Is not owned, operated, or financially controlled by the manufacturer
