1 AN ACT relating to coordination of refugee assistance.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 194A IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "department" means the Department for Community
6 Based Services.
7 (2) The department shall administer funds received under the Refugee Act of 1980, 8
8 U.S.C. sec. 1521 et seq.
9 (3) The department shall:
10 (a) Implement a statewide strategy to ensure compliance with the Refugee Act
11 of 1980;
12 (b) Work with localities to coordinate and support local efforts that align and
13 comply with the Refugee Act of 1980;
14 (c) Serve as the primary liaison between immigrant-serving and refugee-
15 serving organizations and businesses and the federal government;
16 (d) Advise the Governor, appropriate state agencies, cabinet members, and the
17 General Assembly on ways to improve state policies with respect to
18 administering funds pursuant to the Refugee Act of 1980;
19 (e) Make recommendations to the Governor on whether to accept and where to
20 settle refugees based on available housing; and
21 (f) Submit an annual report to the Kentucky Office of Homeland Security, and
22 to the Legislative Research Commission for referral to the Interim Joint
23 Committees on Appropriations and Revenue, State Government, Health
24 Services, and Families and Children, that includes:
25 1. The total number of refugees settled in the Commonwealth and the
26 number of refugees placed in each county. The department shall also
27 include any recommendations made to the Governor with respect to
