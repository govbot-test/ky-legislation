UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2500
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION designating the Billy G. Jones Memorial Bridge in Bath
2 County.
3 WHEREAS, Billy Gene Jones was born on January 25, 1947, to the late William
4 Hobert Jones and Dorothy McKenzie Jones; and
5 WHEREAS, Billy Gene Jones was a successful contractor, developer, and
6 businessman; and
7 WHEREAS, Billy Gene Jones, along with his brother Rudy, was a founder, owner,
8 and operator of Triple J Construction, Inc; specializing in highway construction and site
9 development; and
10 WHEREAS, Billy Gene Jones dream of building a golf course came true when his
11 company was awarded a road and bridge project on Kentucky Route 36 and they bought
12 the adjoining property and built the front nine of the Carrington Golf Course in Bath
13 County; and
14 WHEREAS, Carrington Golf Course later expanded to 18 holes and thousands of
15 people have played golf at "The Rock"; and
16 WHEREAS, Billy Gene Jones and his brother Rudy later developed Rudy's Ranch
17 and Horse Camp, which has been visited by trail riders and campers from all over North
18 America; and
19 WHEREAS, in his spare time, Billy Gene Jones enjoyed spending time with his
20 family and friends, playing golf, fishing, going to Cave Run Lake, watching the
21 University of Kentucky sports teams, and attending high school athletic events; and
22 WHEREAS, most of all, Billy Gene Jones loved spending time with his grandson
23 Will, teaching him the game of golf and watching him play tournaments and basketball
24 games, never missing an event;
25 WHEREAS, Billy Gene Jones left these earthly bounds on August 4, 2021, and it is
26 fitting and proper that his contributions to his community should be tangibly recognized;
27 NOW, THEREFORE,
