UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1085
1 AN ACT relating to poultry.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 217.005 TO 217.215 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) For the purposes of this section, "end consumer" means a person who is the last
6 person to purchase any food product and who does not resell the food product.
7 (2) Notwithstanding any other provisions of law to the contrary, the secretary shall
8 promulgate administrative regulations in accordance with KRS Chapter 13A to:
9 (a) Exclude slaughtering and processing of poultry on a farm for the purpose
10 of conducting sales under 9 C.F.R. sec. 381.10 if the slaughtered and
11 processed poultry or poultry products are sold to an end consumer on a
12 farm, at a farmers market, or at a roadside stand;
13 (b) Require that poultry or poultry products processed under this section that
14 are sold on a farm be refrigerated at the point of sale and labeled in
15 compliance with the requirements of 9 C.F.R. sec. 381.10; and
16 (c) Require that poultry or poultry products processed under this section that
17 are sold at a farmers market or at a roadside stand be frozen at the point of
18 sale and labeled in compliance with the requirements of 9 C.F.R. sec.
19 381.10.
20 (3) Notwithstanding any other provisions of law to the contrary, a local
21 governmental agency shall not require any additional certification or inspection
22 of poultry or poultry products of a farmer acting in compliance with the
23 administrative regulations promulgated pursuant to this section.
