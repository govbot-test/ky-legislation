1 AN ACT relating to moral instruction.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 158.200 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The boards of education of local school districts shall allow[may provide an
5 opportunity for] pupils to attend moral instruction in their jurisdiction, in the
6 manner provided in this section.
7 (2) (a) Local boards of education shall allow pupils to be excused for at least[up to]
8 one (1) hour [on no more than one (1) day ]each week, which may[shall]
9 include time attributed to travel to and from, to attend a [district-approved
10 request for a ]moral instruction offering that complies with the requirements
11 established under subsection (3) of this section and upon receiving the
12 consent of the pupil's parent or guardian.
13 (b) Moral instruction provided under this section shall not take place on school
14 property, unless permitted under a local school board policy authorizing
15 equal access to the use of school property by public members of the
16 community.
17 (c) Pupil participation in moral instruction shall be voluntary and free from
18 coercion by school personnel.
19 (d) Moral instruction shall be given without expense to any local board of
20 education above de minimis administrative expenses incurred in carrying out
21 this section.
22 (3) An individual, organization, entity, or any combination thereof seeking to provide a
23 moral instruction offering for pupils shall submit a written, signed plan[request] to
24 the local board of education of a school district regarding the proposed moral
25 instruction offering. The plan[request] shall include:
26 (a) Contact information for the primary individual who will be providing the
27 moral instruction, as well as all other individuals who may be transporting
