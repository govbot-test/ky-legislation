1 AN ACT relating to offender reentry.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 186.417 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) (a) The Transportation Cabinet shall issue to any felony offender, if the felony
5 offender is eligible, released from:
6 1. The Kentucky Department of Corrections;[,]
7 2. A county jail or other local or regional correctional facility;[, if and
8 when funds are available,] or
9 3. A Federal Bureau of Prisons facility located in Kentucky;
10 on home incarceration, parole, completed service of sentence, shock
11 probation, or pardon, a personal identification card or, if the felony offender is
12 eligible, an operator's license.
13 (b) An offender who wishes to obtain a personal identification card or operator's
14 license shall provide proper documentation to comply with the provisions of
15 this section.
16 (2) Proper documentation under subsection (1) of this section shall consist of:
17 (a) The offender's certificate of birth;
18 (b) A copy of the offender's resident record card and parole certificate or notice of
19 discharge;
20 (c) A photograph of the offender, printed on plastic card or paper; and
21 (d) A release letter that shall contain the offender's:
22 1. Full legal name, subject to the information available to the Kentucky
23 Department of Corrections or a Federal Bureau of Prisons facility
24 located in Kentucky;
25 2. Discharge or [/]release date;
26 3. Signature;
27 4. Social Security number;

4 (3) The Transportation Cabinet shall issue to any felony offender, if the felony offender
5 is eligible, probated, or conditionally discharged by the court and under the

16 offender's status on supervision, that shall contain the offender's:
17 1. Full legal name, subject to the information available to the Division of

19 2. Signature;
20 3. Social Security number;
21 4. Date of birth;
22 5. Present Kentucky address where he or she resides; and
23 6. Physical description.

27 identification card or operator's license pursuant to KRS 186.531.

1 (6) The Transportation Cabinet shall promulgate administrative regulations in
2 accordance with KRS Chapter 13A to establish procedures for current inmates in
3 state and federal prisons, who are deemed eligible by prison officials, to be issued
4 operator's licenses to engage in work release activities or reentry initiatives. The
5 administrative regulations shall address, at a minimum:
6 (a) The information required for application, which shall include all information
7 in paragraph (b) of this subsection that[which] is germane to a current inmate.
8 For purposes of this paragraph, the facility in which the inmate is housed shall
9 be considered the inmate's residence;
10 (b) Required documentation from the Department of Corrections or the Federal
11 Bureau of Prisons that the inmate meets the security criteria to be eligible for
12 work outside of the facility;
13 (c) Procedures for license issuance; and
14 (d) Restrictions on use of the license, including a requirement that the inmate
15 shall surrender the license to prison officials when the inmate is not engaged
16 in work outside the facility.
17 (7) The cabinet shall process applications for operator's licenses and personal
18 identification cards under this section in the same manner as in KRS 186.412 and
19 186.4122.
20 (8) The Transportation Cabinet may enter into an agreement with the Kentucky
21 Department of Corrections, the United States Probation Office, or the Federal
22 Bureau of Prisons to use a mobile unit to begin the issuance process in this section.
