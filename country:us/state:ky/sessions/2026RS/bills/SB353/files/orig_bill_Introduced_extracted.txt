UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2390
1 AN ACT relating to labor.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 337.055 is amended to read as follows:
4 Any employee who leaves or is discharged from his or her employment shall be paid in
5 full all wages or salary earned by him or her; not later than the next normal pay period
6 following the date of dismissal or voluntary leaving or fourteen (14) days following such
7 date of dismissal or voluntary leaving whichever last occurs. Any employee who is
8 absent at the time fixed for payment by an employer, or who, for any other reason, is not
9 paid at that time, shall be paid thereafter at any time or upon fourteen (14) days' demand.
10 No employer shall, by any means, secure exemption from this section.
