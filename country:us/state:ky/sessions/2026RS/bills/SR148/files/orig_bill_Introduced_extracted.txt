2 WHEREAS, the National Opinion Research Center and the Wall Street Journal
3 found in an opinion poll that American support for traditional values, such as faith,
4 family, and patriotism are declining; and
5 WHEREAS, the principles of faith, family, and patriotism have long been seen as
6 crucial for the health of American society; and
7 WHEREAS, children who are raised in a religious or spiritual environment are
8 better protected from illicit drugs and promiscuity; and
9 WHEREAS, children with a religious upbringing experienced greater happiness,
10 increased community involvement, a greater sense of mission and purpose, and higher
11 levels of forgiveness; and
12 WHEREAS, an abundance of research shows that children who grow up in married,
13 two-parent families are more likely to be employed, graduate from college, and are less
14 likely to be depressed, be sentenced for criminal activity, end up poor, or bear children in
15 their teens; and
16 WHEREAS, George Washington stated that, "Of all dispositions and habits which
17 lead to political prosperity, religion, and morality are indispensable supports"; and
18 WHEREAS, the cultivation of such virtues as fidelity to God, family, community,
19 and country leads to human flourishing and a healthy society;
20 NOW, THEREFORE,
21 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
22 Kentucky:
23 Section 1. The members of the Senate hereby recognize the month of June 2026
24 as Fidelity Month, for the purpose of rededicating Kentucky to the civic virtues of faith,
25 family, and patriotism.
26 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
27 Resolution to Senator Aaron Reed.

UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2339
1 A RESOLUTION recognizing June 2026 as Fidelity Month.
2 WHEREAS, the National Opinion Research Center and the Wall Street Journal
3 found in an opinion poll that American support for traditional values, such as faith,
4 family, and patriotism are declining; and
5 WHEREAS, the principles of faith, family, and patriotism have long been seen as
6 crucial for the health of American society; and
7 WHEREAS, children who are raised in a religious or spiritual environment are
8 better protected from illicit drugs and promiscuity; and
9 WHEREAS, children with a religious upbringing experienced greater happiness,
10 increased community involvement, a greater sense of mission and purpose, and higher
11 levels of forgiveness; and
12 WHEREAS, an abundance of research shows that children who grow up in married,
13 two-parent families are more likely to be employed, graduate from college, and are less
14 likely to be depressed, be sentenced for criminal activity, end up poor, or bear children in
15 their teens; and
16 WHEREAS, George Washington stated that, "Of all dispositions and habits which
17 lead to political prosperity, religion, and morality are indispensable supports"; and
18 WHEREAS, the cultivation of such virtues as fidelity to God, family, community,
19 and country leads to human flourishing and a healthy society;
20 NOW, THEREFORE,
21 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
22 Kentucky:
23 Section 1. The members of the Senate hereby recognize the month of June 2026
24 as Fidelity Month, for the purpose of rededicating Kentucky to the civic virtues of faith,
25 family, and patriotism.
26 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
27 Resolution to Senator Aaron Reed.
