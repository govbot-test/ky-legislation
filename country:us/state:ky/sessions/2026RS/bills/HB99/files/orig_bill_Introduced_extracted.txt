1 AN ACT relating to independent school districts.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 160 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 As used in Sections 1 to 6 of this Act:
6 (1) "Altered originating school district" or "altered originating district" means the
7 originating school district as it exists after a new independent school district is
8 removed from its boundaries and that shall continue as the same legal entity as
9 the originating school district;
10 (2) "Interim school board" or "interim board" means the local board of education
11 of the new independent school district that is elected under to Section 2 of this Act
12 or any successor board elected, either of which are serving prior to the deadline
13 to be fully operational established in subsection (4)(h) of Section 4 of this Act;
14 (3) "New independent school district" or "new independent district" means an
15 independent school district that is created under Section 2 of this Act;
16 (4) "Originating school district" or "originating district" means a school district
17 from which a new independent school district and an altered originating school
18 district is created under Section 2 of this Act; and
19 (5) "Qualifying city" means an incorporated city:
20 (a) That has no independent school district currently in existence within the
21 city's boundaries;
22 (b) With boundaries that do not contain the entirety of the county in which the
23 city is located; and
24 (c) That has a population based on the most recent decennial census conducted
25 by the United States Bureau of the Census of at least five thousand (5,000)
26 people.

9 READ AS FOLLOWS:
10 (1) Upon the deadline to become fully functional in subsection (4)(h) of Section 4 of
11 this Act, the new independent district shall be prepared to provide services to the
12 students of the district and the interim board shall no longer serve as an interim
13 but shall exercise the full authorities of a local board of education, except for the
14 provisions of this section and Section 6 of this Act.

17 paying a specific debt or interest shall continue to be levied and collected within
18 the boundaries of the new independent district by the new independent district's
19 board in accordance with the laws under which the levies were originally made
20 until all the associated obligations of the originating district have been retired.
21 (3) Notwithstanding the requirements under KRS 160.345, the new independent
22 district shall not be required to implement school-based decision making in the
23 district for the first fully operational school year, but shall adopt a process to seek
24 the feedback of parents and school faculty during that year. The district shall be
25 subject to KRS 160.345 with the start of the second fully operational year.
