UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1869
1 AN ACT relating to certificate of need.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 216B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 On or after the effective date of this Act, notwithstanding any provision of this chapter
6 or any other law to the contrary, a certificate of need issued by the Cabinet for Health
7 and Family Services or any other state agency shall not be required to establish or
8 operate a health facility or provide health services and all existing certificates of need
9 shall be deemed null and void.
