Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 335
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 365 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Prohibited advertisement" means any written or electronic communication
7 by a contractor that encourages, instructs, or induces a residential property
8 owner to contact any insurance company personnel for the purpose of
9 making an insurance claim for roof damage. This includes but is not limited
10 to a door hanger, business card, magnet, flyer, pamphlet, and email; and
11 (b) "Solicit" means to contact a residential property owner in person or
12 through electronic means, including but not limited to email or telephone.
13 (2) A person shall not:
14 (a) Solicit a residential property owner by means of a prohibited advertisement;
15 (b) Offer a residential property owner a rebate, gift card, cash, coupon, waiver
16 of any insurance deductible, or any other thing of value in exchange for:
17 1. Allowing the person or his or her agent to conduct an inspection of the
18 residential property owner's roof; or
19 2. Making an insurance claim for damage to the residential property
20 owner's roof in a way that purports to interpret an insurance policy;
21 (c) Offer, deliver, receive, or accept any compensation, inducement, or reward
22 for the referral of any work to a business or person that performs repair
23 work services on residential property for which insurance proceeds are
24 payable; or
25 (d) Provide an insured with an agreement authorizing roof repairs without
26 providing a good-faith estimate of the itemized and detailed cost of services
27 and materials for repairs undertaken pursuant to a property insurance
