4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 61 IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) As used in this section:
7 (a) "Jail Enforcement Model" means the United States Immigration and
8 Customs Enforcement program that authorizes state and local law
9 enforcement officers to identify and process removable aliens who are in a
10 jail or detention facility and have pending or active criminal charges;
11 (b) "Task Force Model" means the United States Immigration and Customs
12 Enforcement program that authorizes state and local law enforcement
13 officers to enforce limited immigration authority while performing routine
14 police duties; and
15 (c) "Warrant Service Officer Model" means the United States Immigration and
16 Customs Enforcement program that authorizes state and local law
17 enforcement officers to serve and execute administrative warrants on
18 removable aliens who are in custody.
19 (2) Each local law enforcement agency and the Department of Kentucky State Police
20 shall enter into written agreements with the United States Immigration and
21 Customs Enforcement to participate in the Jail Enforcement Model, Task Force
22 Model, and Warrant Service Officer Model programs established under Section
23 287(g) of the Immigration and Nationality Act, 8 U.S.C. sec. 1357.
