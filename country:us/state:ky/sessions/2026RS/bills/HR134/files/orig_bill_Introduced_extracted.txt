2 Appreciation Day.
3 WHEREAS, the men and women who serve as utility lineworkers build and
4 maintain electric power, telephone, cable television, and fiber optic lines; and
5 WHEREAS, lineworkers are often first responders during storms and other
6 catastrophic events, working to make the scene safe for other public safety heroes; and
7 WHEREAS, to keep electricity flowing, lineworkers work with thousands of volts

20 and Kentucky Utilities Company, on the occasion of National Lineworker Appreciation
21 Day, April 18, 2026.
22 Section 2. When the House of Representatives adjourns this day, it does so in
23 honor, recognition, and celebration of Kentucky's utility lineworkers.

26 parties.
