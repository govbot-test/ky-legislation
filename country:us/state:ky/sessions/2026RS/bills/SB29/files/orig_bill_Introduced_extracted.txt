Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1427
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 109 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a solid waste management
6 facility that is collecting, storing, transporting, transferring, processing, treating, or
7 disposing of solid waste that was generated outside of the county or waste management
8 district where the solid waste management facility is located shall not be subject to any
9 assessment, fee, permitting requirement, or other authorization requirement imposed
10 by the county or waste management district where the solid waste was generated.
