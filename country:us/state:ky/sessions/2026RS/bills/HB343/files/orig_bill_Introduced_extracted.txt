4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Contribution" means the amount contributed to a Kentucky Educational
7 Savings Plan Trust account or STABLE Kentucky account on behalf of an
8 employee;
9 (b) "Kentucky Educational Savings Plan Trust account" means the trust
10 established pursuant to KRS 164A.310, to which contributions are made
11 and withdrawn upon by a beneficiary for qualified educational expenses;
12 and

14 KRS 164A.260 to which contributions are made and withdrawn upon by a
15 beneficiary for qualified expenses.

17 Educational Savings Plan Trust account and STABLE Kentucky account
18 contribution credit shall be available for Kentucky employers making a
19 contribution to a Kentucky Educational Savings Plan Trust account or a
20 STABLE Kentucky account on behalf of one (1) or more of their employees.
21 (b) The credit:
22 1. Shall be nonrefundable and nontransferable;
23 2. May be carried forward for a period that does not exceed five (5)
24 taxable years, if unused or not used in its entirety in the current
25 taxable year;
26 3. May be claimed against the tax imposed under KRS 141.020 or
27 141.040 and 141.0401. The ordering of the credits shall be as provided
