1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor and loving memory of Mac
2 Meadows.
3 WHEREAS, Mac Meadows was born in Paducah, Kentucky, on January 22, 1937,
4 to the late Harvey Meadows and Bertha Mae (Solomon) Meadows; and
5 WHEREAS, Mac Meadows was a member and former Deacon at Benton Church of
6 Christ and a veteran of the United States Air Force; and
7 WHEREAS, Mac Meadows retired from Westlake Corporation where he worked as
8 a chemical operator; and

10 Cardinals, University of Kentucky Wildcats, and Marshall County High School
11 basketball; and
12 WHEREAS, Mac Meadows passed away on Tuesday, April 14, 2026, at his home
13 in Benton, Kentucky; and
14 WHEREAS, Mac Meadows is survived by his wife, Dionna Jean (Owens)
15 Meadows; son, Barry (Kathy) Meadows; daughter, Jeanne (Ronnie) Lovett; sister,

17 Bethany (Shaun) Hayden, Travis (Becky) Meadows, Taylor Meadows, Maddie (Alex)

19 MacKenna Lovett, Hillton Lovett, Riker Keesee, Beau Keesee, River Meadows, Remi
20 Meadows, and Cora Dunn;
21 NOW, THEREFORE,
22 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
23 Kentucky:
24 Section 1. The Senate hereby joins with Senator Danny Carroll in expressing its
