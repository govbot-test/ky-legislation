1 AN ACT relating to total net income limits on loans secured by a mortgage.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 286.8-125 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) (a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this subsection, it shall be unlawful
5 for any licensee or person holding a claim of exemption to originate a loan
6 secured by a mortgage on residential real property in Kentucky if the total net
7 income generated by the licensee or person exceeds the greater of the
8 following:
9 1. Two thousand dollars ($2,000); or
10 2. Four percent (4%) of the total loan amount[, whichever is greater].
11 (b) Paragraph (a) of this subsection shall not apply to a loan for which the total
12 points and fees on the loan do not exceed the threshold set forth in 12
13 C.F.R. sec. 1026.43(e)(3), as amended.
14 (2) As used in this section, unless the context requires otherwise:
15 (a) "Total loan amount" means the amount financed in the mortgage loan less
16 the total net income generated by the:
17 1. Originating licensee or person; or
18 2. Affiliate of the originating licensee or person; and
19 (b) "Total net income":
20 1. Means any and all fees, income, or compensation of any kind collected,
21 received, or charged by:
22 a. The licensee or person holding a claim of exemption;[,] or[ by ]
23 b. An affiliate of the licensee or person holding a claim of
24 exemption;[. "Total net income" ]
25 2. Includes but is not limited to origination fees, broker fees, lender fees
26 and discount points if retained by the originating licensee or person as
27 income, processing fees, administrative fees, document preparation fees,
