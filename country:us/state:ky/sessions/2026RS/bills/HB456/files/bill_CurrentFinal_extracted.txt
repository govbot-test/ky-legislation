UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS HB 456/EN
1 AN ACT relating to the State Treasurer and unclaimed property.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 2 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 The fourth week of September of each year is designated as Unclaimed Property Week.
6 Section 2. KRS 41.020 is amended to read as follows:
7 The Department of the Treasury shall be headed by the State Treasurer. He or she shall[
8 reside and] keep his or her office at the seat of government.
9 Section 3. KRS 393A.740 is amended to read as follows:
10 (1) If a holder enters into a contract or other arrangement for the purpose of evading an
11 obligation under this chapter or otherwise willfully fails to perform a duty imposed
12 on the holder under this chapter, the administrator may require the holder to pay the
13 administrator, in addition to interest as provided in KRS 393A.730, a civil penalty
14 of one thousand dollars ($1,000) for each day the obligation is evaded or the duty is
15 not performed, up to a cumulative maximum amount of twenty-five thousand
16 dollars ($25,000), plus twenty-five percent (25%) of the amount or value of
17 property that should have been but was not reported, paid, or delivered as a result of
18 the evasion or failure to perform.
19 (2) If a holder makes a fraudulent report or files a report which fails to provide all
20 necessary information in their possession under this chapter, the administrator
21 may require the holder to pay to the administrator, in addition to interest under KRS
22 393A.730, a civil penalty of one thousand dollars ($1,000) for each day from the
23 date the report was made until corrected, up to a cumulative maximum of twenty-
24 five thousand dollars ($25,000), plus twenty-five percent (25%) of the amount or
25 value of any property that should have been reported but was not included in the
26 report or was underreported.
