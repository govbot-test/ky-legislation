7 Section 2. KRS 273.207 is amended to read as follows:
8 (1) The affairs of a corporation shall be managed by a board of directors. Except as
9 provided in subsection (2) of this section, directors need not be residents of this
10 state or members of the corporation unless the articles of incorporation or the
11 bylaws so require. The articles of incorporation or the bylaws may prescribe other
12 qualifications for directors.
13 (2) The members of the board of directors of a fire department organized under this
14 chapter shall be residents of the geographic area served by the corporation. A
15 vacancy shall be declared to exist if a member no longer resides within the
16 geographic area served by the corporation at any time during his or her term of
17 office.
18 Section 3. On the effective date of this Act:
19 (1) A vacancy shall be declared to exist in the office of any board of trustee
20 member serving under Section 1 of this Act who does not qualify under subsection
21 (1)(a)2. of Section 1 of this Act, and the vacancy shall be filled in accordance with
22 Section 1 of this Act; and
23 (2) A vacancy shall be declared to exist in the office of any board of director
24 member serving under Section 2 of this Act who does not personally reside in the
25 geographic area served by the corporation, and the vacancy shall be filled in accordance
26 with KRS 273.213.
