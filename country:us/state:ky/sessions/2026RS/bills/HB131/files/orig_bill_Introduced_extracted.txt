4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 141 IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) As used in this section:
7 (a) "Contribution" means the amount contributed to a Kentucky Educational
8 Savings Plan Trust account to be used by a beneficiary as required by 26
9 U.S.C. sec. 529;
10 (b) "Kentucky Educational Savings Plan Trust account" or "savings plan trust
11 account" means the trust established pursuant to KRS 164A.310, to which
12 contributions are made and withdrawn by a beneficiary for qualified
13 educational expenses; and
14 (c) "Qualified educational expenses" has the same meaning as in KRS
15 164A.305.
16 (2) (a) For taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2027, but before
17 January 1, 2031, the Kentucky Educational Savings Plan Trust account
18 contribution credit shall be available for resident taxpayers making
19 contributions to a Kentucky Educational Savings Plan Trust account.
20 (b) The credit shall be nonrefundable, nontransferable, and allowed against the
21 tax imposed under KRS 141.020 with the ordering of the credit as provided
22 in Section 2 of this Act.
23 (c) The credit shall:
24 1. Be equal to the total contributions made to a savings plan trust
25 account for the taxable year; but
26 2. Not exceed nineteen thousand dollars ($19,000), per taxpayer, per
27 taxable year.
