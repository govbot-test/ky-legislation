UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 43
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 150.725 TO 150.735 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, in the event of a natural
6 disaster or declared emergency, as defined in KRS 39A.020, the Department of
7 Agriculture shall have sole authority to administer and enforce the permitting, holding,
8 and fencing requirements of captive cervids.
