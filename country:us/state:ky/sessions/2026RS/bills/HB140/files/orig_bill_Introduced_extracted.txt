Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 953
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 189 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Six (6) months after the completion of each highway pavement marking project
6 or other highway paint application, the Transportation Cabinet shall conduct a
7 quality assurance check. The quality assurance check shall:
8 (a) Include an inspection of the highway pavement markings for adherence to
9 the Federal Highway Administration regulations and standards; and
10 (b) Be conducted after dark to inspect the level of retroreflectivity.
11 (2) If a highway pavement marking project fails the required quality assurance
12 check, the Transportation Cabinet shall repair or reapply the highway pavement
13 markings within thirty (30) days and conduct a quality assurance in accordance
14 with subsection (1) of this section.
