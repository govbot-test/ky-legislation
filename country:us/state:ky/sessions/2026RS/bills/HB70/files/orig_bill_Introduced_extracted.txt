2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 186.4102 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) Except as provided in subsection (10)[(9)] of this section, the Transportation
5 Cabinet shall issue operator's licenses and personal identification cards bearing a
6 photograph of the applicant and other information the cabinet may deem
7 appropriate to qualified applicants under this chapter. When taking the photograph,
8 the applicant shall be prohibited from wearing sunglasses, veils, scarves, or any
9 other attire that obscures or creates shadows upon any features of the applicant's
10 face as determined by the cabinet. An applicant shall be required to remove
11 eyewear that obstructs the iris or the pupil of the eyes and shall not take any action
12 to obstruct a photograph of his or her facial features. The face shall be visible from
13 the crown to the base of the chin and from ear to ear. Any person who refuses to
14 remove attire prohibited by this subsection as directed by the person taking the
15 application shall be prohibited from receiving an operator's license or personal
16 identification card.
17 (2) An operator's license or personal identification card issued by the cabinet shall not
18 contain the applicant's Social Security number. The cabinet shall promulgate
19 administrative regulations in accordance with KRS Chapter 13A that develop a
20 numbering system that uses an identification system other than Social Security
21 numbers. If an applicant submits adequate proof that he or she does not have a
22 Social Security number, the Transportation Cabinet shall assign the applicant a
23 unique identifying number.
24 (3) The license or personal identification card shall also designate by color coding and
25 using the phrase "under 21" if the licensee or card holder is under the age of twenty-
26 one (21); "CDL" if the license is issued pursuant to KRS Chapter 281A; or "under
27 21 CDL" if the licensee holds a commercial driver's license issued pursuant to KRS
