1 AN ACT relating to workforce investment.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 151B.017 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The secretary of the Education and Labor Cabinet shall be appointed by the
5 Governor pursuant to KRS 12.255 and shall serve at the pleasure of the Governor.
6 The secretary shall have general supervision and direction over all activities and
7 functions of the cabinet and its employees shall be responsible for carrying out the
8 programs and policies of the cabinet. The secretary shall be the chief executive
9 officer of the cabinet and shall have authority to enter into contracts, subject to the
10 approval of the secretary of the Finance and Administration Cabinet, when the
11 contracts are deemed necessary to implement and carry out the programs of the
12 cabinet. The secretary shall have the authority to require coordination and
13 nonduplication of services provided under the federal Workforce Investment Act, as
14 amended. The secretary shall have the authority to mandate fiscal responsibility and
15 dispute resolution procedures among state organizational units for services provided
16 under the federal Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act.
17 (2) (a) The secretary of the Education and Labor Cabinet, and the secretary's
18 designated representatives, in the discharge of the duties of the secretary may:
19 1. Administer oaths and affirmations, take depositions, certify official acts,
20 and issue subpoenas to compel the attendance of witnesses and parties
21 and the production of books, papers, correspondence, memoranda, and
22 other records considered necessary and relevant to the matter under
23 investigation;
24 2. Administer oaths;
25 3. Examine witnesses under oath;
26 4. Take verification of proof or written instruments; and
27 5. Take testimony, depositions, and affidavits to carry out any law over

21 responsibilities, power, and authority relating to labor, wages and hours,

23 and all other matters pertaining to Kentucky labor laws and the state's regulation of
24 labor in the Commonwealth.
