2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 158.060 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) Each teacher shall be provided access to a copy of his or her employment contract
5 upon request.
6 (2) Twenty (20) school days, or days in which teachers are actually employed in the
7 schoolroom, shall constitute a school month in the common schools.
8 (3) (a) Each full-time teacher shall be provided the equivalent of two hundred fifty
9 (250) minutes of noninstructional planning time each five (5) day school
10 week, which shall be used at the teacher's discretion and may include but is
11 not limited to grading, instructional planning, curriculum development, and
12 outreach activities involving the teacher's students. If a school week does
13 not contain five (5) instructional days, the required minimum number of
14 minutes may be reduced proportionally.
15 (b) Each full-time teacher shall be provided with a duty-free lunch period each
16 day during the regularly scheduled student lunch period in addition to the
17 noninstructional planning time described in paragraph (a) of this
18 subsection. The duty-free lunch period shall be not less than the length of the
19 lunch period specified in the school calendar approved by the chief state
20 school officer.
21 (c) Each full-time teacher shall receive additional compensation of at least his
22 or her usual hourly rate of pay for all duties assigned during the
23 noninstructional planning time required by paragraph (a) of this subsection
24 or duty-free lunch required by paragraph (b) of this subsection. A full-time
25 teacher may be assigned duties during any[to lunch room duty during the
26 regularly scheduled student lunch period only for an amount of time equal to
27 the] noninstructional time in excess of the minimum time required by

15 hours beyond the teacher's usual hourly rate of pay; or

17 employer-employee bargained contract.
18 (7) Character education programs and activities shall be considered valuable and
19 legitimate components of the actual school work constituting a school day under
20 subsection (4) of this section.
