UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2574
1 A RESOLUTION recognizing April 18, 2026, as National Lineworker
2 Appreciation Day.
3 WHEREAS, the men and women who serve as utility lineworkers build and
4 maintain electric power, telephone, cable television, and fiber optic lines; and
5 WHEREAS, lineworkers are often first responders during storms and other
6 catastrophic events, working to make the scene safe for other public safety heroes; and
7 WHEREAS, to keep electricity flowing, lineworkers work with thousands of volts
8 of electricity high atop powerlines 24 hours a day, 365 days a year; and
9 WHEREAS, lineworkers must often work under dangerous conditions far from
10 their families to construct and maintain the energy infrastructure of the Commonwealth;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, lineworkers put their lives on the line every day with little recognition
13 from the community regarding the danger of their work;
14 NOW, THEREFORE,
15 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
16 Kentucky:
17 Section 1. The members of the Senate hereby express, on behalf of their
18 constituents, their sincere appreciation for the important work of the lineworkers of Duke
19 Energy, Kentucky Power Company, and Louisville Gas and Electric and Kentucky
20 Utilities Company, on the occasion of National Lineworker Appreciation Day, April 18,
21 2026.
22 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor, recognition,
23 and celebration of Kentucky's utility lineworkers.
24 Section 3. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
25 Resolution to Senator Amanda Mays Bledsoe for delivery to the appropriate parties.
