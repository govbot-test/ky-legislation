UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1539
1 AN ACT relating to calorie information.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 217.280 TO 217.390 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 A food service establishment required under 21 C.F.R. sec. 101.11 to provide calorie
6 information for standard menu items shall display in a prominent place easily seen by
7 customers a printed sign at least eight and one-half (8-1/2) by eleven (11) inches in size
8 stating that the food service establishment's menu is also available without calorie
9 information:
10 (1) Upon the request of a customer; or
11 (2) On the website of the food service establishment.
