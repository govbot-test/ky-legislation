3 Section 1. KRS 258.005 is amended to read as follows:
4 As used in KRS 258.005 to 258.087, unless the context requires otherwise:

8 Kentucky;

10 practitioner of veterinary medicine];

12 family services to vaccinate his or her own dog against rabies;

16 and family services;

18 United States Department of Agriculture approved rabies vaccine;

11 Section 2. KRS 258.015 is amended to read as follows:
12 (1) Every owner shall have his or her dog, cat, or ferret initially vaccinated against
13 rabies by the age of four (4) months and revaccinated at the expiration of the
14 immunization period as certified by a Kentucky-licensed[the] veterinarian. The
15 veterinarian, or a Kentucky-licensed veterinary technician in accordance with
16 KRS 321.190, who vaccinates a dog, cat, or ferret shall issue to the owner a
17 vaccination certificate signed by the veterinarian on a form approved by the
18 Cabinet for Health and Family Services. The vaccination certificate shall be
19 prepared and issued in duplicate, one (1) copy to be retained by the issuing
20 veterinarian and one (1) copy to be given to the owner of the dog, cat, or ferret
21 vaccinated. Each certificate shall bear the name and address of the veterinarian who
22 issued it. The veterinarian shall also furnish each dog owner with a rabies tag
23 bearing a serial number corresponding to the vaccination certificate with the year of
24 immunization. The tag shall be affixed to a collar or harness furnished by the owner
25 and shall be worn by the dog for which the tag was issued. No one except the owner
26 or his or her duly authorized agent shall remove the tag.
