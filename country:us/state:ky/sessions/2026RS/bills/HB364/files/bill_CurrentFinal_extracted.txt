1 AN ACT relating to benefits for Kentucky National Guard members.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 38.030 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) As used in this section:
5 (a) "Commonwealth emergency response pay" means a monetary incentive
6 paid to Kentucky National Guard members when the Kentucky National
7 Guard is called to hazardous duty assignments while on state active duty
8 service during a state of emergency as declared by the Governor; and
9 (b) "Hazardous duty" means work performed in conditions under which a
10 service member has an increased risk of serious injury, illness, or death.
11 (2) Only the Governor shall have the authority to order units and members of the
12 Kentucky National Guard into state active duty.
13 (a) The Governor may order units and individual members of the Kentucky
14 National Guard into state active duty for any of the following purposes:
15 1. Protecting lives and property;
16 2. Assisting in disaster relief or other humanitarian efforts;
17 3. Preventing or suppressing riot or civil disorder;
18 4. Enforcing the laws of the Commonwealth; or
19 5. Other similar purpose.
20 (b) In addition to ordering personnel and units to state active duty, the Governor
21 may order other members of the Kentucky National Guard to participate in or
22 perform duty in support of state active duty missions, notwithstanding the fact
23 that they are then entitled to receive federal pay and allowances pursuant to
24 Title 32 of the United States Code. The personnel may be ordered to perform
25 state active duty support missions either prior to, during, or after the time that
26 state active duty missions are planned or performed.
