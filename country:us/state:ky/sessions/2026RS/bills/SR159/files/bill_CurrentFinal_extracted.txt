3 WHEREAS, Wallace "Wah Wah" Jones, Sr. was a two-sport All-American at the

22 game, and he led the Bobcats to a remarkable record of 46 wins and only 9 losses, two
23 regional championships, and one state finals appearance; and
24 WHEREAS, on the hardwood, Blake Burnett has been a four-year leader as a
25 starting point guard, and has led the team in both scoring and rebounding the past two
