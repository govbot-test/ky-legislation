UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 566
1 A RESOLUTION confirming the appointment of Steven Olt to the Kentucky
2 Agricultural Finance Corporation.
3 WHEREAS, on June 26, 2025, in accordance with KRS 247.944 and by virtue of
4 Executive Order 2025-007, Commissioner Jonathan Shell appointed Steven Olt to the
5 Kentucky Agricultural Finance Corporation for a term expiring June 26, 2029; and
6 WHEREAS, the appointment is subject to Senate confirmation; and
7 WHEREAS, Steven Olt has been determined to meet the requirements of KRS
8 247.944, representing dairy farmers;
9 NOW, THEREFORE,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
11 Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The Senate hereby confirms the appointment of Steven Olt to the
13 Kentucky Agricultural Finance Corporation.
14 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
15 notification of its adoption to Steven Olt of Green County, Kentucky, and to
16 Commissioner Jonathan Shell, Corporate Drive Complex, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
