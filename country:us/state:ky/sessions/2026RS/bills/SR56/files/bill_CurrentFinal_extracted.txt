1 A RESOLUTION honoring Elder Nathan Craig and his wife, Sister Amy Craig,
2 upon their calling to serve the Ghana Accra South Mission.
3 WHEREAS, Elder Nathan Craig and his wife, Sister Amy Craig, are esteemed
4 residents of Hopkinsville, Kentucky, who have consistently demonstrated a profound
5 commitment to faith, family, and the service of others; and
6 WHEREAS, Elder Craig has served with high distinction as an Area Seventy for
7 The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, providing vital spiritual guidance and
8 administrative leadership for the Southeast region of the United States; and
9 WHEREAS, a respected member of the Kentucky Bar Association and partner at
10 Richardson & Craig, Elder Nathan Craig has balanced a successful professional career
11 with significant ecclesiastical responsibilities, including his previous service as President
12 of the Hopkinsville Kentucky Stake; and
13 WHEREAS, Elder Nathan Craig and Sister Amy Craig have accepted a sacred
14 three-year call to lead the Ghana Accra South Mission, a transition that will see them
15 depart their home in the Commonwealth to provide leadership and mentorship to
16 hundreds of young missionaries in West Africa; and
17 WHEREAS, their willingness to set aside their professional and personal pursuits in
18 Kentucky to engage in global humanitarian and spiritual service is a testament to their
19 character and the values of the Bluegrass State; and
20 WHEREAS, the Senate of the Commonwealth of Kentucky recognizes the
21 significant impact the Craigs have had on the moral and social fabric of Christian County
22 and the broader Southeast region;
23 NOW, THEREFORE,
24 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
25 Kentucky:
26 Section 1. The members of the Senate, both individually and collectively, do
27 hereby recognize and commend Elder Nathan Craig and Sister Amy Craig for their
