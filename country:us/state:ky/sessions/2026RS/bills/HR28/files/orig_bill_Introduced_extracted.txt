4 WHEREAS, Cuba is the largest and most populous country of all the Caribbean

17 Houston, Texas; and
18 WHEREAS, Cuban Americans now represent the largest single immigrant group in
19 Louisville. With a population estimated at more than 50,000 people today and expected
20 to double within the next five years, the community's increasing numbers have helped
21 offset a population decline in Metro Louisville; and
22 WHEREAS, the Cuban American community is known for their strong work ethic
23 and healthy entrepreneurial spirit. Thousands of Cubans proudly work in local factories
24 and warehouses, and in professional capacities as teachers, engineers, dentists, doctors,
25 and lawyers. In addition, Louisville currently boasts more than 500 small Cuban-owned
26 businesses that bring innovative products and services to the area, pay taxes, and improve
27 the labor market; and

1 WHEREAS, Cuban Americans in Kentucky significantly enrich the
2 Commonwealth's well-established reputation for good music, fine arts, and wonderful
3 dining experiences. Cultural elements of their home country shine through the work of
4 dozens of accomplished Cuban American artists, musicians, photographers, actors, and
5 dancers. Additionally, Kentucky's culinary scene continues to evolve and grow through
6 fresh flavors and dishes offered at some of the most popular restaurants and cafés in
7 Louisville;
8 NOW, THEREFORE,
9 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
10 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
11 Section 1. The House of Representatives hereby recognizes and honors the
12 cultural, social, and economic contributions of the Cuban American community to the
13 rich history of the city of Louisville and the Commonwealth of Kentucky.
14 Section 2. The House of Representatives acknowledges the resilience of the
15 Cuban American people in overcoming geographical, financial, and language barriers to
16 thrive in their new home country, and expresses strong solidarity with their aspirations
17 for a future where Cuba is free from authoritarian rule.
18 Section 3. The Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to transmit a
19 copy of this Resolution to Representative Daniel Grossberg.
