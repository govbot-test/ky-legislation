1 A RESOLUTION confirming the appointment of Fritz Giesecke to the Kentucky

5 Kentucky Agricultural Development Board for a term expiring July 6, 2029; and

8 248.707, representing Kentucky Farm Bureau Federation;

10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
11 Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The Senate hereby confirms the appointment of Fritz Giesecke to the
13 Kentucky Agricultural Development Board.
14 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
15 notification of its adoption to Fritz Giesecke of Hart County, Kentucky, and to
16 Commissioner Jonathan Shell, Corporate Drive Complex, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
