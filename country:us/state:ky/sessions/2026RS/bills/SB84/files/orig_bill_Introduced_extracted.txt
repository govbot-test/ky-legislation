UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1668
1 AN ACT relating to timely emergency response by coroners, making an
2 appropriation therefor and declaring an emergency.
3 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 39A IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) As used in this section, "association" means the Kentucky Coroners Association.
7 (2) County coroners, in consultation with the association, shall:
8 (a) Create ten (10) emergency response districts that include twelve (12)
9 counties each to cover all counties in Kentucky to ensure a timely response
10 to multiple fatality events; and
11 (b) Equip each emergency response district with an emergency response trailer
12 and necessary equipment to enable a response to any natural disaster,
13 multiple fatality event, or as needed to respond to fatalities within the
14 Commonwealth.
15 (3) The Division of Emergency Management shall promulgate administrative
16 regulations in accordance with KRS Chapter 13A to effectuate this section.
17 Section 2. There is hereby appropriated General Fund moneys in the amount of
18 $200,000 in fiscal year 2026-2027 to the Military Affairs budget unit to distribute to the
19 Kentucky Coroners Association for the actual expense of purchasing and outfitting
20 emergency response trailers and necessary equipment to respond to events outlined in
21 Section 1 of this Act.
22 Section 3. Whereas providing timely funding for multiple fatality events in the
23 Commonwealth is important for the citizens of the state, an emergency is declared to
24 exist, and this Act takes effect July 1, 2026.
