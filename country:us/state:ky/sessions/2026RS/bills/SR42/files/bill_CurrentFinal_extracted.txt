2 WHEREAS, the Ale-8-One Bottling Company was established in 1902 by George
3 Lee Wainscott in Winchester, Kentucky; and
4 WHEREAS, in 1926, Wainscott released Ale-8-One, originally named "A Late
5 One," at the Clark County Fair; and
6 WHEREAS, Ale-8-One is one of the founding members of the Kentucky Proud
7 Program, which promotes Kentucky-grown and made products; and
8 WHEREAS, the Ale-8-One family includes 120 employees who work and live in
9 Winchester and the surrounding communities; and
10 WHEREAS, every bottle of Ale-8-One originates from small batches of the secret
11 family recipe, hand-blended by Fielding Rogers, the fourth generation family owner,
12 following his great-great-uncle’s handwritten notes; and
13 WHEREAS, Ale-8-One received the prestigious Bluegrass Steward Award in 2025,
14 in recognition of its stewardship in Kentucky, contribution to the Kentucky Proud
15 Program, and its support of their employees and the Winchester community; and
16 WHEREAS, as Ale-8-One celebrates its 100th anniversary, it remains family-
17 owned and based in Winchester, Kentucky, and holds the distinction as the only soft
18 drink invented in Kentucky that continues to be made in Kentucky;
19 NOW, THEREFORE,
20 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
21 Kentucky:
22 Section 1. The members of the Senate hereby recognize and congratulate Ale-8-
23 One on its 100th Anniversary and honor it for 100 years of growth and success.
24 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor and
25 celebration of Ale-8-One.
26 Section 3. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
27 Resolution to Senator Greg Elkins.
