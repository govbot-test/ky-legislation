UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1362
1 AN ACT relating to branch budget recommendations.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 48.165 is amended to read as follows:
4 In the submission of a branch budget recommendation to the General Assembly no
5 branch of government shall submit any item as a "necessary government expense" or
6 submit any item without a dollar amount affixed thereto, unless the entire proceeds of a
7 particular trust and agency or other designated fund or account are to be assigned to
8 specific budget units. In the event that all proceeds from a particular fund or account are
9 to be assigned to specific budget units, an estimate of the receipts of each budget unit for
10 each year of the fiscal biennium shall be provided together with the receipts from the
11 previous two (2) fiscal bienniums.
