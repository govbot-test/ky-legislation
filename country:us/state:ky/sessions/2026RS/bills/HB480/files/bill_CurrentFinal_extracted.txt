14 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 45A IS CREATED TO

18 receives goods or services from a contractor or vendor; and
19 (b) "Undisputed amount" means a good-faith, valid, accurate, and timely
20 request for payment that has been submitted to an entity owing money, and
21 for which the entity has reviewed and confirmed that the money is due and
22 owing.
23 (2) All contracts shall include but not be limited to the following provisions for:
24 (a) Timely payments in accordance with KRS 45.451;
