UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 563
1 A RESOLUTION confirming the appointment of Dr. Kenny Burdine to the
2 Kentucky Agricultural Finance Corporation.
3 WHEREAS, on June 26, 2025, in accordance with KRS 247.944 and by virtue of
4 Executive Order 2025-007, Commissioner Jonathan Shell appointed Dr. Kenny Burdine
5 to the Kentucky Agricultural Finance Corporation for a term expiring June 26, 2029; and
6 WHEREAS, the appointment is subject to Senate confirmation; and
7 WHEREAS, Dr. Kenny Burdine has been determined to meet the requirements of
8 KRS 247.944, representing agricultural economists;
9 NOW, THEREFORE,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
11 Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The Senate hereby confirms the appointment of Dr. Kenny Burdine
13 to the Kentucky Agricultural Finance Corporation.
14 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
15 notification of its adoption to Dr. Kenny Burdine of Jessamine County, Kentucky, and to
16 Commissioner Jonathan Shell, Corporate Drive Complex, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
