UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 160
1 AN ACT relating to planning and zoning.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 100.401 TO 100.419 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 If a land use development plan or subdivision plan is for a proposed development
6 adjacent to the boundary of a county that is not part of the planning unit and the
7 development would likely have a substantial impact on the transportation or other
8 infrastructure located in the adjacent county, then the planning unit shall require as a
9 binding element of any application for the plan a memorandum of understanding or
10 other similar agreement approving the plan between the applicant and the adjacent
11 county that is not part of the planning unit.
