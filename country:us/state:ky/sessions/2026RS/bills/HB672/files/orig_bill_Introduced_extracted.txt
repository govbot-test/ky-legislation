1 AN ACT relating to branch budget bills.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 48.210 is amended to read as follows:
4 From the time of the submission of the budget recommendation of each branch of
5 government to the General Assembly until the enactment of all branch budget bills,
6 representatives of each branch of government shall be at the disposal of the General
7 Assembly and its appropriations committees and shall devote as much of their time as

9 chairmen. The Finance and Administration Cabinet, the Transportation Cabinet, and the
10 Office of State Budget Director shall provide such additional assistance to each branch of
11 government as may be required.
