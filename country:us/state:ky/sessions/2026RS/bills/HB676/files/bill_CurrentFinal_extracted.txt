1 AN ACT relating to the Kentucky statewide health data utility.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. The staff of the Legislative Research Commission is directed to
4 conduct a study on the development of a Kentucky statewide health data utility. The
5 study shall analyze and provide:
6 (1) Policy options for establishing a Kentucky statewide health data utility that:
7 (a) Is the sole statewide network for the sharing and exchange of health
8 information;
9 (b) Aggregates health information received from participants; and
10 (c) Provides access, in a form and manner that ensures the privacy and security of
11 personal health information as required under federal and state law, to health information
12 in the statewide health data utility to authorized recipients for the purposes of:
13 1. Collecting and analyzing data to inform the authorized recipients as to health
14 care access, utilization, cost, safety, and quality in Kentucky;
15 2. Enabling health care professionals and health facilities to evaluate and
16 monitor care and treatment of patients; and
17 3. Enabling patients to access, through a technology enabled interface, the
18 patient's electronic longitudinal health record;
19 (2) Strategies for the direction and governance of the statewide health data utility
20 by and through the Legislative Research Commission; and
21 (3) Strategies for development of the statewide health data utility in a timely,
22 efficient, secure, sustainable, and cost-effective manner.
23 Section 2. In carrying out the duties in Section 1 of this Act, the Legislative
24 Research Commission may:
25 (1) Consult with officials and other entities in this state and other states to study
26 how they are engaging in the development, implementation, and operation of health data
27 utilities and collection efforts;
