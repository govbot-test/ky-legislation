1 AN ACT relating to sentencing.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 532.110 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) When multiple sentences of imprisonment are imposed on a defendant for more
5 than one (1) crime, including a crime for which a previous sentence of probation or
6 conditional discharge has been revoked, the multiple sentences shall run
7 concurrently or consecutively as the court shall determine at the time of sentence,
8 except that:
9 (a) A definite and an indeterminate term shall run concurrently and both
10 sentences shall be satisfied by service of the indeterminate term;
11 (b) The aggregate of consecutive definite terms shall not exceed one (1) year; and
12 (c) [1. Except as provided in paragraph (d) of this subsection, the aggregate of
13 consecutive indeterminate terms shall not exceed in maximum length
14 the longest extended term which would be authorized by KRS 532.080
15 for the highest class of crime for which any of the sentences is imposed,
16 except as described in KRS 533.060(2) or (3).
17 2. In no event shall the aggregate of consecutive indeterminate terms
18 exceed seventy (70) years; and
19 (d) ]The sentences of a defendant convicted of two (2) or more felony sex crimes,
20 as defined in KRS 17.500, involving two (2) or more victims shall run
21 consecutively.
22 (2) If the court does not specify the manner in which a sentence imposed by it is to run,
23 the sentence shall run concurrently with any other sentence which the defendant
24 must serve unless the sentence is required by subsection (3) of this section or KRS
25 533.060 to run consecutively.
