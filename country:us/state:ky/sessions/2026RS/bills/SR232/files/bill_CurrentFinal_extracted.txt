1 A RESOLUTION confirming the reappointment of Angela Hatton to the Public

8 278.060;

10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
11 Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The Senate confirms the reappointment of Angela Hatton to the
13 Public Service Commission.
14 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
15 notification of its adoption to Angela Hatton of Whitesburg, Letcher County, Kentucky,
16 and to Governor Andy Beshear, 703 Capital Avenue, Room 133, Frankfort, Kentucky
17 40601.
