5 Coyle to the Kentucky State Fair Board for a term expiring May 19, 2029; and

8 of KRS 247.090, representing the Kentucky Farm Bureau Federation;
9 NOW, THEREFORE,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
11 Kentucky:
12 Section 1. The Senate hereby confirms the appointment of Andrew Marshall
13 Coyle to the Kentucky State Fair Board.
14 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall forward a copy of this Resolution and
15 notification of its adoption to Andrew Marshall Coyle of Bath County, Kentucky, and to
16 Commissioner Jonathan Shell, Corporate Drive Complex, Frankfort, Kentucky 40601.
