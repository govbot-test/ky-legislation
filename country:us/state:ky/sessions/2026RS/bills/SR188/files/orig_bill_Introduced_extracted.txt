UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2571
1 A RESOLUTION honoring the Piner Elementary School archery team for winning
2 the 2026 Kentucky National Archery in the Schools Program 3D state championship.
3 WHEREAS, on March 14, 2026, the Piner Elementary School archery team
4 competed in the Kentucky National Archery in the Schools Program (NASP) state 3D
5 tournament and won first place with an impressive score of 1529; and
6 WHEREAS, this victory reflects the diligent effort and unwavering commitment of
7 the young archers throughout the season; and
8 WHEREAS, the team consistently performed well in both 3D and Bullseye
9 competitions throughout the season, winning several local tournaments; and
10 WHEREAS, at the Walton-Verona High School "Thwackdown" tournament, the
11 Piner Elementary School archery team achieved a season-high score of 3003 in Bullseye,
12 which marked the team as one of only seven Kentucky elementary schools to hit the
13 3000-point milestone during the season; and
14 WHEREAS, these victories provided the confidence and momentum needed for the
15 team to rise to the occasion and take home the state title; and
16 WHEREAS, the Piner Elementary School archery team has had a long history of
17 prior successes leading up to their 2026 NASP state tournament win; and
18 WHEREAS, since 2010, the team has appeared in 13 of the past 16 Bullseye state
19 tournaments; and
20 WHEREAS, the most recent victory of the team in securing the state championship
21 highlights their long tradition of achievement and the dedication of the coaches and
22 student archers; and
23 WHEREAS, the 2026 Piner Elementary School archery team is composed of 29
24 student archers and five coaches, who, through their combined effort, accomplished many
25 impressive feats throughout the season and secured the state title at the 2026 NASP 3D
26 state tournament;
27 NOW, THEREFORE,
