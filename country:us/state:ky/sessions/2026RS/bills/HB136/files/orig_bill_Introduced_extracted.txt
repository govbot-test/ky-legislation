1 AN ACT relating to campaign finance.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 121.175 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) (a) No candidate, committee, or contributing organization shall permit funds in a
5 campaign account to be expended for any purpose other than for allowable
6 campaign expenditures.
7 (b) As used in this section "allowable campaign expenditures":
8 1. Means expenditures including reimbursement for actual expenses, made
9 directly and primarily in support of or opposition to a candidate,
10 constitutional amendment, or public question which will appear on the
11 ballot and includes, but is not limited to, expenditures for staff salaries,
12 gifts and meals for volunteer campaign workers and fundraisers, food
13 and beverages provided at a campaign rally, advertising, office space,
14 necessary travel if reported, campaign paraphernalia, purchases of
15 advertisements in athletic and scholastic publications, communications
16 with constituents or prospective voters, polling and consulting, printing,
17 graphic arts, or advertising services, postage, office supplies, stationery,
18 newsletters, and equipment which is used primarily for the
19 administration of the campaign, or for fees incurred from legal services
20 while defending a matter before the Kentucky Legislative Ethics
21 Commission in which the final adjudication is rendered in favor of the
22 candidate; and.[
23 "Allowable campaign expenditures"]2. Does not include necessary
24 travel unless reported, expenditures of funds in a campaign account for
25 any purpose made unlawful by other provisions of the Kentucky
26 Revised Statutes or which would bestow a private pecuniary benefit,
27 except for payment of the reasonable value of goods and services
