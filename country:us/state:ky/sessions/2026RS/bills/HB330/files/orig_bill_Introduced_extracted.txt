UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 337
1 AN ACT relating to jurisdiction of contractor disputes involving real property.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 411 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 Except as may be provided under KRS Chapter 3, and notwithstanding any other law to
6 the contrary, the courts of this Commonwealth shall have exclusive jurisdiction in any
7 legal action arising from work performed by a contractor involving real property
8 located in this state. As used in this section, "real property" includes any:
9 (1) Estate, whether held by leasehold or in fee simple;
10 (2) Easement;
11 (3) Fixture or appurtenance;
12 (4) Other interest or right in and to the property; and
13 (5) Improvements and structures on the property.
