1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor and loving memory of Tristan
2 Markese York Brown.
3 WHEREAS, Tristan Markese York Brown was born May 1, 2009, to Lindsey
4 Danielle York Brown and Rico Brown, Jr.; and
5 WHEREAS, Tristan Markese York Brown was a vibrant 16 year old from Berea
6 and a sophomore at Madison Southern High School, where his presence was a gift to his
7 peers and teachers alike. He loved football, shoes, photography, fishing, his dogs, and
8 playing video games, especially Grand Theft Auto 5. He also enjoyed traveling and
9 marveling at the sunset; and
10 WHEREAS, Tristan Markese York Brown was defined by a heart that knew no
11 bounds. He was a steadfast friend, a pillar of reliability, and a young man whose respect
12 for others was matched only by his kindness. Above all, he embraced his role as a
13 devoted big brother to his sister, Laniya, with a protective and gentle spirit; and
14 WHEREAS, Tristan Markese York Brown's life was anchored by his faith. He was
15 a dedicated servant of Christ and a faithful member of Life Church in Richmond. Tristan
16 lived with a grace that extended far beyond his years; and
17 WHEREAS, on July 20, 2025, Tristan Markese York Brown tragically departed
18 these earthly bounds. Tristan's life was taken in a traffic catastrophe when a semi-truck
19 rearended the pickup truck driven by his father, throwing Tristan from the vehicle. The
20 impact also severely injured his father and sister. The investigation following the accident
21 showed that the semi-truck driver did not engage his brakes prior to the accident; and
22 WHEREAS, in addition to his parents, Tristan Markese York Brown is survived by
23 his sister, Laniya Brown; paternal grandparents, Rico Brown, Sr., Mary Maccall, and
24 Pamela Powell; maternal grandparents, Chris (Kristi) York, Melissa McGowan, and
25 Jamie York; great grandmothers, Judy Smith and Alma Crew; paternal aunts and uncles,
26 Raquel Brown, Derico Brown, Rodney Huggins, Jr., Alexis Huggins, Gracie Brown, and
27 Ella Brown; and maternal aunts and uncles, Rhyan York, Gabby (Keith) Hughes, and
