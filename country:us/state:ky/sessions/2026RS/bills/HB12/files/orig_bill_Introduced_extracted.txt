UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 164
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 61.7991 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) As used in this section:
5 (a) "Covered person" means a:
6 1. Judicial officer or an immediate family member of a judicial officer; or
7 2. Current member of the General Assembly or his or her immediate
8 family member;
9 (b) "Disclose" means to post, display, publish, or otherwise make publicly
10 available;
11 (c) "Immediate family member" means:
12 1. A spouse, child, parent, or person under the familial custody or care of a
13 covered person[judicial officer]; or
14 2. Any other familial relative who resides in the same household as the
15 covered person[judicial officer];
16 (d) "Judicial officer" means any active or senior judge and includes a:
17 1. Justice of the United States or a judge of the United States as those
18 terms are defined in 28 U.S.C. sec. 451;
19 2. Bankruptcy judge appointed under 28 U.S.C. sec. 152 or recalled
20 pursuant to 28 U.S.C. sec. 375;
21 3. United States magistrate judge appointed under 28 U.S.C. sec. 631 or
22 recalled pursuant to 28 U.S.C. sec. 375;
23 4. Judge confirmed by the United States Senate and empowered by statute
24 in any commonwealth, territory, or possession to perform the duties of a
25 federal judge;
26 5. Judge of the United States Court of Federal Claims appointed under 28
27 U.S.C. sec. 171; and

1 6. Justice, judge, trial commissioner, or domestic relations commissioner
2 of the Kentucky Court of Justice;
3 (e) "Personally identifiable information" means data that can identify a covered
4 person and includes:
5 1. Date of birth;
6 2. Biometric, health, or medical data, or insurance information;
7 3. Residence addresses of the covered person;
8 4. Home or cellular telephone numbers;
9 5. Personal email addresses;
10 6. Identities of the children of a covered person[judicial officer] and names
11 and locations of schools and daycare facilities they attend;
12 7. Social Security number; and
13 8. The name of an immediate family member's employer; and
14 (f) "Written request" means a notice signed by a covered person requesting a
15 government agency refrain from posting or displaying publicly available
16 content that includes the personally identifiable information of the covered
17 person.
18 (2) (a) A government agency shall not disclose the personally identifiable
19 information of any covered person if the covered person has made a written
20 request to the government agency that this personally identifiable information
21 not be disclosed.
22 (b) Upon receipt of the written request, personally identifiable information shall
23 be removed from publicly available content within seventy-two (72) hours.
24 (c) A request under this subsection may be made on behalf of a minor who is a
25 covered person by a parent or guardian of the minor.
26 (3) (a) After the government agency has removed the personally identifiable
27 information from publicly available content, the government agency shall not
