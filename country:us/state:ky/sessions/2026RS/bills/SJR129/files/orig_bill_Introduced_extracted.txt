2 Marion County.
3 WHEREAS, Brian Hatt served honorably as a volunteer firefighter with the
4 Raywick Fire Department, dedicating his life to the protection of the citizens of Marion
5 County; and
6 WHEREAS, throughout his career as a firefighter, Brian Hatt demonstrated
7 extraordinary courage, selflessness, and commitment to public safety; and
8 WHEREAS, on August 25, 2025, Brian Hatt tragically lost his life in the line of
9 duty while responding to an emergency; and
10 WHEREAS, the entire community of Marion County was shocked and saddened by
11 the passing of Brian Hatt, and he is sorely missed and fondly remembered by all who
12 knew him; and
13 WHEREAS, the Marion County Fiscal Court has adopted a Resolution requesting
14 that a memorial highway designation for Brian Hatt be approved by the General
15 Assembly as a permanent reminder of his service and sacrifice;
16 NOW, THEREFORE,
17 Be it resolved by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
18 Section 1. The Transportation Cabinet shall designate a portion of Kentucky
19 Route 84 in Marion County, from the intersection with Kentucky Route 527 and
20 Broadway Street (mile point 5.25) to the intersection with Sallie Ray Pike (mile point
21 7.269), as the "Lt. Brian Hatt Memorial Highway" and shall, within 30 days of the
22 effective date of this Resolution, erect appropriate signs denoting this designation.

UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2402
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION designating the Lt. Brian Hatt Memorial Highway in
2 Marion County.
3 WHEREAS, Brian Hatt served honorably as a volunteer firefighter with the
4 Raywick Fire Department, dedicating his life to the protection of the citizens of Marion
5 County; and

7 extraordinary courage, selflessness, and commitment to public safety; and

15 Assembly as a permanent reminder of his service and sacrifice;

17 Be it resolved by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
18 Section 1. The Transportation Cabinet shall designate a portion of Kentucky
19 Route 84 in Marion County, from the intersection with Kentucky Route 527 and
20 Broadway Street (mile point 5.25) to the intersection with Sallie Ray Pike (mile point
