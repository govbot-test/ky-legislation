1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor and loving memory of Edward
2 Todd, Jr.
3 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., was the loving and beloved son of the late Edward
4 Todd, Sr., and Gwendolyn Davis; and
5 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., answered the call of his country, serving in the
6 United States Army for an exceptional and notable career of 30 years from which he
7 retired at the rank of First Sergeant; and
8 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., served God through his membership in the Freeman
9 Chapel Christian Methodist Episcopal Church; and
10 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., served others through his work as a licensed
11 practical nurse after his retirement from the United States Army; and
12 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., was known for his love of yard work and raising
13 exquisite roses as well as his love of reading, racquetball, history, and current events; and
14 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., set an excellent example for all the young people he
15 knew; and
16 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., was the loving and beloved husband of his wife of
17 50 years, Willie Frances "Billie" Todd; the loving and beloved father of Sharon Todd and
18 Felicia (Robert) Todd-Jones; the loving and beloved father-in-law of Pastor Bridgette
19 Todd; the loving and beloved brother of Pamela Todd and Dorothy Gamble-Matthews;
20 the loving and beloved stepbrother of Sandra Perkins, Mattie Dennis, Elizabeth Dock,
21 and James Perkins; the doting grandfather of Shannon Beard, Joshua Grays, Edward
22 Todd III, Gregory Todd, Jr., and Michael Todd; and the doting great-grandfather of
23 Shalanee Triplett, Jordan Grays, Jaylon Grays, and Anya Todd; and
24 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., was the loving and beloved father of Gregory Todd,
25 Sr., loving and beloved brother of Blanche Todd Thomas, and the loving and beloved
26 cousin of Laverne Martin, all of whom preceded him in passing from this life; and
27 WHEREAS, Edward Todd, Jr., was held in love, honor and esteem by many others;
