Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1730
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 2.147 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The nineteenth of June each year shall be observed in Kentucky as "Juneteenth
5 National Freedom Day."
6 (2) The Governor shall proclaim June 19 of each year as "Juneteenth National Freedom
7 Day" and will encourage all Kentuckians celebrating this day to honor and reflect
8 on the significant roles that African-Americans have played in the history of the
9 United States and the Commonwealth of Kentucky.
