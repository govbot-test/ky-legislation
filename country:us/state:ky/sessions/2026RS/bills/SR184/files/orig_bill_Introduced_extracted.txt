1 A RESOLUTION honoring the Murray State University Racers baseball team,
2 2025 College World Series participants.
3 WHEREAS, on June 9, 2025, at Jack Coombs field on the campus of Duke
4 University, the Murray State University Racers defeated the Duke Blue Devils by a score
5 of 5-4 to win the Durham Super Regional and advance to the 2025 College World Series
6 (CWS) in Omaha, Nebraska; and
7 WHEREAS, the underdog Racers' Cinderella run to Omaha marked only the fourth
8 time in the history of the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) Baseball
9 Tournament that a team seeded fourth in one of the tournament's 16 regionals advanced
10 through regional and super regional play to the CWS; and
11 WHEREAS, the Racers earned their way into the NCAA Tournament by winning
12 the Missouri Valley Conference Tournament championship, their first since joining that
13 conference in 2022; and
14 WHEREAS, in the first game in the double-elimination regional round, the Racers
15 scored a 9-6 win over the Ole Miss Rebels, who were ranked as the 10th-best team in the
16 entire 64-team field. The Racers then defeated Georgia Tech and lost a game against Ole
17 Miss, setting up a winner-take-all regional final; and
18 WHEREAS, the regional final saw an offensive explosion, with the Racers staking
19 themselves to a 12-3 lead after 7½ innings. After a spirited Rebel rally, Graham Kelham
20 came on in relief to record the last eight outs of the game for the Racers, six of which
21 came by strikeout; and
22 WHEREAS, with the victory, the Racers advanced to a best-of-three game super
23 regional on the road against Duke University. After splitting the first two games of the
24 series, the Racers edged the Blue Devils 5-4 in game three, with Luke Mistone hitting the
25 go-ahead home run in the eighth inning and Kelham earning the win with another stellar
26 relief appearance; and
