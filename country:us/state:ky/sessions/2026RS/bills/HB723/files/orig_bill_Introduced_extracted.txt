Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2043
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 139 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 On or before October 1, 2026, and every October 1 thereafter, eighty percent (80%) of
6 the tax receipts, interest, and penalties of sales and use taxes collected during the
7 immediately preceding fiscal year from establishments classified under the 2022 North
8 American Industry Classification System Code 459110 shall be transferred to the
9 Kentucky Heritage Land Conservation fund established in KRS 146.570.
