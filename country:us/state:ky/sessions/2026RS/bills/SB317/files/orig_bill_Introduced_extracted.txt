Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2362
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 454.185 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The surety in every bond provided for by any statute derived from the former Civil
5 Code[,] or by the Rules of Civil Procedure shall:[must ]
6 (a) Be a resident of this state;[, and ]
7 (b) Be worth double the sum to be secured beyond the amount of the surety's[his]
8 debts;[,] and
9 (c) Have property liable to execution in this state equal to the sum to be secured.
10 (2) If there are[be] two (2) or more sureties in the same bond, they shall[must], in the
11 aggregate, have the qualifications prescribed in this section.
