1 A RESOLUTION confirming the appointment of Carrie Camille Caples to the
2 Standards and Assessments Process Review Committee.

4 of Executive Order 2025-656, Governor Andy Beshear appointed Carrie Camille Caples
5 to the Standards and Assessments Process Review Committee for a term expiring
6 October 8, 2029; and
7 WHEREAS, the appointment is subject to Senate confirmation; and

9 of KRS 158.6453, representing principals;

12 Kentucky:

14 Caples to the Standards and Assessments Process Review Committee.

16 notification of its adoption to Carrie Camille Caples of Hopkinsville, Christian County,
17 Kentucky, and to Governor Andy Beshear, 703 Capital Avenue, Room 133, Frankfort,
18 Kentucky 40601.
