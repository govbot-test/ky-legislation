UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 1383
1 AN ACT relating to branch budget recommendations.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 48.100 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) A branch budget recommendation shall be submitted to the General Assembly by
5 the Governor for the executive branch, including a separate recommendation for
6 the Transportation Cabinet, the Chief Justice for the judicial branch, and the
7 Legislative Research Commission for the legislative branch. The budget
8 recommendations shall be submitted to the General Assembly on or before the tenth
9 legislative day of each even-numbered-year regular session, except in the year
10 following the election of a new Governor when the budget recommendations shall
11 be submitted not later than the fifteenth legislative day of an even-numbered-year
12 regular session.
13 (2) Budget recommendations shall be submitted on the first day of any extraordinary
14 session called for the purpose of amending a branch budget bill.
