Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2273
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 500 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 A person shall not be penalized, prosecuted, or otherwise subjected to adverse action
6 based on that person's actual, potential, perceived, or alleged pregnancy outcomes,
7 including but not limited to miscarriage, stillbirth, or abortion.
8 Section 2. The following KRS section is repealed:
9 530.030 Concealing birth of infant.
