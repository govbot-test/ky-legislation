5 ATHLETIC TRAINER COMPACT
6 SECTION 1. TITLE AND PURPOSE
7 This statute shall be known and cited as the Athletic Trainer Compact. The
8 purposes of this compact are to expand mobility of athletic training practice and
9 improve public access to services by providing qualified licensed athletic trainers the
10 ability to practice in other member states. This compact preserves the regulatory
11 authority of states to protect public health and safety through the current system of
12 state licensure.
13 This compact is designed to achieve the following objectives:
14 A. Increase public access to athletic training and enhance continuity of care by
15 providing for the mutual recognition of other licenses issued by member states;
16 B. Provide an additional streamlined opportunity for interstate practice by
17 licensed athletic trainers who meet compact uniform requirements;
18 C. Promote mobility and workforce development by eliminating the necessity
19 for licenses in multiple states by providing for the mutual recognition of other licenses
20 issued by member states;
21 D. Reduce administrative burdens on licensed athletic trainers and member
22 states;
23 E. Enhance the states’ ability to protect the public’s health and safety;
24 F. Encourage the cooperation of member states in regulating interstate
25 practice of licensed athletic trainers;
26 G. Support relocating active military members and their spouses;
27 H. Enhance the exchange of licensure, investigative, and disciplinary
