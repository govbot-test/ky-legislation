1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor and loving memory of Carolyn
2 Greenwell Haydon and Joseph "Jodie" Albert Haydon.
3 WHEREAS, Carolyn and Jodie Haydon were born in Bardstown and were
4 cherished residents of the city, Carolyn being the daughter of James Bernard "Jimmy"
5 Greenwell and Verna Mae Greenwell, and Jodie Haydon being the son of Joseph Albert
6 and Leona Isabelle Blanford Haydon; and
7 WHEREAS, Carolyn Haydon graduated from Bethlehem High School and
8 Edgecliff College, and Jodie Haydon graduated from St. Joseph Preparatory School and
9 Bellarmine College; and
10 WHEREAS, Carolyn and Jodie Haydon were bonded in holy matrimony, a sacred
11 and loving bond that would continue throughout their lives; and
12 WHEREAS, Carolyn and Jodie Haydon were guided deeply by their faith as
13 members of St. Joseph Catholic Church and the St. Joseph Parish of which it is a part;
14 and
15 WHEREAS, Carolyn Haydon was known for her warmth, kindness, and generosity
16 of her time and considerable talents, and Jodie Haydon was known for his leadership and
17 public service, was an active member of the Army National Guard, a member of the Sons
18 of Bardstown in the 138th Field Artillery, Battery C, and survived the pivotal attack on
19 Firebase Tomahawk during the Vietnam Conflict, served as a Kentucky State
20 Representative, and was a member of many organizations such as the Kentucky Lottery
21 Commission, Bardstown City Council, Knights of Columbus, and Flaget Memorial
22 Hospital; and
23 WHEREAS, Carolyn and Jodie Haydon lived for their family and relished their
24 manifold friendships; and
25 WHEREAS, Carolyn and Jodie Haydon are survived by their children, Allison
26 Haydon (Chris) Osborne, and Amy Haydon Pehlke, and grandchildren, Caroline,
27 Christopher (Amy Luu), Haydon, Nicholas, and Miller Osborne, and Amelia and Hilary

19 Section 1. The Senate expresses its most profound sympathy upon the passing
20 of Carolyn and Jodie Haydon and extends heartfelt condolences to their family, friends,

23 memory of Carolyn and Jodie Haydon.
