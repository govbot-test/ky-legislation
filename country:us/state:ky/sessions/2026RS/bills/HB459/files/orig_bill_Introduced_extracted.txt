1 AN ACT relating to licensed occupations.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 335B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Each licensing authority listed in subsection (4) of this section shall collect the
6 following data using a data collection application, during the annual, biennial, or
7 triennial licensure renewal process:
8 (a) Licensee's personal information, including but not limited to:
9 1. The licensee's first, middle, and last name;
10 2. Email address and phone number;
11 3. Physical address of all practice locations;
12 4. License type;
13 5. License number and date of issuance;
14 6. License expiration; and
15 7. National Provider Identifier number; and
16 (b) Workforce participation information, including but not limited to:
17 1. Degree level or certificates completed;
18 2. Employment type;
19 3. Employment specialty;
20 4. Employment setting;
21 5. Employment status, including but not limited to:
22 a. Actively employed in respective field;
23 b. Actively employed in a clinical setting, either in person or
24 through telehealth, or both;
25 c. Actively employed outside of respective field;
26 d. Unemployed but seeking work in respective field; or
27 e. Unemployed but not seeking work in respective field;

22 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
23 (1) The board shall grant licensure under KRS 335.320 and 335.330 to an applicant
24 who:
25 (a) Holds an active license from another state to practice as an independent
26 marriage and family therapist;
27 (b) Is in good standing with the certifying or licensing board of that state;

2 (d) Has not had disciplinary actions of a serious nature brought against him or
3 her by a registration, certification, or licensing board or agency;
4 (e) Has met the examination requirement in KRS 335.330(3); and

10 SECTION 3. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 335.300 TO 335.399 IS CREATED
11 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
12 (1) If the board identifies any technical error in any application submitted for
13 issuance, renewal, or reinstatement of a license or permit under KRS 335.300 to
14 335.399:
15 (a) The board shall notify the applicant of the error within one (1) week of its
16 discovery;
17 (b) The applicant shall correct the error within two (2) weeks of notification of
18 the error; and
19 (c) The board shall review the correction within one (1) week of receipt of the
20 submitted correction.
21 (2) The board shall issue the license or permit within two (2) weeks of the date the
22 application is complete.
23 Section 4. Each licensing authority required to collect data in accordance with
24 Section 1 of this Act shall have until the licensing authority's next licensure renewal date
25 following the effective date of this Act to comply with the collection requirement.
26 Section 5. Section 1 of this Act takes effect January 1, 2028.
