1 AN ACT relating to licensed occupations.

3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 335B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Each licensing authority listed in subsection (4) of this section shall collect the
6 following data using a data collection application, during the annual, biennial, or
7 triennial licensure renewal process:
8 (a) Licensee's personal information, including but not limited to:
9 1. The licensee's first, middle, and last name;
10 2. Date of birth;
11 3. Mailing address, email address, and phone number;
12 4. Physical address of all practice locations;
13 5. License type;
14 6. License number and date of issuance;
15 7. License expiration; and
16 8. National Provider Identifier number; and
17 (b) Workforce participation information, including but not limited to:
18 1. Degree level or certificates completed;
19 2. Employment type;
20 3. Employment specialty;
21 4. Employment setting;
22 5. Employment status, including but not limited to:
23 a. Actively employed in respective field;
24 b. Actively employed in a clinical setting, either in person or
25 through telehealth, or both;
26 c. Actively employed outside of respective field;
27 d. Unemployed but seeking work in respective field; or

23 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
24 (1) The board shall grant licensure under KRS 335.320 and 335.330 to an applicant
25 who:
26 (a) Holds an active license from another state to practice as an independent
27 marriage and family therapist;

2 (c) Is of good moral character;
3 (d) Has not had disciplinary actions of a serious nature brought against him or
4 her by a registration, certification, or licensing board or agency;

11 SECTION 3. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 335.300 TO 335.399 IS CREATED
12 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
13 (1) If the board identifies any technical error in any application submitted for
14 issuance, renewal, or reinstatement of a license or permit under KRS 335.300 to
15 335.399:
16 (a) The board shall notify the applicant of the error within one (1) week of its
17 discovery;
18 (b) The applicant shall correct the error within two (2) weeks of notification of
19 the error; and
20 (c) The board shall review the correction within one (1) week of receipt of the
21 submitted correction.
22 (2) The board shall issue the license or permit within two (2) weeks of the date the
23 application is complete.
24 Section 4. Each licensing authority required to collect data in accordance with
25 Section 1 of this Act shall have until the licensing authority's next licensure renewal date
26 following the effective date of this Act to comply with the collection requirement.
27 Section 5. Section 1 of this Act takes effect January 1, 2028.
