3 WHEREAS, the labor movement has played an essential role in shaping the
4 economic, social, and civic foundations of the Commonwealth of Kentucky and the

6 WHEREAS, generations of workers across the Commonwealth have organized,
7 advocated, and sacrificed to secure safer workplaces, fair wages, reasonable hours, and

9 WHEREAS, Kentucky's labor organizations continue to champion the values of

13 enduring legacy and ongoing contributions of the labor movement and honor the workers
14 whose efforts sustain the Commonwealth each day;

16 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
17 Kentucky:

19 Day, to honor the labor movement for its historic and continuing contributions to the
20 welfare, prosperity, and democratic strength of the Commonwealth.
21 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor of the labor
22 movement and the working men and women of Kentucky whose service, sacrifice,
23 solidarity continue to move the Commonwealth forward.
