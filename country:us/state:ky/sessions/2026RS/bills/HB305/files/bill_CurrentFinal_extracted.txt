UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS HB 305/EN
1 AN ACT relating to grand jury service.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 29A IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Except as allowed under the Kentucky Rules of Criminal Procedure, a person
6 present during any part of a grand jury proceeding shall not knowingly:
7 (a) Record any testimony or evidence presented during the proceeding; or
8 (b) Disclose or disseminate at any time, any evidence, testimony, or other
9 information that the person witnessed, read, or heard while present at a
10 grand jury proceeding.
11 (2) This section shall not apply to a:
12 (a) Commonwealth's attorney or any person employed by a Commonwealth's
13 attorney; or
14 (b) Peace officer;
15 while engaged in the performance of his or her official duties.
16 Section 2. KRS 29A.990 is amended to read as follows:
17 (1) Any employer who violates[ subsection (1) or (2) of] KRS 29A.160(1) or (2) is
18 guilty of a Class B misdemeanor.
19 (2) Any willful violation of KRS 29A.010 to 29A.330 for which a penalty is not
20 otherwise provided by statute shall be punishable as a Class A misdemeanor.
21 (3) Any person who violates:
22 (a) Subsection (1)(a) of Section 1 of this Act shall be guilty of a Class A
23 misdemeanor; or
24 (b) Subsection (1)(b) of Section 1 of this Act shall be guilty of a Class D felony;
25 unless the person is a public servant as defined in KRS 519.010, in which case the
26 person shall be penalized one (1) class more severely than provided under this
27 subsection.
