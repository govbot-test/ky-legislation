1 AN ACT relating to teachers.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 161 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The General Assembly hereby finds that:
6 (a) Respecting the authority of teachers is essential to creating an environment
7 conducive to learning, effective instruction in the classroom, and proper
8 administration of the public schools; and
9 (b) To maintain and protect the authority of teachers, it is important that
10 teachers, school and district administrators, parents, and students are fully
11 informed of the various rights conferred upon teachers pursuant to this
12 section.
13 (2) Notwithstanding any statute to the contrary, a teacher in a local school district
14 has the right to:
15 (a) Teach free from the fear of frivolous lawsuits, including the right to
16 qualified immunity and to a legal defense, and to indemnification by the
17 employing local school board for actions taken in the performance of duties
18 of the teacher's employment;
19 (b) Appropriately discipline students in accordance with KRS 158.150 and local
20 school board policy;
21 (c) Establish classroom rules of conduct applicable to students in the
22 classroom;
23 (d) Remove any persistently disruptive student from his or her classroom when
24 the student's behavior prevents the orderly instruction of other students or
25 when the student displays impudent or defiant behavior and to place the
26 student in the custody of the principal or his or her designee;
27 (e) Have his or her professional judgment and discretion respected by school
