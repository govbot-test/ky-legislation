UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2094
1 A RESOLUTION adjourning the Senate in honor of the service of Anthony S.
2 "Tony" Campbell, former President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of East Kentucky
3 Power Cooperative (EKPC).
4 WHEREAS, Tony Campbell served with distinction as the President and CEO of
5 EKPC from June 2009 to December 2025; and
6 WHEREAS, over the past 16 years under his leadership, even as EKPC's system
7 energy needs grew by more than 20 percent, EKPC continued to provide safe, reliable,
8 and affordable electric through 16 distribution cooperatives that serve over 1.1 million
9 Kentucky residents, as well as farms, businesses, and large industrial sites in 89 counties
10 across the Commonwealth; and
11 WHEREAS, under Tony Campbell's leadership, EKPC's board and management
12 pursued projects to ensure long-term reliable, competitively priced power for
13 Kentuckians by adding a 745 megawatt natural gas combined cycle unit at Copper
14 Station, building the 214 megawatt Liberty Station in Casey County featuring
15 reciprocating internal combustion engine units, and co-firing units to use both coal and
16 natural gas; and
17 WHEREAS, Tony Campbell additionally placed EKPC on a strong path to financial
18 strength and stability, building member's equity, and saving EKPC's owner-members
19 significant cost-increases while maintaining long-term reliability; and
20 WHEREAS, in 2013, Tony Campbell reinforced EKPC's energy reliability and
21 affordability through its membership with the Pennsylvania-New Jersey-Maryland
22 Interconnection, a regional transmission organization that coordinates the movement of
23 wholesale electricity across 13 states and the District of Columbia, to garner the full
24 value of its generation and transmission fleet; and
25 WHEREAS, Tony Campbell is a trusted leader within the energy generation and
26 transmission industry, using his voice to amplify the message that we need dependable
27 generation to keep us strong and safe, alongside the voice of United States President
