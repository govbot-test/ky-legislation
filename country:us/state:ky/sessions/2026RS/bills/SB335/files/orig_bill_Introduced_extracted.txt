Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS BR 2372
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 65 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) A city, county, charter county government, urban-county government, unified
6 local government, consolidated local government, or special district shall not
7 adopt or enforce any ordinance, regulation, or other rule that restricts the
8 occupancy of any dwelling unit:
9 (a) To less than two (2) occupants per bedroom; or
10 (b) Based on the familial or nonfamilial relationship or marital status among
11 the occupants.
12 (2) This section shall not be construed to affect a local government's ability to
13 enforce the state building code or any uniform code or administrative regulation
14 related to fire safety or the construction or safety of buildings.
