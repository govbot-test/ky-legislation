1 AN ACT relating to sickle cell disease.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 216B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 By January 1, 2027, each hospital licensed by the Cabinet for Health and Family
6 Services shall:
7 (1) Establish policies and procedures to improve care for sickle cell disease patients
8 during hospital and emergency room visits, including:
9 (a) Expedited pain management protocols implementing standardized
10 procedures to prioritize pain relief for sickle cell disease patients within
11 thirty (30) minutes of arrival;
12 (b) Sickle cell disease medical care teams trained in sickle cell disease crisis
13 management for hospital emergency rooms; and
14 (c) Required training for all emergency room staff on sickle cell disease that
15 focuses on recognizing pain episodes, addressing care and treatment bias,
16 and providing culturally competent care;
17 (2) Develop programs to prevent and address addiction risks for sickle cell disease
18 patients, including:
19 (a) Alternative pain management options including nonopioid pain
20 management techniques such as cognitive behavioral therapy and
21 integrative medicine; and
22 (b) Accessible addiction treatment specifically designed for sickle cell disease
23 patients who have developed opioid dependence to ensure a holistic and
24 compassionate approach to recovery; and
25 (3) Provide patients with education and resources, including:
26 (a) Self-efficacy education on managing sickle cell disease as a chronic illness
27 and promoting independence rather than dependence on frequent hospital
