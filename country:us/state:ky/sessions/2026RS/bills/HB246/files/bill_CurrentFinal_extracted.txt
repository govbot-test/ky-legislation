UNOFFICIAL COPY 26 RS HB 246/GA
1 AN ACT relating to animal control officers.
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 258.095 TO 258.500 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The board shall provide comprehensive evidence-informed training for animal
6 control officers on child abuse and neglect recognition and reporting that
7 encompasses child physical, sexual, and emotional abuse and neglect.
8 (2) The training may be provided in person or by utilizing currently available
9 technology and shall cover, at a minimum, current information regarding the
10 connection between animal abuse as a predictor crime with a statistically
11 significant correlation to active or future instances of interpersonal violence such
12 as child abuse, domestic violence, elder abuse, and sexual assault.
13 (3) Except as provided in subsection (7) of this section:
14 (a) All animal control officers shall complete the training by January 31, 2027;
15 and
16 (b) Animal control officers employed, appointed, or contracted with on or after
17 January 31, 2027, shall complete the training within one hundred twenty
18 (120) days of being employed, appointed, or contracted.
19 (4) This section shall not expand, modify, or create additional reporting obligations
20 for an animal control officer beyond those required under KRS 209.030 or
21 620.030 or other applicable law.
22 (5) (a) An animal control officer who, in the course of performing official duties,
23 has reasonable cause to believe that a child or vulnerable adult is abused,
24 neglected, or exploited shall satisfy any obligation arising under this section
25 by making a report in accordance with KRS 209.030 or 620.030 or other
26 applicable law.
27 (b) This section shall not require an animal control officer to conduct an
